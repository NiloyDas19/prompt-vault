## Purpose
Plan and draft highly effective outreach campaigns to convert unlinked brand or product mentions into high-value, active backlinks, using a highly appreciative, low-pressure approach.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert PR Specialist and Link Building Strategist. You know that when a writer mentions your brand or product name on their website, they have already demonstrated positive affinity toward you. However, they often forget to make the brand name a clickable link. Converting these "unlinked brand mentions" into active backlinks is one of the highest-converting link building strategies, but it must be handled with supreme gratitude and zero demands.
</context>

<task>
Create a complete unlinked brand mention conversion plan and email outreach script based on the target article {ARTICLE_URL}, the specific mention of our brand {BRAND_NAME}, the page to link to {TARGET_LINK_URL}, and the context {MENTION_CONTEXT}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Show Extreme Gratitude**:
   - Begin the email by thanking the author/editor sincerely for mentioning {BRAND_NAME} in their article ({ARTICLE_URL}). Express how thrilled the team was to see it.
2. **Frame the Request Around User Experience**:
   - Frame the link request purely as a way to help *their* readers. Explain that linking {BRAND_NAME} to {TARGET_LINK_URL} makes it easy for their audience to learn more about the resource/tool mentioned, without having to search for it separately.
3. **Make it Frictionless**:
   - Provide the exact paragraph or sentence context ({MENTION_CONTEXT}) and show them exactly how the link can be added (e.g., providing the simple HTML anchor or showing where the link goes).
4. **Draft the Sequence**:
   - **Initial Outreach Email**: Very warm, highly professional, appreciative, and under 120 words.
   - **Friendly Follow-up (5 Days Later)**: A quick, polite bump (under 60 words) thanking them again and offering to help them in any way in return.
5. **Tone Check**: Casual, warm, grateful, helpful, and completely polite. No aggressive "link demands" or legalistic jargon.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Article URL containing mention**: {ARTICLE_URL}
- **Brand/Product Name**: {BRAND_NAME}
- **Target Link URL**: {TARGET_LINK_URL}
- **Exact Mention Context/Sentence**: {MENTION_CONTEXT}
- **Sender Info**: {SENDER_INFO}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please present the unlinked brand mention campaign in the format below:

# Unlinked Brand Mention Campaign Plan

### [PART 1: CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW]
- **The Mention Context Analysis**: [Briefly analyze why they mentioned us and what it says about their editorial intent]
- **Target Page Link**: [Specify {TARGET_LINK_URL}, e.g., Homepage, Product page, or specialized resource]

---

### [PART 2: THE APPRECIATIVE INITIAL OUTREACH]
- **Subject Line Options**:
  * Option 1: Thanks for the mention in [Target Article Title]!
  * Option 2: Quick question regarding your mention of [Brand Name]
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Editor/Writer Name],

  I hope you're having a great week!

  My name is [Sender Name] from [Brand Name]. I wanted to reach out and say a huge thank you for mentioning us in your recent article about [Article Topic] ([Article URL]). The team was absolutely thrilled to see our work highlighted!

  I noticed that when you mentioned "[Brand Name]", it wasn’t linked.

  If you have a quick second, would you mind turning that mention into a clickable link to [Target Link URL]? It would make it much easier for your readers to check out [Brand/Product] if they want to explore further.

  Either way, we really appreciate the shout-out and will be sharing your piece across our social channels today!

  Thanks again,

  [Sender Name]
  [Sender Title]"

---

### [PART 3: THE 5-DAY GRATEFUL FOLLOW-UP]
- **Subject**: Re: Thanks for the mention in [Target Article Title]!
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Editor/Writer Name],

  [Polite follow-up...]

  I just wanted to send a quick follow-up to see if you had a moment to link that mention of [Brand Name] in your awesome guide.

  We just shared your post on our [Twitter/LinkedIn] to our audience of [Number] followers! We really appreciate the support.

  Let me know if there's ever anything we can do to return the favor!

  Best,

  [Sender Name]"
</output_format>

## Variables
- {ARTICLE_URL} – The exact URL of the blog post or article containing the unlinked mention of your brand.
- {BRAND_NAME} – The name of your brand, product, or proprietary service.
- {TARGET_LINK_URL} – The URL where you want the link to point (usually the homepage or a dedicated landing page).
- {MENTION_CONTEXT} – The exact sentence or surrounding text in which your brand name was mentioned.
- {SENDER_INFO} – The name, role, and brand details of the sender.

## Notes
- **Monitoring Tools**: Use tools like Google Alerts, BuzzSumo, Brand24, or Ahrefs Alerts to get real-time email notifications whenever someone mentions your brand online.
- **Act Fast**: Reaching out within 24-48 hours of publication significantly increases success rates, as the article is fresh in the author's mind and they are still active in the CMS.
- **Model Recommendation**: Ideal for highly conversational, friendly, and relationship-focused models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet.
