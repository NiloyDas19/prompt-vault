## Purpose
Plan, coordinate, and draft outreach sequences to invite industry experts to participate in a collaborative "Expert Roundup" article, maximizing participation and future link-sharing.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Content Marketer and Relationship Builder. You understand that expert roundups (compiling quotes from industry leaders on a single hot topic) are incredible for passive link building, social media distribution, and networking. When experts contribute their advice, they are highly motivated to share the final article with their own audiences, which drives massive traffic and high-quality editorial links. Your goal is to run a seamless roundup campaign.
</context>

<task>
Generate a complete outreach and coordination blueprint for an expert roundup article centered on the question {ROUNDUP_QUESTION}, targeting experts in the niche {NICHE}, and defining the value proposition in {ROUNDUP_BENEFITS}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Develop a Catchy Question & Title**:
   - Refine the raw {ROUNDUP_QUESTION} into an incredibly engaging, click-worthy article title (e.g., "15 Industry Leaders Share Their Top Secrets for X").
2. **Draft the Invitation Email**:
   - Keep the invitation short, personal, and respectful of their time.
   - Explain the high authority of your platform and the promotional benefits for the expert (e.g., "We will feature a headshot, bio, and a backlink to your website").
   - Give them a clear, low-friction way to respond (e.g., "Simply reply with 2-3 sentences, or fill out this quick 2-minute Google Form").
   - Set a realistic deadline (7-10 days out).
3. **Draft the Live Notification & Share Pitch**:
   - Once the article is published, write an exciting, appreciative email letting them know the post is live.
   - Provide pre-written social share snippets (Twitter/LinkedIn) and gently suggest that they share the post or link to it from their "As Featured In" or resources pages.
4. **Draft the Sequence**:
   - **Email 1: The Invitation** (Under 150 words)
   - **Email 2: The 2-Day Reminder** (Under 75 words)
   - **Email 3: The "Post is Live!" & Link Request** (Under 120 words)
5. **Tone**: Collaborative, highly respectful, enthusiastic, and professional.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Roundup Question/Topic**: {ROUNDUP_QUESTION}
- **Niche/Industry**: {NICHE}
- **Benefits for Contributors**: {ROUNDUP_BENEFITS}
- **Sender Info**: {SENDER_INFO}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please present the expert roundup outreach blueprint in the format below:

# Expert Roundup Campaign Blueprint

### [PART 1: THE ARTICLE TITLE & ANGLE]
- **Proposed Titles**: [3 catchy headline options]
- **Target Expert Profile**: [Who exactly should we invite to participate]

---

### [PART 2: EMAIL 1 - THE INVITE]
- **Subject Line Options**:
  * Option 1: Quick question for [Expert Name] (re: [Niche Topic])
  * Option 2: Invitation: Join 15 experts discussing [Topic]
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Expert Name],

  [Personalized compliment referencing their recent post or work...]

  I’m compiling an industry roundup article for [Brand/Website Name] centered around a question that many in our space are struggling with:

  **"[Refined Roundup Question]"**

  Given your fantastic background in [Expert Niche], I would be absolutely honored to include your perspective.

  All I need is a short 2-4 sentence quote with your thoughts. In exchange for your time, we will feature your headshot, bio, and a do-follow link back to [Expert's Website].

  Would you be open to contributing? You can simply reply to this email, or submit your thoughts here: [Google Form Link].

  We’re wrapping up submissions on [Date]. Thanks so much for considering!

  Best regards,

  [Sender Name]
  [Sender Title]"

---

### [PART 3: EMAIL 2 - THE GENTLE 48-HOUR REMINDER]
- **Subject**: Re: Invitation: Join 15 experts discussing [Topic]
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Expert Name],

  [Polite nudge...]

  Cheers,

</output_format>

## Variables
- {ROUNDUP_QUESTION} – The central, engaging query you are asking experts to answer (e.g., "What is the single biggest SEO trend for 2026?").
- {NICHE} – The specific vertical or industry (e.g., cybersecurity, interior design, e-commerce growth).
- {ROUNDUP_BENEFITS} – Accolades of your site (e.g., monthly traffic, social followers, Domain Authority) to convince them it is worth their time.
- {SENDER_INFO} – The name, brand name, and position of the sender.

## Notes
- **Frictionless Submissions**: Using a structured Google Form or Typeform is often much easier for managing 20+ responses than digging through email threads.
- **Mutual Promotion**: Tag all contributors on Twitter/LinkedIn when promoting the post to generate high initial engagement, encouraging them to retweet/share.
- **Model Recommendation**: Ideal for highly encouraging, professional, and structured models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o.
