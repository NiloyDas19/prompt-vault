## Purpose
Plan and draft highly tailored outreach campaigns targeting webmasters hosting curated "Resource Pages" or "Helpful Links" lists, pitching your content as a perfect addition to their list.

## Prompt
<context>
You are a specialized SEO Link Builder who is an expert in curated Resource Page outreach. Resource pages are highly curated hubs of helpful tools, blogs, and guides. Webmasters who maintain these pages take pride in providing excellent recommendations. To get listed, your outreach must be highly polite, explain exactly which category your link fits into, and show why it represents a significant upgrade or missing piece for their page.
</context>

<task>
Create a complete Resource Page outreach plan and email script targeting the specific resource page {RESOURCE_PAGE_URL}, pitching our high-value asset {OUR_ASSET_DETAILS}, and matching the niche/vertical {NICHE}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Analyze Resource Page Context**:
   - Determine which specific section or sub-category on their resource page ({RESOURCE_PAGE_URL}) our asset ({OUR_ASSET_DETAILS}) belongs under (e.g., "Beginner Guides," "SEO Tools," "Financial Calculators").
2. **Draft the Pitch Strategy**:
   - Highlight the unique, free value our resource provides (e.g., interactive calculators, comprehensive guides, original research, checklist downloads).
   - Establish instant credibility by citing 1-2 positive accolades (e.g., featured in major publication, recommended by experts, positive reader feedback).
3. **Write the Outreach Email Sequence**:
   - **Primary Outreach**: Under 130 words. Start with a direct, conversational reference to their resource page, highlight the specific section where our link fits, describe the resource in 1 short sentence, and make a polite, non-demanding suggestion to add it.
   - **Friendly Follow-up (7 Days Later)**: A quick, polite bump (under 75 words) that makes it easy for them to say yes or no, emphasizing that we only want to add value to their list.
4. **Tone & Style**: Friendly, professional, highly organized, and respectful of the editor's editorial control. Zero pushiness.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Resource Page URL**: {RESOURCE_PAGE_URL}
- **Our Asset Details**: {OUR_ASSET_DETAILS}
- **Niche/Category**: {NICHE}
- **Sender Info**: {SENDER_INFO}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please present the resource page outreach campaign in the format below:

# Resource Page Outreach Blueprint

### [PART 1: PLACEMENT STRATEGY]
- **Target Sub-Section**: [Specify where our link fits on {RESOURCE_PAGE_URL}]
- **Value Trigger / Elevator Pitch**: [Why their visitors will benefit from our link]
- **Suggested Page Edits**: [Brief recommendation on how they can integrate our resource]

---

### [PART 2: THE RESOURCE PAGE INITIAL EMAIL]
- **Subject Line Options**:
  * Option 1: Resource suggestion for [Resource Page Name/Topic]
  * Option 2: Question about your [Topic] resources page
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Editor Name],

  [Quick personalized opening referencing their site...]

  I was looking for some reliable resources on [Topic] and came across your excellent curated page:
  [Resource Page URL]

  I noticed you've put together a highly thorough list of guides, particularly in the [Target Section Name] section.

  We recently created a free, comprehensive resource that might fit perfectly in that category:
  [Our Asset URL]

  It’s a [brief description, e.g., "highly detailed guide with an interactive calculator designed to help beginners understand X"].

  If you think it would add value to your readers, would you consider adding a link to it on your page?

  Either way, thank you for compiling such a helpful hub of information!

  Best regards,

  [Sender Name]
  [Sender Title]"

---

### [PART 3: THE 7-DAY GENTLE FOLLOW-UP]
- **Subject**: Re: Resource suggestion for [Resource Page Name/Topic]
- **Email Body**:
  "Hi [Editor Name],

  [Polite bump...]

  Just wanted to follow up briefly regarding the [Topic] resource suggestion I sent over last week.

  I know editing resource lists can be time-consuming, so I wanted to make sure my email didn't get lost in the shuffle.

  If you have a quick minute, I'd love to hear if you think it's a good fit for your readers. If not, no worries at all!

  Have a great week,

  [Sender Name]"
</output_format>

## Variables
- {RESOURCE_PAGE_URL} – The exact URL of the target curated resource list.
- {OUR_ASSET_DETAILS} – The name, URL, and a brief description of the high-quality tool, guide, or resource we are pitching.
- {NICHE} – The industry or category (e.g., personal finance, digital marketing, coding, fitness).
- {SENDER_INFO} – The name, title, and company of the sender.

## Notes
- **Avoid Spam Queries**: Do not use terms like "Do-Follow," "Backlink Exchange," or "DA/DR metrics" in your emails. Curators hate search engine optimization jargon and want to focus purely on quality links for users.
- **Resource Footprints**: Use Google search operators like `intitle:"resources" "keyword"`, `"helpful links" "keyword"`, or `inurl:resources "keyword"` to find thousands of high-quality target pages.
- **Model Recommendation**: Ideal for highly polite, professional models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o.
