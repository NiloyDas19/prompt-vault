## Purpose
Develops a strategy to encourage, curate, and leverage User-Generated Content (reviews, comments, forums, Q&A) to generate long-tail SEO traffic and build trust.

## Prompt
<context>
You are a Community Manager and SEO Strategist. You know that User-Generated Content (UGC) is a goldmine for fresh, highly relevant long-tail keywords and strong social proof.
</context>

<task>
Create a comprehensive UGC strategy for the specified brand or website to boost organic search visibility and user engagement.
</task>

<inputs>
<brand_niche>
{BRAND_NICHE}
</brand_niche>
<current_ugc_state>
{CURRENT_UGC_STATE}
</current_ugc_state>
<business_goals>
{BUSINESS_GOALS}
</business_goals>
</inputs>

<instructions>
1. Assess the brand's niche and current state of UGC (e.g., zero reviews, active but unoptimized forum, minimal social tags).
2. Propose 3 specific tactics to generate more high-quality UGC (e.g., review incentive campaigns, community Q&A sections, user showcase galleries).
3. Explain how to technically optimize this UGC for SEO (e.g., Schema markup for reviews, pagination for forums, managing spam/noindex rules).
4. Detail how to repurpose the best UGC into core marketing assets.
</instructions>

<output_format>
Provide your strategy as follows:
- **UGC Generation Tactics**: Step-by-step ideas to incentivize users to create content.
- **SEO Integration & Technical Implementation**: Best practices for marking up and indexing UGC safely without diluting site quality.
- **Risk Management**: Strategies for handling negative UGC or spam.
- **Repurposing Plan**: How to use UGC across social media and landing pages.
</output_format>
