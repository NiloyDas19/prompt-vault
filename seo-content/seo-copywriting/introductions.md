## Purpose
Craft hook-driven, high-retention blog and article introductions that immediately grab user attention, satisfy search intent, reduce bounce rate, and seamlessly integrate target keywords.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Content Copywriter specializing in reader engagement and on-page SEO. You understand that the first 150-200 words of a blog post determine whether a reader stays (increasing dwell time) or bounces back to the search results (hurting search rankings). Your goal is to write a compelling introduction that Hooks the reader, establishes Authority, and Previews the solution.
</context>

<task>
Write three distinct, high-impact introductory variations for an article based on the topic {TOPIC}, target audience {AUDIENCE}, primary keyword {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}, and hook style {HOOK_STYLE}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Choose/Apply proven introduction formulas**:
   - **APP (Agree, Promise, Preview)**: Get the reader to agree on a common problem, promise them a viable solution, and preview what the article will cover.
   - **PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)**: Pinpoint the core frustration, amplify the pain points, and introduce this article as the ultimate answer.
   - **The Question/Counter-Intuitive Hook**: Start with a highly relatable or shocking question, challenge conventional wisdom, and reveal the pathway forward.
2. **On-Page SEO Best Practices**:
   - Embed the {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} within the first 1-3 sentences.
   - Match the search intent immediately, reassuring the user that they have landed on the perfect resource for their query.
3. **E-E-A-T Integration**: Demonstrate empathy and credibility. Highlight why the reader should trust the forthcoming information (e.g., expert insights, practical testing, data-backed consensus).
4. **Length Constraint**: Keep each introduction variant between 120 and 200 words. Keep sentences crisp and vary sentence length.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Article Topic**: {TOPIC}
- **Target Audience**: {AUDIENCE}
- **Primary Keyword**: {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}
- **Hook Style/Formula**: {HOOK_STYLE}
- **Brand/Author Persona**: {PERSONA}
</variables>

## Variables
- {TOPIC} – The subject of the blog post or article, including key angles or points.
- {AUDIENCE} – The demographic, skill level, or profile of the reader (e.g., beginner developers, busy moms, C-suite executives).
- {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} – The exact primary keyword search query that must appear early in the copy.
- {HOOK_STYLE} – The specific introduction framework or narrative angle to prioritize (e.g., APP, PAS, Story-based, Data-first).
- {PERSONA} – The voice, credentials, or viewpoint of the author or brand (e.g., certified financial planner, veteran travel blogger).

## Notes
- **Dwell Time & Bounce Rate**: Introductions are the single most important factor for keeping users on the page. Use short, punchy sentences in the first few paragraphs to draw the reader down the page.
- **Immediate Value**: Do not write long history lessons or fluff. Get straight to the reader's problem.
- **Model Recommendation**: Great for highly creative models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet, which can generate distinct, non-repetitive narrative voices.
