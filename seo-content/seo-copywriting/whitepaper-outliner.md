## Purpose
Design a highly structured, authoritative outline for a B2B whitepaper or e-book that establishes industry credibility, serves as an optimized lead magnet, and aligns with high-value SEO topics.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Content Architect and Senior B2B Content Strategist. Your specialty is outlining high-authority, comprehensive whitepapers, e-books, and research reports. You know how to structure complex technical ideas, proprietary methodologies, and industry insights into a clean, compelling, and logically sound reading experience that drives lead generation and establishes absolute market authority.
</context>

<task>
Generate a comprehensive, chapter-by-chapter outline for an authoritative B2B whitepaper or E-book based on the core topic {CORE_TOPIC}, targeting {TARGET_AUDIENCE}, addressing {KEY_PAIN_POINTS}, and aligning with the overarching SEO theme {SEO_THEME}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Define the Big Idea**: Develop a compelling, industry-disrupting title and subtitle options that position this whitepaper as a "must-read" document.
2. **Establish Logical Flow**: Outline a classic, highly persuasive B2B thesis structure:
   - **Executive Summary / Abstract**: High-level key findings and immediate value.
   - **Chapter 1: The New Landscape / The Problem**: Establish the macroeconomic shifts or technical disruptions driving the need for change.
   - **Chapter 2: The Core Analysis**: Deep dive into the data, mechanics, or failures of existing standard solutions.
   - **Chapter 3: The Framework / The Methodology**: Introduce your company's proprietary framework, methodology, or system as the optimal solution.
   - **Chapter 4: Implementation & Action Plan**: Concrete, step-by-step instructions on how the reader can implement this framework.
   - **Chapter 5: Case Study / Proof Points**: Real-world evidence validating your framework.
   - **Conclusion & Next Steps**: Forward-looking summary and clear path to action.
3. **Chapter Breakdown Details**: For each chapter, specify:
   - **Chapter Goal**: The primary objective of this chapter.
   - **Core Thesis**: The main argument or point to prove.
   - **Key Subsections (H2 & H3)**: A nested structural layout.
   - **Data/Research Points**: Suggestions on what statistics, diagrams, or case studies to include.
   - **Callout Box Opportunities**: Text block ideas (e.g., industry warnings, expert quotes, definitions).
4. **Align with SEO Theme**: Ensure the subheadings and topics represent primary and secondary keyword groups related to {SEO_THEME} to facilitate eventual blog syndication or pillar page landing page optimization.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Core Topic**: {CORE_TOPIC}
- **Target Audience**: {TARGET_AUDIENCE}
- **Key Pain Points to Address**: {KEY_PAIN_POINTS}
- **SEO Theme / Pillar Topic**: {SEO_THEME}
- **Proprietary Methodology/Angle**: {PROPRIETARY_ANGLE}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please output the structured outline in the format below:

- **Recommended Theme**: [E.g., Dark modern corporate, data-centric minimal]
- **Target Page Count**: [E.g., 10-15 pages]

- **Objective**: [Goal for the reader]
- **Core Thesis**: [Main point to prove]
- **Heading Structure**:

  * **H2**: [Heading 2]
- **Data & Visual Ideas**: [E.g., "Insert a bar chart showing the rise in X over 5 years"]
- **Callout Box Idea**: [Details of the callout box]
- **SEO Keyword Alignment**: [Keywords from {SEO_THEME}]

[Repeat this detailed breakdown for all chapters...]

## Final CTA Page Design
- **Headline**: [Urgent, authoritative headline]
- **Body Copy**: [Pitch for a demo, consultation, or assessment]
- **Button CTA**: [Action phrase]
</output_format>

## Variables
- {CORE_TOPIC} – The main theme, research topic, or industry development your whitepaper covers.
- {TARGET_AUDIENCE} – The decision-makers you want to read this (e.g., VP of Security, Chief Financial Officer, HR Directors).
- {KEY_PAIN_POINTS} – The major frustrations, risks, or financial threats your target audience is currently facing.
- {SEO_THEME} – The parent SEO category or pillar topic to ensure your whitepaper chapters can be repurposed into search-ranking blog articles.
- {PROPRIETARY_ANGLE} – Your brand's unique method, software, data set, or approach that differentiates you from standard market competitors.

## Notes
- **Content Syndication**: A great whitepaper outline should be modular. Each chapter should be able to stand alone as an SEO-optimized blog post or guest article.
- **Top-of-Funnel vs. Bottom-of-Funnel**: Keep the tone highly educational and objective until the CTA page. B2B decision-makers will immediately bounce if they detect a sales pitch too early.
- **Model Recommendation**: Ideal for high-level reasoning models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o which excel at structural logic and thematic coherence.
