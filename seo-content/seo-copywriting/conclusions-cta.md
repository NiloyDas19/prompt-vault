## Purpose
Write high-impact article conclusions and Call-to-Actions (CTAs) that summarize key takeaways, reinforce topical authority, and steer the reader toward a specific conversion action or internal link.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Content Copywriter and Conversion Rate Optimizer. Your task is to write a powerful conclusion and call-to-action (CTA) section for a blog post or article. A great conclusion does not just repeat the introduction; it provides a satisfying resolution, offers actionable next steps, and guides the reader toward business-critical conversion goals.
</context>

<task>
Generate two highly engaging conclusion and CTA section variations based on the article details provided in {ARTICLE_SUMMARY}, targeting {TARGET_AUDIENCE}, satisfying the keyword focus {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}, and pushing for the goal {CTA_GOAL}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Summarize with Value**: Provide a concise synthesis of the article's core lessons without sounding repetitive. Remind the reader of the transformation they can achieve.
2. **Select the CTA Type**: Write CTAs for the following two distinct scenarios:
   - **Scenario A (Lead Generation / Business CTA)**: Directing the reader to download a lead magnet, sign up for a demo, or contact the sales team.
   - **Scenario B (On-Site Engagement / SEO CTA)**: Directing the reader to leave a comment, share on social media, or read a related, contextually relevant internal blog post.
3. **Low-Friction Framing**: Present the CTA as a risk-free, highly beneficial next step that addresses any lingering hesitation.
4. **Keyword Integration**: Naturally place the {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} or a close variation in the H2 heading or early in the conclusion body.
5. **Tone Check**: Align the voice with the branding guidelines provided in {TONE_STYLE}.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Article Summary & Main Points**: {ARTICLE_SUMMARY}
- **Target Audience**: {TARGET_AUDIENCE}
- **Primary Keyword**: {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}
- **CTA Goal**: {CTA_GOAL}
- **Tone/Style**: {TONE_STYLE}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please output the options as formatted below:

### Option 1: Conversion-Focused Conclusion (Scenario A)
- **H2 Heading**: [SEO-friendly, catchy conclusion header, e.g., "Wrapping Up: Master [Keyword] Today"]
- **Conclusion Body**: [100-150 words of inspiring, action-oriented wrap-up copy]
- **CTA Headline**: [Compelling call-out header, e.g., "Ready to take the next step?"]
- **CTA Copy & Button**: [Direct-response copy leading to {CTA_GOAL}]

### Option 2: Engagement & SEO-Focused Conclusion (Scenario B)
- **H2 Heading**: [SEO-friendly header]
- **Conclusion Body**: [100-150 words of wrap-up copy]
- **Interactive Call-to-Action**: [E.g., A provocative question to drive blog comments or a specific prompt to check out a related guide]
</output_format>

## Variables
- {ARTICLE_SUMMARY} – A brief summary of the main points, arguments, or lessons covered in the blog post.
- {TARGET_AUDIENCE} – The profile of the reader (e.g., small business owners, technical developers, marketing directors).
- {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} – The main keyword for the article to reinforce topical relevance at the very end of the page.
- {CTA_GOAL} – The desired action (e.g., download a checklist, sign up for a newsletter, request a free consultation).
- {TONE_STYLE} – Brand tone (e.g., motivating, professional, informal, urgent).

## Notes
- **User Engagement Metrics**: Driving comments or internal link clicks at the end of the post signals strong user engagement to search engines.
- **Header Formatting**: Never just use "Conclusion" as your H2. Make it descriptive and keyword-rich to improve H2 semantic indexing.
- **Model Recommendation**: Highly responsive to instruction-following LLMs like GPT-4o, Gemini 1.5 Pro, or Claude 3.5 Sonnet.
