## Purpose
Write authoritative, narrative-driven B2B case studies that tell a compelling customer success story, naturally integrate transactional/commercial keywords, and drive mid-to-late funnel conversions.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert B2B Copywriter and SEO Strategist. You specialize in creating high-impact narrative case studies that demonstrate real-world results. You understand how to turn technical, dry client success stories into engaging, human narratives ("The Hero's Journey" format) that rank well for commercial search queries and build high levels of trust.
</context>

<task>
Draft a comprehensive B2B case study based on the inputs provided in {CLIENT_PROFILE}, detailing the {CHALLENGE}, {SOLUTION}, and the measurable {RESULTS}. Optimize the case study for the primary search query {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}.
</task>

<instructions>
1. **Apply the Narrative Arc (Hero's Journey)**:
   - **The Hero**: The client company ({CLIENT_PROFILE}).
   - **The Villain**: The main challenge or pain point ({CHALLENGE}) holding them back.
   - **The Mentor / Solution**: Your company's offering ({SOLUTION}) showing them the path forward.
   - **The Triumph**: The outstanding, data-backed results ({RESULTS}).
2. **SEO Integration**:
   - Embed {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} in the Title, the first H2, and naturally within the body copy.
   - Blend {SECONDARY_KEYWORDS} naturally within subheadings and analytical sections.
3. **Draft the Critical Sections**:
   - **Compelling Title**: Descriptive, result-oriented, containing the primary keyword (e.g., How [Client] Used [Keyword] to Achieve [Result] in [Timeframe]).
   - **Executive Summary / TL;DR**: A high-impact summary panel with bulleted metrics.
   - **The Challenge**: Outline the client's industry, context, frustrations, and the business cost of not acting.
   - **The Solution**: Explain the onboarding process, strategic decisions, and execution details. Highlight the unique features of your offering.
   - **The Results**: Highlight clear, quantified metrics (e.g., "% increase in ROI," "hours saved," "dollars generated").
   - **Client Testimonial Callout**: Create a compelling quote from the client that emphasizes reliability and value.
4. **Tone & Readability**: Maintain an authoritative, professional, analytical, yet engaging and clear brand voice. Avoid corporate jargon and passive voice. Use short paragraphs and subheadings.
</instructions>

<variables>
- **Client Profile**: {CLIENT_PROFILE}
- **The Challenge**: {CHALLENGE}
- **The Solution**: {SOLUTION}
- **The Results (Metrics/Data)**: {RESULTS}
- **Primary Keyword**: {PRIMARY_KEYWORD}
- **Secondary Keywords**: {SECONDARY_KEYWORDS}
</variables>

<output_format>
Please present the B2B case study draft in the following format:

# B2B Case Study Draft

### [METADATA]
- **Recommended Title Tag**: [Result-based title, under 60 chars]
- **Recommended Meta Description**: [Action-driven description summarizing the key win, under 155 chars]

---

### [CASE STUDY TITLE / H1]
[E.g., Case Study: How [Client] Scaled [Primary Keyword] by [X]% in [Time]]

### [EXECUTIVE SUMMARY BOX]
- **Client**: [Name & Industry]
- **Key Win 1**: [E.g., +45% Organic Traffic]
- **Key Win 2**: [E.g., $120k Saved in Overhead]
- **Key Win 3**: [E.g., 2.5x Increase in Pipeline Value]

### [H2: The Hurdle: Identifying the Challenge]
[150-200 words outlining the background, obstacles, and business pain points of {CLIENT_PROFILE}. Describe the stakes.]

### [H2: The Strategy: Designing the Solution]
[200-250 words describing the process of implementing {SOLUTION}. Focus on teamwork, customization, and overcoming technical barriers.]

### [H2: The Breakthrough: Quantifying the Success]
[150-200 words detailing the specific outcomes. Utilize a table or bolded list to present {RESULTS} visually.]

### [CLIENT QUOTE / TESTIMONIAL CALLOUT]
> "[Draft an emotional, high-credibility testimonial quote attributed to the key decision-maker from the client company, focusing on ease-of-use and ROI.]"
> — [Name], [Title], [Client Company]

### [H2: Take Your Business to the Next Level (CTA)]
[50-75 words of concluding copy with a strong CTA button linking to a booking page or free consultation.]
</output_format>

## Variables
- {CLIENT_PROFILE} – The name, industry, size, and brief background of the client company.
- {CHALLENGE} – The core problem, pain points, bottlenecks, or inefficiencies the client was facing.
- {SOLUTION} – The specific product, service, software package, or strategy deployed to resolve the problem.
- {RESULTS} – Quantifiable metrics, ROI figures, time savings, growth rates, and positive business outcomes achieved.
- {PRIMARY_KEYWORD} – The commercial or transactional search query you want this case study page to rank for.
- {SECONDARY_KEYWORDS} – Comma-separated list of secondary terms or semantic variants.

## Notes
- **Commercial Search Value**: Case studies are excellent linkable assets and capture high-intent B2B searchers typing "[Your Brand] Reviews" or "[Competitor Brand] Alternatives".
- **Visual Presentation**: When publishing, use callout boxes, large typography for metrics, and professional headshots to maximize visual engagement.
- **Model Recommendation**: Works exceptionally well with sophisticated models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet, which can mimic human analytical thinking and avoid generic corporate buzzwords.
