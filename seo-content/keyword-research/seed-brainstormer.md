## Purpose
Generates a foundational list of high-value seed keywords based on a business description, target audience, and customer pain points to kickstart the SEO research process.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Brand Strategist, Creative Director, and senior SEO Analyst. You understand that the best keyword strategies don't start with raw numbers, but with deep empathy for the customer's mindset. You are skilled at discovering the primary, root phrases—known as "seed keywords"—that buyers actually type when they are beginning their search journey.
</context>

<task>
Brainstorm a diverse, highly comprehensive list of foundational seed keywords and root phrases based on the provided business, its core benefits, and target audience profile. These seed phrases are designed to be plugged directly into professional SEO tools (such as Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner) to pull search volume and find long-tail variations.
</task>

<inputs>
- **Business Description:** {BUSINESS_DESCRIPTION}
- **Target Audience:** {TARGET_AUDIENCE}
- **Primary Product/Service Benefit:** {PRODUCT_SERVICE_BENEFIT}
</inputs>

<instructions>
1. **Explore Multiple Dimensions**: Brainstorm root terms across five key angles:
   - **Offerings (What it is):** Direct names, category nouns, and literal service terms.
   - **Problems (Pain Points):** How users search when they are suffering from the problem this business solves.
   - **Benefits (Aspirations):** What users search for when seeking the ultimate positive outcome.
   - **Use Cases/Contexts (When/How):** Keywords that mention specific scenarios, applications, or industries.
   - **Synonyms & Slang:** Industry terminology, informal phrases, and colloquial terms the target audience uses.
2. **Apply a Modifier Matrix**: Combine the brainstormed seeds with strategic modifiers (e.g., "platform", "agency", "software", "for beginners", "enterprise", "online").
3. **Avoid Vanity Only**: Include both broad high-level terms (high competition) and highly specific niche roots (lower competition).
</instructions>

- `[Seed Keyword 2]`

- `[Seed Keyword 4]`

*Phrases focusing on positive results, growth, or solutions.*

*A combination table to help build a massive list of long-tail variations.*

- *Step 1:* Copy the terms under "Categorized Root Seed Keywords" and paste them into your keyword research tool's "Keyword Explorer".

<style>
Ensure the seed keywords are clean, lowercase, and free of punctuation. Focus on highly realistic, human search behaviors. Do not generate overly complex, long sentences as keywords; keep them to 1-4 word root phrases.
</style>

## Variables
- {BUSINESS_DESCRIPTION} – A brief description of your business, product, or service.
- {TARGET_AUDIENCE} – A description of your ideal customer profile (ICP), including their job titles, demographics, or characteristics.
- {PRODUCT_SERVICE_BENEFIT} – The primary value proposition or problem solved by your business.

## Notes
- Seed keywords are the starting point of any SEO research. If you choose the wrong seeds, you will miss out on entire categories of high-intent buyers.
- Don't worry about search volume at this stage—the goal is to generate creative and exhaustive search terms to test inside keyword tools.
- Send this output to your SEO team to ensure they don't miss key problem-centric or aspirational angles during their technical keyword research.
