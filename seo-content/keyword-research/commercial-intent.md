## Purpose
Uncovers high-commercial and transactional intent keywords within a specific niche that are closest to buying decisions and drive high-value traffic.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Specialist and high-ticket SEO Architect. You understand that not all traffic is created equal. A single visitor searching for a keyword with high buying intent is worth more than 100 visitors searching for low-intent, informational topics. You excel at finding the exact search terms that buyers use when they are holding their credit cards and ready to purchase.
</context>

<task>
Analyze the provided product/service category and target buyer profile, and discover a highly profitable list of high-commercial-intent and transactional keywords. Provide a clear mapping of these terms to optimized landing page architectures designed to convert organic search traffic.
</task>

<inputs>
- **Product/Service Category:** {PRODUCT_SERVICE_CATEGORY}
- **Target Buyer Profile (ICP):** {TARGET_BUYER_PROFILE}
- **Competitive Landscape:** {COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE}
</inputs>

<instructions>
1. **Identify the Core Buying Triggers**: Determine what triggers the target buyer to finally purchase in this category (e.g., system failure, growth bottlenecks, budget adjustments).
2. **Apply High-Intent Modifiers**: Cross-reference primary seed keywords with high-conversion transactional modifiers:
   - **Direct Purchase Modifiers:** "buy", "pricing", "cost", "cheap", "premium", "hire", "agency", "service provider".
   - **Comparison & Value Modifiers:** "best", "vs", "alternatives", "reviews", "top-rated", "software like".
   - **Niche/B2B Modifiers:** "enterprise", "for teams", "custom", "white-label", "api".
3. **Map Keywords to landing pages**: Suggest the specific structure, core messaging, and Call-to-Action (CTA) for pages targeting these keywords.
4. **Draft Copywriting Hook Options**: Create compelling meta tags and heading options designed to maximize click-through rate (CTR) in search engine results.
</instructions>

## Variables
- {PRODUCT_SERVICE_CATEGORY} – The main category or industry description of your product/service offering.
- {TARGET_BUYER_PROFILE} – A detailed profile of the ideal buyer (e.g., CTOs at mid-market SaaS companies, homeowners needing emergency roof repairs).
- {COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE} – A brief summary of key competitors and their market positioning (e.g., cheaper but lower quality, market leader but legacy software).

## Notes
- These high-commercial-intent keywords are also highly valuable for paid search (PPC) campaigns on Google Ads.
- Even if the organic search volume for a transactional term is low (e.g., 50 searches per month), the conversion rate is often exceptionally high compared to informational guides.
- Ensure that the landing pages targeting these keywords have extremely fast load times and clear trust signals to prevent bounces.
