## Variables
- {WEEKLY_ROUTINE} – A summary of how you spend your typical week, including work hours, commute, chores, family obligations, and free time.
- {CURRENT_STRESSORS} – The biggest factors currently causing you stress, anxiety, or fatigue (e.g., tight project deadlines, understaffed team, micro-managing boss).

- {BOUNDARIES_NEEDED} – The specific areas where you struggle to say no or maintain separation (e.g., checking emails at 9 PM, working weekends, taking on extra work without compensation).

## Notes
- **Time vs. Energy**: Time is a finite resource, but energy can be managed and expanded. Focus on restoring your energy reservoirs rather than just calendar scheduling.
- **The "Yes" Trade-off**: Every time you say "yes" to an unplanned professional request, remember that you are automatically saying "no" to a personal boundary, family time, or self-care.
- **Micro-Recoveries**: Incorporate short, 5-10 minute disconnects throughout the workday (e.g., box breathing, stretching, walking away from screens) to mitigate cumulative cognitive fatigue.
