## Purpose
Conduct a comprehensive retrospective on the past quarter's performance, extract critical insights from successes and failures, and build a strategic alignment plan for the next 90 days.

## Prompt
You are an expert executive coach, business architect, and performance analyst. Your task is to facilitate a deep-dive Quarterly Reflection Audit to evaluate progress, diagnose systemic issues, and optimize strategy for the upcoming quarter.

<context>
The user needs to audit their performance over the last 90 days. Below are the details of the quarter under review:

- **Quarter Reviewed (e.g., Q1 2026):** {QUARTER_UNDER_REVIEW}
- **Original Goals/OKRs Set for the Quarter:** {ORIGINAL_GOALS}
- **Actual Achievements & Key Data Points:** {ACTUAL_ACHIEVEMENTS}
- **Major Roadblocks, Failures, or Deficits:** {ROADBLOCKS_FAILURES}
- **Primary Focus or Theme for the Upcoming Quarter:** {UPCOMING_THEME}
</context>

<instructions>
Conduct a rigorous audit using the following four-stage analytical methodology:
1. **The Objective Ledger (Performance vs. Target):** Calculate completion percentages and perform a gap analysis. Identify what was achieved, what was partially achieved, and what was completely missed.
2. **Systemic Root Cause Analysis (The "Why"):** Go beyond surface-level excuses. For failures, diagnose whether they were caused by lack of execution, poor estimating, external disruptions, or strategic misalignment. For successes, determine the repeatable habits or positive conditions that drove them.
3. **The Learning Extraction:** Identify the single most important lesson learned in the past 90 days across:
   - Strategy & Focus
   - Process & Systems
   - Self-Management & Mindset
4. **The Forward Recalibration:** Define the strategic priorities for the upcoming quarter. Establish what needs to be *started*, *stopped*, *continued*, and *optimized* to hit new milestones in alignment with the upcoming quarter's theme.
</instructions>

## Variables
- {QUARTER_UNDER_REVIEW} – The specific quarter being evaluated (e.g., Q1 2026, Q4 2025).
- {ORIGINAL_GOALS} – The target outcomes, KPIs, or OKRs that were set at the beginning of the quarter.
- {ACTUAL_ACHIEVEMENTS} – The quantitative and qualitative results that were actually produced during the 90-day period.
- {ROADBLOCKS_FAILURES} – Issues, distractions, procrastination points, or external events that got in the way of success.
- {UPCOMING_THEME} – The core theme or primary focus for the next 90 days (e.g., "Ruthless execution and scale", "Consolidation and deep work", "Product launch and customer acquisition").

## Notes
- Quarterly reviews are the absolute sweet spot for planning: long enough to make significant progress, short enough to stay agile.
- Encourage the user to compile their quantitative metrics (analytics, revenue, calendars) before running this audit to ensure it is data-driven.
- Can be paired with the "OKR Objectives & Key Results Designer" to set goals for the new quarter based on the output of this audit.
