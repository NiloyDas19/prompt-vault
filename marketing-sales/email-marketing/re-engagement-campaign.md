## Purpose
Write a re-engagement email campaign targeting inactive subscribers or churned customers, designed to bring them back or clean your list.

## Prompt
Write a 3-email re-engagement campaign for subscribers/customers who haven't interacted in {INACTIVE_PERIOD}. The goal is to either re-activate them or gracefully remove them from the list.

**Brand:** {BRAND_NAME}
**What they originally signed up for:** {ORIGINAL_VALUE}
**How long they've been inactive:** {INACTIVE_PERIOD}
**What's changed since they left:** {WHATS_NEW}
**Win-back incentive:** {INCENTIVE}
**Brand Voice:** {BRAND_VOICE}

### Email 1: "We Miss You" (Day 1)
- Acknowledge the silence without guilt-tripping
- Remind them why they signed up in the first place
- Highlight the single best thing they've been missing
- Soft CTA: "Come take a look at what's new"

### Email 2: "Here's What You're Missing" (Day 4)
- Showcase 2–3 new features, products, or pieces of content since they disengaged
- Include a win-back incentive: {INCENTIVE}
- Social proof: what other returning customers are saying
- Stronger CTA with the incentive attached

### Email 3: "Last Chance Before We Say Goodbye" (Day 8)
- Be direct: "We'll remove you from our list if we don't hear back"
- Frame it as respect for their inbox, not a threat
- One-click "Keep me subscribed" button
- If they don't click, they'll be unsubscribed automatically
- Thank them regardless of their choice

For each email:
- **Subject line** (emotional, personal)
- **Preview text**
- **Full body copy** (short — max 100 words)
- **CTA button text**
- **Segment action** — what to do with subscribers who open vs. don't open

## Variables
- {BRAND_NAME} – Your brand
- {ORIGINAL_VALUE} – Why they signed up (e.g., "weekly marketing tips", "exclusive deals on electronics")
- {INACTIVE_PERIOD} – How long since their last engagement (e.g., "90 days", "6 months")
- {WHATS_NEW} – Recent improvements or content they've missed
- {INCENTIVE} – Win-back offer (e.g., "20% off your next order", "free month of premium", "exclusive content")
- {BRAND_VOICE} – Tone

## Notes
- Cleaning inactive subscribers IMPROVES your email deliverability. A smaller, engaged list outperforms a large, dead one.
- The "goodbye" email (Email 3) often has the highest open rate — people respond strongly to loss aversion.
- After this sequence, unsubscribe anyone who didn't open any of the 3 emails. It's healthy list hygiene.
