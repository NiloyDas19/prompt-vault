## Purpose
Plan 3 months of engaging newsletter content with content pillars, themes, and complete edition outlines that keep subscribers opening every week.

## Prompt
Create a 12-week newsletter content plan that keeps subscribers engaged and eagerly anticipating every edition.

**Newsletter Name:** {NEWSLETTER_NAME}
**Topic/Niche:** {NICHE}
**Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Frequency:** {FREQUENCY}
**Primary Goal:** {GOAL}
**Content Pillars:** {CONTENT_PILLARS}
**Monetization Model:** {MONETIZATION}

For the 12-week plan, provide:

### Content Calendar
A table with:
| Week | Theme | Subject Line | Lead Story | Secondary Content | CTA |
|------|-------|-------------|------------|-------------------|-----|

### Content Mix Formula
Recommend a repeating content ratio, e.g.:
- 40% Educational (how-tos, frameworks, tutorials)
- 25% Curated (best links, tools, resources)
- 20% Opinion/Analysis (hot takes, trends, predictions)
- 10% Personal/Behind-the-scenes (humanize the brand)
- 5% Promotional (your products, affiliate offers)

### For Each Week, Include:
1. **Theme** — The overarching topic
2. **Subject line** — Optimized for opens
3. **Hook** — The compelling first 2 sentences
4. **Main content** — 3–4 bullet point outline of the lead story
5. **Quick wins** — A tip the reader can implement in under 5 minutes
6. **Curated link** — One external resource with a one-line summary
7. **CTA** — What action you want the reader to take

### Engagement Boosters
- 3 ideas for interactive elements (polls, quizzes, reader spotlights)
- A "referral program" email concept to grow the subscriber base
- A re-engagement email for subscribers who haven't opened in 30 days

## Variables
- {NEWSLETTER_NAME} – Name of the newsletter
- {NICHE} – Topic area (e.g., "AI and productivity", "indie hacking", "personal finance")
- {AUDIENCE} – Who reads it (e.g., "startup founders", "marketing managers", "developers")
- {FREQUENCY} – How often (e.g., "weekly", "biweekly")
- {GOAL} – Primary purpose (e.g., "build authority", "drive product sales", "grow a community")
- {CONTENT_PILLARS} – 3–5 core topics you cover consistently
- {MONETIZATION} – Revenue model (e.g., "sponsored sections", "premium tier", "affiliate links", "not monetized yet")

## Notes
- The best newsletters have a consistent format readers can rely on. Create a "template" structure for every issue.
- Newsletters with a personality outperform corporate-sounding ones by 3–5x in engagement.
- Always include one "quick win" — something actionable the reader can do immediately. This is what makes them open next week.
