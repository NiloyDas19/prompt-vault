## Purpose
Build a complete product launch email sequence that creates anticipation, drives excitement, and maximizes day-one sales.

## Prompt
Write a 7-email product launch sequence that builds hype before launch day and converts on the day of release.

**Product Being Launched:** {PRODUCT_NAME}
**What It Does:** {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION}
**Launch Date:** {LAUNCH_DATE}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Pricing:** {PRICING}
**Early Bird / Launch Offer:** {LAUNCH_OFFER}
**Brand Voice:** {BRAND_VOICE}

### Pre-Launch Phase
**Email 1 (14 days before): The Teaser**
- Hint at what's coming without revealing specifics
- Create curiosity and anticipation
- Ask: "Want early access? Reply YES"

**Email 2 (10 days before): The Problem**
- Deep dive into the problem your product solves
- Make the pain vivid and relatable
- "We've been working on something to fix this..."

**Email 3 (7 days before): The Sneak Peek**
- Reveal the product name and core features
- Share a behind-the-scenes story of building it
- Open a waitlist or early access signup

### Launch Phase
**Email 4 (Launch Day, morning): The Reveal**
- Full product announcement with key benefits
- Early bird pricing or launch bonus
- Countdown timer urgency
- Primary CTA: "Get it now"

**Email 5 (Launch Day, evening): Social Proof**
- Share early customer reactions, first reviews, or purchase numbers
- "100 people already grabbed this in the first 4 hours"
- Handle the #1 objection in a P.S.

### Post-Launch Phase
**Email 6 (Day 3): The Deep Dive**
- Detailed feature breakdown or demo walkthrough
- Customer use case: "Here's how {persona} is using it"
- FAQ addressing common questions

**Email 7 (Day 7): Last Call**
- Launch pricing ends tonight
- Final testimonials and results
- "After midnight, the price goes to {REGULAR_PRICE}"
- Strong urgency CTA

For each email: subject line, preview text, full copy, and CTA.

## Variables
- {PRODUCT_NAME} – Product name
- {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION} – What it does
- {LAUNCH_DATE} – When it launches
- {AUDIENCE} – Who it's for
- {PRICING} – Launch price vs. regular price
- {LAUNCH_OFFER} – Special launch deal (e.g., "40% off for the first 48 hours", "free bonus module")
- {BRAND_VOICE} – Tone
- {REGULAR_PRICE} – Price after launch period ends

## Notes
- The pre-launch phase is as important as launch day. An audience that's been warmed up converts at 3–5x higher rates.
- "Reply YES for early access" is a powerful engagement trick — it trains email providers to prioritize your messages.
- Real urgency (deadline-based) outperforms fake urgency. Don't say "limited time" if it's not actually limited.
