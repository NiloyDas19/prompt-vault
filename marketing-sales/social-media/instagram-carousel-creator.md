## Purpose
Design complete Instagram carousel posts with slide-by-slide content, captions, and strategic calls-to-action that maximize saves and shares.

## Prompt
Create a 10-slide Instagram carousel post on the following topic. Design each slide for maximum visual impact and educational value.

**Topic:** {TOPIC}
**Account Niche:** {NICHE}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Goal:** {GOAL}
**Visual Style Preference:** {VISUAL_STYLE}
**Brand Colors:** {BRAND_COLORS}

### Slide-by-Slide Content:

**Slide 1 (Cover/Hook):**
- Bold headline (max 6 words) that makes people swipe
- Design note: large text, eye-catching contrast

**Slides 2–8 (Content Slides):**
- One key point per slide
- Headline (3–5 words) + supporting text (1–2 short sentences max)
- Each slide should make sense on its own AND flow as a sequence
- Use a mix of: tips, stats, examples, quotes, mini-stories

**Slide 9 (Summary/Recap):**
- Quick recap of all key points as a bullet list
- "Save this for later 🔖" nudge

**Slide 10 (CTA Slide):**
- Clear call-to-action: follow, save, share, visit link in bio
- Tag your handle
- Brief value prop: "Follow @{handle} for daily {topic} tips"

### Caption:
- Strong opening hook (first line visible before "...more")
- 3–4 paragraph summary of the carousel's value
- Engagement question to drive comments
- 20–30 relevant hashtags (mix of reach and niche)
- Alt text suggestions for accessibility

### Design Direction:
- Font pairing recommendation
- Color palette for the slides
- Layout style (left-aligned text, centered, mixed)
- Icon or illustration suggestions per slide

## Variables
- {TOPIC} – Carousel topic (e.g., "5 SEO mistakes killing your traffic", "Morning routine of successful founders")
- {NICHE} – Your content niche
- {AUDIENCE} – Target audience
- {GOAL} – Goal (e.g., "maximize saves", "drive profile visits", "grow followers")
- {VISUAL_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "clean minimalist with pastels", "bold dark mode with neon text", "muted earth tones")
- {BRAND_COLORS} – Hex codes or color names

## Notes
- Carousels get 1.4x more reach and 3.1x more engagement than single images on Instagram.
- "Save" is the most valuable engagement signal for the Instagram algorithm. Design for saves.
- The first and last slides are the most important: the first stops the scroll, the last drives the action.
