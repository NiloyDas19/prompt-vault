## Purpose
Generate a complete 30-day social media content calendar with post ideas, captions, hashtags, and optimal posting times across platforms.

## Prompt
Create a detailed 30-day social media content calendar for my brand. Every post should serve a strategic purpose — no filler content.

**Brand:** {BRAND_NAME}
**Industry:** {INDUSTRY}
**Platforms:** {PLATFORMS}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Content Pillars:** {CONTENT_PILLARS}
**Primary Goal:** {GOAL}
**Posting Frequency:** {FREQUENCY}
**Brand Voice:** {BRAND_VOICE}

For each day, provide:

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic/Hook | Caption (first 2 lines) | Hashtags | Best Time |
|-----|----------|-------------|------------|------------------------|----------|-----------|

### Content Type Mix (across 30 days):
- **Educational** (8 posts): Teach something valuable
- **Social Proof** (4 posts): Testimonials, reviews, case studies
- **Behind-the-Scenes** (4 posts): Humanize the brand
- **Engagement Bait** (4 posts): Polls, questions, "this or that"
- **Promotional** (3 posts): Direct product/service promotion
- **Trending/Timely** (3 posts): React to trends, news, or memes in your niche
- **User-Generated Content** (2 posts): Reshare customer content
- **Storytelling** (2 posts): Brand story, founder journey, customer stories

### Also Include:
1. **3 Scroll-stopping hooks** — Opening lines designed to stop the scroll
2. **Platform-specific adaptations** — How to adjust one piece of content for Instagram vs. LinkedIn vs. Twitter/X
3. **Engagement strategy** — What to comment, how to respond, when to engage with others' content
4. **Hashtag strategy** — A mix of reach hashtags (large), niche hashtags (medium), and branded hashtags (small)
5. **Key metrics to track** — Which KPIs matter for each content type

## Variables
- {BRAND_NAME} – Your brand name
- {INDUSTRY} – Your industry
- {PLATFORMS} – Which platforms (e.g., "Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X")
- {AUDIENCE} – Who you're reaching
- {CONTENT_PILLARS} – 3–5 core themes (e.g., "product tips, industry news, team culture, customer stories")
- {GOAL} – Primary objective (e.g., "grow followers", "drive website traffic", "generate leads")
- {FREQUENCY} – Posts per day/week per platform
- {BRAND_VOICE} – Tone

## Notes
- The 80/20 rule: 80% value-driven content, 20% promotional. Break this rule and watch engagement plummet.
- Batch content creation: write all 30 days in one session, then schedule. It's 10x more efficient than daily posting.
- Always have 2–3 "evergreen" posts ready to fill gaps when you miss a day.
