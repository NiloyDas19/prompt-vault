## Purpose
Transform one piece of pillar content (blog post, podcast, video) into 10+ pieces of social media content across multiple platforms.

## Prompt
Take the following pillar content and repurpose it into at least 15 pieces of social media content across multiple platforms.

**Original Content:**
{PASTE_ORIGINAL_CONTENT}

**Content Type:** {CONTENT_TYPE}
**Target Platforms:** {PLATFORMS}
**Brand Voice:** {BRAND_VOICE}
**Goal:** {GOAL}

Transform this into:

### Twitter/X Thread (1)
- 8–12 tweet thread breaking down the key insights
- Strong hook tweet, numbered points, CTA tweet at the end

### LinkedIn Posts (3)
- **Story-style post**: Turn a key insight into a personal narrative
- **Listicle post**: "7 things I learned about {topic}" format
- **Hot take post**: Controversial angle from the content

### Instagram Content (3)
- **Carousel** (10 slides): Key takeaways as a visual slide deck with captions
- **Reel script** (30 seconds): Hook → key insight → CTA
- **Story series** (5 slides): Interactive polls and questions based on the content

### Short-Form Video Scripts (2)
- **TikTok/Reel #1**: The "myth vs. reality" angle
- **TikTok/Reel #2**: The "one thing nobody tells you" angle

### Other Formats (3)
- **Newsletter snippet**: 3-paragraph email teasing the full content
- **Quote graphics**: 3 quotable one-liners from the content, formatted for sharing
- **Poll/Question**: An engagement post derived from the content's theme

### Bonus:
- **SEO-optimized summary**: 150-word version for meta descriptions or YouTube descriptions
- **Podcast talking points**: 5 bullet points if you wanted to discuss this content on a podcast

For each piece:
- Full ready-to-post copy
- Platform-specific formatting notes
- Suggested posting order (which to post first, second, etc.)

## Variables
- {PASTE_ORIGINAL_CONTENT} – Paste the full blog post, transcript, or content
- {CONTENT_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "3,000-word blog post", "45-min podcast transcript", "20-min YouTube video")
- {PLATFORMS} – Target platforms
- {BRAND_VOICE} – Tone
- {GOAL} – Objective (e.g., "drive traffic to the original post", "grow followers", "generate leads")

## Notes
- One great piece of content should give you 2+ weeks of social media posts. If you're creating new content for every post, you're working too hard.
- Post the repurposed content 3–7 days apart to avoid audience fatigue.
- The original content is your "pillar" — the social posts are the "spokes" that drive people back to it.
