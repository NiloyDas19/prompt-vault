## Purpose
Develop a strategic community engagement plan that grows organic reach through meaningful conversations, comment strategies, and audience participation.

## Prompt
Create a comprehensive community engagement strategy for my brand's social media presence. Go beyond "post and pray" — design a system for proactive engagement that builds real relationships.

**Brand:** {BRAND_NAME}
**Platforms:** {PLATFORMS}
**Current Follower Count:** {FOLLOWER_COUNT}
**Industry:** {INDUSTRY}
**Content Focus:** {CONTENT_FOCUS}
**Time Available for Engagement:** {TIME_PER_DAY}

### 1. Daily Engagement Routine ({TIME_PER_DAY}/day)
- Minute-by-minute schedule: what to do in the first 15 min, 30 min, and 60 min
- Which accounts to engage with (competitors' followers, industry leaders, target customers)
- How many comments, likes, and DMs per day

### 2. Comment Strategy
- **On your own posts:** How to reply to comments in a way that triggers more conversation (not just "Thanks! 🙏")
- Provide 10 example "conversation-extending" reply templates
- **On others' posts:** How to leave comments that attract people to your profile
- Provide 10 example "value-add" comment templates (not generic "Great post!" comments)

### 3. Engagement-Bait Content Ideas
- 10 post ideas specifically designed to maximize comments:
  - "This or That" polls
  - "Fill in the blank" posts
  - Controversial opinion prompts
  - "Wrong answers only" games
  - "Rate my..." posts
  - Caption contests

### 4. DM Outreach Strategy
- How to start genuine conversations via DMs without being salesy
- 5 DM templates for different scenarios: new followers, potential collaborators, ideal customers, industry peers

### 5. Community Building Tactics
- How to create a "brand evangelist" program from your most engaged followers
- UGC (User-Generated Content) campaign ideas
- Community hashtag strategy

## Variables
- {BRAND_NAME} – Your brand
- {PLATFORMS} – Active platforms
- {FOLLOWER_COUNT} – Current size (affects strategy — 500 followers needs different tactics than 50,000)
- {INDUSTRY} – Your industry
- {CONTENT_FOCUS} – What you post about
- {TIME_PER_DAY} – Realistic daily time budget for engagement (e.g., "30 minutes", "1 hour")

## Notes
- Engagement is more important than posting. A brand that posts 3x/week but engages daily will outgrow one that posts daily but never engages.
- The "Golden Hour" rule: the first 60 minutes after posting are critical. Engage with every comment immediately to signal the algorithm.
- Reply with questions, not statements. "Thanks!" kills a conversation. "Thanks! What's been your biggest challenge with this?" extends it.
