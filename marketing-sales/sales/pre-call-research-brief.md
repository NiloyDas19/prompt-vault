## Purpose
Generate a structured pre-call research brief on a prospect company and contact to help a salesperson walk into every meeting prepared and confident.

## Prompt
Help me prepare for a sales call by researching and organizing everything I need to know about this prospect. I want to walk into this meeting as the most prepared person in the room.

**Prospect Company:** {COMPANY_NAME}
**Industry:** {INDUSTRY}
**Company Website:** {WEBSITE}
**Contact Name:** {CONTACT_NAME}
**Contact Title:** {CONTACT_TITLE}
**My Product/Service:** {MY_PRODUCT}

Using the information available, compile a pre-call brief:

### 1. Company Overview
- What does the company do? (1–2 sentences)
- Company size (employees, revenue if public)
- Business model
- Target customers
- Key products or services

### 2. Recent News & Trigger Events
- Any recent funding, acquisitions, or product launches?
- Leadership changes or hires?
- Earnings reports or press releases?
- Awards, partnerships, or milestones?
- Why these events make NOW a good time to reach out

### 3. Industry Context
- Top 3 challenges facing this industry right now
- How our product addresses these challenges
- Relevant industry trends to reference on the call

### 4. Contact Intelligence
- Professional background (based on LinkedIn profile summary)
- How long in current role?
- Previous companies and roles
- Likely priorities given their title
- Potential talking points for rapport building

### 5. Competitive Landscape
- What solutions are they likely using today?
- Signs they may be evaluating alternatives
- How we differentiate from their likely current solution

### 6. Personalized Talk Track
Based on all of the above:
- **Opening line** personalized to this prospect
- **3 discovery questions** specific to their situation
- **Key value prop** framed for their industry and role
- **Potential objections** to anticipate
- **Ideal next step** for this type of prospect

## Variables
- {COMPANY_NAME} – The prospect company
- {INDUSTRY} – Their industry
- {WEBSITE} – Company website URL
- {CONTACT_NAME} – Person you're meeting
- {CONTACT_TITLE} – Their job title
- {MY_PRODUCT} – What you're selling

## Notes
- 5 minutes of research before a call is worth more than 30 minutes of feature presentations during the call.
- Reference something specific about the prospect in the first 60 seconds. "I noticed your company just launched X..." immediately signals you're not a generic cold caller.
- Save this research in your CRM for future calls with the same prospect.
