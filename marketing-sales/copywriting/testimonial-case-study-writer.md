## Purpose
Transform raw customer feedback, data, and interview notes into polished, persuasive testimonials and case studies.

## Prompt
Help me turn raw customer feedback into powerful marketing assets. Take the following information and create professional social proof content.

**Customer Name / Company:** {CUSTOMER_NAME}
**Their Industry:** {CUSTOMER_INDUSTRY}
**The problem they had BEFORE:** {BEFORE_STATE}
**What they used (your product):** {YOUR_PRODUCT}
**The result they achieved AFTER:** {AFTER_STATE}
**Specific metrics or data:** {METRICS}
**Raw quote / notes from the customer:** {RAW_FEEDBACK}

Create the following assets:

### 1. Short Testimonial (2–3 sentences)
- Punchy, quotable, could go on a landing page
- Format: "Before... Now... Thanks to..."

### 2. Detailed Case Study (500 words)
Structure:
- **The Challenge** — What the customer was struggling with
- **The Search** — What they tried before and why it didn't work
- **The Solution** — How they discovered and implemented your product
- **The Results** — Concrete outcomes with specific metrics
- **The Quote** — A polished version of their raw feedback
- **Key Takeaway** — One sentence summary a prospect can remember

### 3. Social Media Proof Snippet
- A short, engaging version for a social media post (Twitter/LinkedIn format)
- Include a hook, the key result, and a CTA

### 4. Video Testimonial Script (60 seconds)
- Write a natural-sounding script the customer could say on camera
- Structure: Problem → Discovery → Result → Recommendation

For all outputs:
- Never fabricate data — only use the metrics provided
- Keep the customer's authentic voice while making it more polished
- Highlight the most impressive metric in a "pull quote" format

## Variables
- {CUSTOMER_NAME} – Customer or company name (can be anonymized: "A Fortune 500 Retailer")
- {CUSTOMER_INDUSTRY} – Their industry
- {BEFORE_STATE} – Their situation before your product
- {YOUR_PRODUCT} – Your product/service name
- {AFTER_STATE} – Their situation after
- {METRICS} – Hard numbers (e.g., "40% increase in conversions", "saved 15 hours/week")
- {RAW_FEEDBACK} – Unpolished customer quotes or interview notes

## Notes
- The most persuasive testimonials include specific numbers. "It was great!" is weak. "We increased revenue by 40% in 90 days" is irresistible.
- Always get written permission from customers before publishing any testimonial.
- Case studies with a clear "Before vs. After" structure outperform feature-focused case studies every time.
