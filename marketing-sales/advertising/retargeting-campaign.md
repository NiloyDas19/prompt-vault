## Purpose
Design a retargeting campaign strategy that re-engages website visitors and abandoned users across ad platforms to recover lost conversions.

## Prompt
Design a complete retargeting campaign strategy. Help me bring back people who visited my site but didn't convert.

**Business:** {BUSINESS}
**Website Monthly Traffic:** {TRAFFIC}
**Average Conversion Rate:** {CONVERSION_RATE}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Retargeting Budget:** {BUDGET}
**Platforms:** {PLATFORMS}
**Pixel/Tracking Status:** {TRACKING}

### 1. Audience Segmentation
Create retargeting audiences based on behavior:
| Segment | Behavior | Recency Window | Priority | Message |
|---------|----------|---------------|----------|---------|
| Hot leads | Cart/form abandoners | 1–3 days | Highest | |
| Warm leads | Product page visitors | 1–7 days | High | |
| Engaged visitors | Multiple page visits | 7–14 days | Medium | |
| Cold visitors | Bounced from homepage | 14–30 days | Low | |

### 2. Creative Strategy Per Segment
For each segment:
- Ad message (what to say to bring them back)
- Offer/incentive (if applicable)
- Creative format recommendation (image, video, carousel)
- Ad copy (primary text + headline)
- CTA

### 3. Frequency Capping
- Maximum times to show ads per person per day/week
- When to stop retargeting (after X days or Y impressions with no action)
- How to prevent ad fatigue

### 4. Cross-Platform Coordination
- How to coordinate retargeting across Google Display, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.
- Budget allocation per platform based on audience behavior
- Unified measurement approach

### 5. Exclusion Strategy
- Who to EXCLUDE from retargeting (existing customers, irrelevant visitors, bots)
- How to prevent showing retargeting ads to people who already converted

### 6. Performance Tracking
- Key metrics: click-through rate, conversion rate, ROAS, cost per recovered conversion
- Attribution model recommendation
- How to measure true incremental lift from retargeting

## Variables
- {BUSINESS} – Your business
- {TRAFFIC} – Monthly website traffic
- {CONVERSION_RATE} – Current conversion rate
- {PRODUCT} – What you sell
- {BUDGET} – Retargeting budget
- {PLATFORMS} – Ad platforms to use
- {TRACKING} – Pixel/tracking setup status

## Notes
- Retargeting is the highest-ROI ad channel for most businesses because the audience already knows you. Don't neglect it.
- The #1 mistake: showing the same ad to every retargeting audience. Cart abandoners need a different message than homepage bouncers.
- Frequency capping is crucial. Nobody buys from the brand that stalks them across the internet for 3 months with the same ad.
