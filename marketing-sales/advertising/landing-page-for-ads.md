## Purpose
Design a high-converting landing page specifically matched to an ad campaign, with message match, trust signals, and conversion optimization built in.

## Prompt
Design a landing page that matches my ad campaign and maximizes conversions. The visitor clicked an ad — now convert them.

**Ad that sends traffic here:**
- Ad headline: {AD_HEADLINE}
- Ad promise: {AD_PROMISE}
- Target keyword (if search): {KEYWORD}

**Offer:** {OFFER}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Conversion Goal:** {CONVERSION_GOAL}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}

### Landing Page Design:

**1. Message Match (Above the Fold)**
- Headline that mirrors the ad headline (prevents "where am I?" confusion)
- Subheadline that expands on the promise
- Hero visual recommendation
- Primary CTA button (text and color)
- Trust indicator: "Trusted by 500+ companies" or similar

**2. Problem Section**
- 3 pain points the visitor is likely experiencing
- Written in their language, not yours

**3. Solution Section**
- How your offer solves each pain point
- Benefits, not features (what they GET, not what it DOES)
- Visual proof: screenshot, demo GIF, or before/after

**4. Social Proof Section**
- Testimonial format and placement
- Logo bar of recognizable customers
- Statistics: "X customers", "Y% improvement", "Z results"

**5. How It Works**
- 3-step process (simple enough to not feel overwhelming)
- Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3 → Result

**6. Objection Handling**
- Top 3 objections addressed with copy
- Risk reversal: money-back guarantee, free trial, no credit card required
- Trust badges: security, privacy, review platforms

**7. Final CTA Section**
- Urgency element (if authentic)
- Restate the key benefit
- CTA button with action-oriented text
- What happens after they click (set expectations)

**8. Technical Specs**
- Mobile optimization requirements
- Page load speed targets (under 3 seconds)
- Form field recommendations (fewer = higher conversion)
- Tracking: pixels, UTM parameters, conversion events

### No-Go List:
- Navigation menu (remove it — prevent exit)
- Multiple offers (one offer, one CTA)
- External links (keep them on this page)

## Variables
- {AD_HEADLINE} – The headline in your ad
- {AD_PROMISE} – What the ad promised
- {KEYWORD} – Search keyword if applicable
- {OFFER} – What you're offering on this page
- {AUDIENCE} – Who clicked the ad
- {CONVERSION_GOAL} – What you want them to do (e.g., "fill out form", "start free trial", "purchase")
- {PRODUCT} – Your product/service

## Notes
- "Message match" between ad and landing page is the #1 conversion factor. If the ad says "Free SEO Audit" and the landing page says "Welcome to Our Agency," you've lost them.
- Remove ALL navigation from ad landing pages. The only options should be: convert or leave.
- Every element on the page should answer one question: "Does this move the visitor closer to converting?" If not, remove it.
