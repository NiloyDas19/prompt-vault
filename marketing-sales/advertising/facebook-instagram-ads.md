## Purpose
Design a Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising strategy with audience targeting, creative briefs, and campaign structure optimized for your budget.

## Prompt
Design a complete Facebook/Instagram advertising strategy for my business. Structure the campaigns for efficient spending and maximum conversions.

**Business:** {BUSINESS}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Monthly Budget:** {BUDGET}
**Campaign Objective:** {OBJECTIVE}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Existing Pixel Data:** {PIXEL_STATUS}
**Creative Assets Available:** {ASSETS}

### Campaign Strategy:

**1. Campaign Architecture**
- Full funnel structure: TOFU (awareness), MOFU (consideration), BOFU (conversion)
- Campaign objective for each funnel stage
- Budget split recommendation across funnel stages (e.g., 20/30/50)

**2. Audience Targeting**
For each funnel stage:
- **Cold audiences:** Interest-based, lookalike, demographic targeting details
- **Warm audiences:** Website visitors, video viewers, engagers
- **Hot audiences:** Add-to-cart, checkout abandoners, past buyers
- Audience size recommendations
- Exclusion audiences (prevent ad fatigue and wasted spend)

**3. Creative Strategy**
For each funnel stage, write:
- **Primary text** (3 variations: short, medium, long)
- **Headline** (3 variations)
- **Description** (2 variations)
- **Creative brief:** What the image/video should show
- **CTA button** recommendation

**4. Ad Formats**
Recommend and brief these formats:
- Single image ad
- Video ad (15-sec and 30-sec concepts)
- Carousel ad (slide-by-slide outline)
- Collection ad (if e-commerce)

**5. Testing Plan**
- Phase 1: Creative testing (what to test first)
- Phase 2: Audience testing (which audiences respond best)
- Phase 3: Scaling (increase spend on winners)
- When to kill an ad (performance thresholds)

**6. Reporting & Optimization**
- Key metrics per funnel stage
- Optimization schedule: daily, weekly, monthly actions
- When to refresh creative (ad fatigue indicators)

## Variables
- {BUSINESS} – Your business
- {PRODUCT} – What you're advertising
- {BUDGET} – Monthly ad budget
- {OBJECTIVE} – Goal (e.g., "leads", "purchases", "app installs", "brand awareness")
- {AUDIENCE} – Target audience description
- {PIXEL_STATUS} – Facebook Pixel status (e.g., "fresh install, no data", "established with 500+ conversions")
- {ASSETS} – Available creative (e.g., "product photos only", "we can shoot video", "UGC available")

## Notes
- Cold traffic needs education and trust. Don't show a cold audience a "Buy Now" ad — show them a problem/solution ad first.
- Creative is the new targeting. With Meta's broad targeting improvements, the quality and variety of your ad creative matters more than ever.
- Budget pacing: let campaigns run for 3–5 days before making changes. The learning phase needs data to optimize.
