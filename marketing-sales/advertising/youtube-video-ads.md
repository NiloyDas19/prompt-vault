Design a YouTube and video advertising strategy with script concepts, targeting, and campaign structure optimized for different video ad formats.

### 1. Campaign Architecture
- Campaign types: Skippable in-stream, non-skippable, bumper, discovery, Shorts
- Recommended mix based on budget and goal
- Audience targeting strategy: demographics, interests, custom intent, placements

### 2. Video Ad Scripts

**Script A: 6-Second Bumper Ad**
- Visual + voiceover for a non-skippable 6-second ad
- Key rule: ONE message, ONE visual, ONE CTA

**Script B: 15-Second Non-Skippable**
- Hook (0–3 sec) → Problem/Solution (3–12 sec) → CTA (12–15 sec)

**Script C: 30-Second Skippable In-Stream**
- Hook (0–5 sec): Must earn the viewer's attention BEFORE they can skip
- Core message (5–20 sec): Problem/solution with proof
- CTA (20–30 sec): Clear next step
- Note: The first 5 seconds are everything in skippable ads

**Script D: 60-Second Storytelling Ad**
- Full narrative arc: relatable character → problem → discovery → transformation → CTA
- Optimized for "TrueView for Action" campaigns

### 3. Targeting Strategy
- In-market audiences relevant to your product
- Custom intent audiences (based on search behavior)
- Placement targeting (specific YouTube channels and videos)
- Remarketing lists: website visitors, channel viewers, past customers

### 4. Bidding & Optimization
- Recommended bid strategy per campaign type
- Cost benchmarks: CPV, CPM, CPA ranges for your industry
- Optimization tips for the first 2 weeks

### 5. Creative Testing Plan
- What elements to test (hook, CTA, length, talent)
- How to measure creative performance beyond views (watch-through rate, earned actions)

## Notes
- YouTube is the 2nd largest search engine. Use it like search: target people based on what they're looking for, not just demographics.
- The first 5 seconds of a skippable ad ARE your ad. If someone skips at 5 seconds, you still got 5 seconds of free brand exposure.
- YouTube Shorts ads are rapidly growing. If you have vertical video content, test Shorts campaigns — CPMs are significantly lower.
