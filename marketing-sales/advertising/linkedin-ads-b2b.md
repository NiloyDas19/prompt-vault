## Purpose
Design LinkedIn advertising campaigns optimized for B2B lead generation with precise professional targeting and high-converting ad formats.

## Prompt
Design a LinkedIn advertising strategy for B2B lead generation. LinkedIn ads are expensive — help me maximize every dollar.

**Company:** {COMPANY}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Monthly Budget:** {BUDGET}
**Target Decision Maker:** {TARGET_PERSONA}
**Offer/Lead Magnet:** {OFFER}
**Average Deal Size:** {DEAL_SIZE}
**Sales Cycle Length:** {SALES_CYCLE}

### 1. Campaign Architecture
- Campaign objective selection (Lead Gen Form vs. Website Conversions vs. Brand Awareness)
- Campaign structure recommendation
- Budget allocation across campaigns

### 2. Targeting Strategy
Create 3 audience segments:
- **Segment A (Narrow/High-Intent):** Job titles, company size, industry filters
- **Segment B (Broader/Interest-Based):** Skills, groups, member interests
- **Segment C (Retargeting):** Website visitors, company page visitors, video viewers
For each: estimated audience size, expected CPC, and rationale

### 3. Ad Creative (per campaign)
**Sponsored Content Ad (3 variations):**
- Intro text (under 150 characters for mobile)
- Headline (under 70 characters)
- Description
- CTA button selection
- Image/video direction

**Message Ad (InMail):**
- Subject line
- Body copy (under 500 characters for best performance)
- CTA button text
- Sender profile recommendation

**Lead Gen Form Design:**
- Which fields to include (fewer = higher conversion)
- Custom questions
- Thank you message
- Hidden fields for tracking

### 4. Content Strategy for LinkedIn Ads
What to promote at each funnel stage:
- TOFU: Thought leadership, industry reports, educational content
- MOFU: Case studies, webinars, comparison guides
- BOFU: Free trials, demos, consultations

### 5. Optimization Playbook
- First 2 weeks: what to monitor and when to make changes
- Key metrics: CTR benchmark (0.4–0.6% for feed ads), CPL targets
- When to pause underperforming ads
- How to scale winning campaigns

## Variables
- {COMPANY} – Your company
- {PRODUCT} – What you sell
- {BUDGET} – Monthly LinkedIn ad budget
- {TARGET_PERSONA} – Ideal decision maker (title, seniority, company size, industry)
- {OFFER} – What you're offering (e.g., "free whitepaper", "demo booking", "free trial")
- {DEAL_SIZE} – Average deal value (justifies higher CPC on LinkedIn)
- {SALES_CYCLE} – How long deals take

## Notes
- LinkedIn CPCs are 5–10x higher than Facebook. This only makes sense for B2B with deal sizes above $5K+ where precise professional targeting justifies the cost.
- Lead Gen Forms consistently outperform landing page campaigns on LinkedIn because they pre-fill user data — reducing friction to almost zero.
- LinkedIn ads perform best Tuesday–Thursday during business hours. Avoid weekends for B2B campaigns.
