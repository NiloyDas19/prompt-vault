## Purpose
Design a complete Google Ads campaign structure with ad groups, keywords, ad copy, and bidding strategy from scratch.

## Prompt
Design a complete Google Ads campaign for the following business. Structure it for maximum relevance and minimum wasted spend.

**Business:** {BUSINESS}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Monthly Budget:** {BUDGET}
**Target Geography:** {GEOGRAPHY}
**Campaign Goal:** {GOAL}
**Target Customer:** {CUSTOMER}
**Landing Page(s):** {LANDING_PAGES}

### Campaign Architecture:

**1. Campaign Structure**
- Recommended campaign type (Search, Performance Max, Display, etc.) and why
- Campaign-level settings: bidding strategy, budget allocation, schedule, locations

**2. Ad Groups (5–8 per campaign)**
For each ad group:
- Theme/intent
- 10 keywords (mix of exact, phrase, and broad match)
- 5 negative keywords
- Match type strategy

**3. Ad Copy (per ad group)**
Responsive Search Ad:
- 10 headlines (max 30 characters each)
- 4 descriptions (max 90 characters each)
- Pin recommendations (which headlines to pin to which positions)
- All headlines must include keywords naturally

**4. Extensions**
- Sitelink extensions (4): text and description
- Callout extensions (4): key selling points
- Structured snippets: relevant category
- Call extension details

**5. Keyword Strategy**
- High-intent keywords (bottom of funnel, ready to buy)
- Research-intent keywords (top of funnel, learning)
- Competitor keywords (brand names to target or avoid)
- Negative keyword list (prevent wasted spend)

**6. Bidding & Budget Recommendations**
- Starting bid strategy recommendation
- Daily budget allocation across campaigns
- Expected CPC range for this industry
- When to switch from manual to automated bidding

**7. Optimization Roadmap**
- Week 1: Launch settings and monitoring plan
- Week 2–4: First optimizations based on data
- Month 2+: Scaling and testing plan

## Variables
- {BUSINESS} – Your business
- {PRODUCT} – What you're advertising
- {BUDGET} – Monthly ad budget
- {GEOGRAPHY} – Target locations
- {GOAL} – Campaign objective (e.g., "lead generation", "e-commerce sales", "phone calls", "app installs")
- {CUSTOMER} – Target customer
- {LANDING_PAGES} – URLs you'll send traffic to

## Notes
- The #1 mistake in Google Ads: sending traffic to your homepage instead of a dedicated landing page. Always match the ad to a specific, relevant landing page.
- Start with exact match keywords and expand to phrase/broad as you learn what converts. Going broad too early burns budget.
- Negative keywords are as important as target keywords. Review your search terms report weekly and add negatives aggressively.
