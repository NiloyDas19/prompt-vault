## Purpose
Write a detailed creative brief for designers and videographers to produce ad creatives that align with your campaign strategy and brand.

## Prompt
Write a comprehensive creative brief for ad creative production. This brief should give a designer or videographer everything they need to produce on-brand, high-performing ad assets.

**Campaign Name:** {CAMPAIGN_NAME}
**Brand:** {BRAND}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Campaign Objective:** {OBJECTIVE}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Platforms:** {PLATFORMS}
**Budget for Creative Production:** {PRODUCTION_BUDGET}
**Deadline:** {DEADLINE}

### Creative Brief:

**1. Project Overview**
- Campaign background and context
- What we're promoting and why now

**2. Target Audience Profile**
- Who we're talking to (demographics, psychographics)
- Their current mindset (what they're thinking and feeling)
- What action we want them to take

**3. Key Message**
- Single most important takeaway (ONE sentence)
- Supporting messages (2–3 bullet points)
- Mandatory inclusions (logo, CTA, legal disclaimers)

**4. Creative Deliverables**
| Asset | Format | Dimensions | Duration | Platform |
|-------|--------|-----------|----------|----------|

**5. Visual Direction**
- Mood and style (describe the feeling, not just the look)
- Color palette references
- Photography/illustration style
- Typography preferences
- Do's and don'ts (with visual references if possible)

**6. Copy Elements**
For each asset:
- Headline
- Body copy
- CTA text
- Legal/disclaimer text

**7. Competitive References**
- 3 competitor ads to learn from (what works about each)
- 2 competitor ads to avoid resembling (what doesn't work)

**8. Brand Guidelines**
- Logo usage requirements
- Brand colors (hex codes)
- Font specifications
- Tone of voice reminders

**9. Approval Process**
- Who reviews and approves
- Number of revision rounds included
- Final file format and delivery specifications

## Variables
- {CAMPAIGN_NAME} – Campaign name
- {BRAND} – Your brand
- {PRODUCT} – What you're promoting
- {OBJECTIVE} – Campaign goal
- {AUDIENCE} – Target audience
- {PLATFORMS} – Where ads will run (determines dimensions and specs)
- {PRODUCTION_BUDGET} – Budget for creative production
- {DEADLINE} – When assets are due

## Notes
- A great creative brief prevents 80% of revision rounds. Invest the time upfront.
- Include reference images/videos whenever possible. "Make it feel premium and modern" means different things to different designers. Show, don't just tell.
- The single most important element: ONE clear message. Ads that try to say 3 things end up saying nothing.
