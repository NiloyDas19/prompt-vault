## Purpose
Write a complete promotional content suite for an event, webinar, or virtual summit — from registration page copy to post-event follow-up.

## Prompt
Create a complete promotional content suite for the following event. Cover every touchpoint from announcement to post-event follow-up.

**Event Name:** {EVENT_NAME}
**Event Type:** {EVENT_TYPE}
**Date/Time:** {DATE_TIME}
**Speakers/Host:** {SPEAKERS}
**Topic:** {TOPIC}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Registration Goal:** {REG_GOAL}
**Brand Voice:** {BRAND_VOICE}

### Pre-Event Content:

**1. Registration Page Copy**
- Headline and subheadline
- "What You'll Learn" (3–5 bullet points)
- Speaker bios (brief)
- Trust elements (attendee count, company logos)
- CTA button text
- FAQ section

**2. Email Promotion Sequence (4 emails)**
- **Email 1 (3 weeks before):** Announcement + early bird
- **Email 2 (2 weeks before):** Speaker spotlight + agenda
- **Email 3 (1 week before):** Social proof + urgency
- **Email 4 (Day of):** Last chance + direct link

**3. Social Media Promotion (5 posts)**
- Announcement post
- Speaker feature post
- "What you'll learn" carousel outline
- Countdown post (1 day before)
- Live/happening now post

### During Event:
- Live-tweeting templates (5 tweet templates with blanks for key insights)
- Audience engagement prompts (polls, questions to ask during the event)

### Post-Event:

**4. Follow-Up Email (for attendees)**
- Thank you + recording link
- Key takeaways summary
- Next step CTA

**5. Follow-Up Email (for no-shows)**
- "You missed it, but here's the recording"
- FOMO highlights
- CTA for next event

**6. Post-Event Content**
- Blog post summarizing key takeaways
- Social media posts with best quotes from the event

## Variables
- {EVENT_NAME} – Event name
- {EVENT_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "live webinar", "virtual summit", "in-person conference talk", "workshop")
- {DATE_TIME} – Date and time
- {SPEAKERS} – Who's presenting
- {TOPIC} – What the event covers
- {AUDIENCE} – Target attendees
- {REG_GOAL} – Registration target (e.g., "500 registrations")
- {BRAND_VOICE} – Tone

## Notes
- Expect 40–50% of registrants to actually attend live. Plan your registration goal accordingly.
- The recording is often more valuable than the live event. Promote the replay aggressively to no-shows and new audiences.
- Follow-up emails within 24 hours of the event have the highest engagement. Don't wait a week.
