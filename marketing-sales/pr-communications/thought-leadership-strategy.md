## Purpose
Build a thought leadership strategy that positions you or your company as a recognized authority in your industry.

## Prompt
Design a thought leadership strategy that gets me recognized as an authority in {INDUSTRY}. I want people to think of me when they think of {TOPIC}.

**Person/Brand:** {NAME}
**Current Role:** {ROLE}
**Industry:** {INDUSTRY}
**Topic to Own:** {TOPIC}
**Current Visibility:** {CURRENT_VISIBILITY}
**Goal:** {GOAL}
**Time Available:** {TIME_COMMITMENT}

### 1. Thought Leadership Positioning
- Your unique point of view: what contrarian or distinctive perspective do you bring?
- 3 "signature ideas" you'll be known for
- Your credibility foundation: experience, data, results that back your authority

### 2. Content Strategy (90-Day Plan)
**Owned media (monthly):**
- 2 long-form articles (LinkedIn, personal blog, or Medium)
- Topics, angles, and outlines for each

**Social media (weekly):**
- 3 LinkedIn posts per week: 1 insight, 1 story, 1 opinion
- Content themes mapped to your signature ideas

**Speaking & Events:**
- 5 conference or podcast pitch targets
- Speaker bio and talk abstracts for 3 potential talks
- How to get on podcast guest lists

### 3. Earned Media Strategy
- 5 publications to target for guest articles or expert commentary
- How to pitch yourself as an expert source for journalists
- HARO/Qwoted strategy for reactive media opportunities

### 4. Community Building
- Where your audience gathers (communities, events, forums)
- How to contribute meaningfully without self-promoting
- Relationship building with other thought leaders in the space

### 5. Content Amplification
- How to maximize reach for each piece of content
- Repurposing strategy: one talk → 10 content pieces
- Strategic commenting and engagement plan

### 6. Measurement
- How to track thought leadership impact (it's not just followers)
- Leading indicators: speaking invitations, journalist inbound, DMs from peers
- Lagging indicators: brand awareness surveys, inbound leads, partnership offers

## Variables
- {NAME} – Your name or brand
- {ROLE} – Current job title
- {INDUSTRY} – Your industry
- {TOPIC} – The specific topic you want to own
- {CURRENT_VISIBILITY} – Where you are now (e.g., "unknown", "known in my company", "known in my local market", "moderately visible in the industry")
- {GOAL} – What you want (e.g., "become a go-to media source", "get invited to keynote conferences", "generate inbound leads")
- {TIME_COMMITMENT} – How much time per week (e.g., "2 hours", "5 hours", "10+ hours")

## Notes
- Thought leadership is a 12–24 month investment. There are no shortcuts to genuine authority.
- The key differentiator: don't repeat what everyone else is saying. Have a distinctive point of view, even if it's controversial.
- Consistency beats intensity. 2 hours per week for a year beats 40 hours in one month.
