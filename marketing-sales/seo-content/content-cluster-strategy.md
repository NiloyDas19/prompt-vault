## Purpose
Design a topic cluster architecture with pillar pages and supporting content that establishes topical authority and dominates search results for your niche.

## Prompt
Design a complete content cluster strategy for my website. Build a topic architecture that signals topical authority to Google and creates a comprehensive resource for my audience.

**Main Topic/Niche:** {MAIN_TOPIC}
**Website:** {WEBSITE}
**Business Goal:** {BUSINESS_GOAL}
**Current Content (approximate):** {EXISTING_CONTENT}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Top 3 Competitors:** {COMPETITORS}

### Deliverables:

**1. Topic Cluster Architecture**
Design 3–5 topic clusters. For each cluster:
- **Pillar Page** — The comprehensive, 3,000+ word resource page
  - Title and target keyword
  - Outline of what it should cover (10+ sections)
  - Content format (ultimate guide, hub page, or how-to)
- **Supporting Content** (8–12 articles per cluster)
  - Title, target keyword, and word count for each
  - How each article links back to the pillar page
  - How articles interlink with each other within the cluster

**2. Internal Linking Map**
- Visual description of how all content connects
- Anchor text suggestions for each link
- Link priority: which links matter most for SEO

**3. Content Production Roadmap**
- Recommended publishing order (which pillar first, which supporting articles)
- Timeline: weeks 1–12
- Priority tiers: must-have, should-have, nice-to-have

**4. Topical Authority Scoring**
- Estimate how comprehensive your coverage is vs. competitors
- Identify the biggest topical gaps to fill
- Suggest "adjacent topics" that would expand your authority

**5. Measurement Plan**
- KPIs to track: organic traffic, keyword rankings, internal link clicks
- When to expect results (realistic timeline)
- How to know if a cluster is "done" vs. needs more content

## Variables
- {MAIN_TOPIC} – Your core topic area
- {WEBSITE} – Your website URL
- {BUSINESS_GOAL} – What you want SEO to achieve (e.g., "drive demo requests", "increase organic product sales")
- {EXISTING_CONTENT} – What you already have (e.g., "50 blog posts, no clear structure")
- {AUDIENCE} – Who you're writing for
- {COMPETITORS} – Top competing websites

## Notes
- Google's Helpful Content system rewards sites that demonstrate topical depth. A cluster of 15 interlinked articles on one topic will outrank 15 random articles on different topics.
- Build ONE cluster to completion before starting the next. Half-built clusters provide minimal SEO value.
- Pillar pages should be "living documents" — update them quarterly with new data, links, and sections.
