## Purpose
Expand a seed keyword into a comprehensive keyword universe with search intent classification, difficulty estimates, and content mapping.

## Prompt
Take the following seed keyword and expand it into a complete keyword research universe. Go beyond the obvious — find the long-tail gold.

**Seed Keyword:** {SEED_KEYWORD}
**Industry/Niche:** {NICHE}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Business Model:** {BUSINESS_MODEL}
**Current Domain Authority (approximate):** {DOMAIN_AUTHORITY}

Generate:

### 1. Core Keywords (10)
- Direct variations of the seed keyword
- Classify each by search intent: Informational (I), Commercial (C), Transactional (T), Navigational (N)

### 2. Long-Tail Keywords (20)
- 3+ word phrases that are more specific and less competitive
- Focus on "question keywords" (how, what, why, when, where)
- Include "comparison keywords" (X vs Y, best X for Y)

### 3. "People Also Ask" Keywords (10)
- Questions people are asking related to this topic
- These are prime Featured Snippet opportunities

### 4. Commercial Intent Keywords (10)
- Keywords that indicate buying readiness
- Include: "best", "review", "pricing", "alternative to", "vs", "comparison"

### 5. Content Cluster Map
- Group all keywords into 4–6 topic clusters
- Identify a "pillar page" keyword for each cluster
- Map supporting keywords to individual blog posts
- Show the interlinking structure

### 6. Keyword Prioritization Matrix
| Keyword | Est. Volume | Difficulty | Intent | Priority | Content Type |
|---------|-------------|------------|--------|----------|-------------|

Prioritize by: Quick Wins (low difficulty, decent volume), Strategic Plays (high volume, worth the effort), and Long Shots (high difficulty, only if you have authority).

### 7. Content Calendar Suggestion
- Which keywords to target first, second, third
- Recommended publishing cadence

## Variables
- {SEED_KEYWORD} – Starting keyword (e.g., "email marketing", "project management tool", "vegan protein powder")
- {NICHE} – Your niche
- {AUDIENCE} – Who you're targeting
- {BUSINESS_MODEL} – How you make money (e.g., "SaaS", "e-commerce", "affiliate", "agency")
- {DOMAIN_AUTHORITY} – Estimated DA (affects difficulty targeting — lower DA should focus on lower competition keywords)

## Notes
- AI can't give you real search volume data — use this output as a starting framework, then validate with Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google Keyword Planner.
- Long-tail keywords (4+ words) often have lower volume but much higher conversion rates.
- Target keywords where you can realistically rank on page 1 given your current domain authority.
