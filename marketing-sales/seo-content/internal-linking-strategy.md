## Purpose
Design an internal linking strategy that distributes page authority, improves crawlability, and helps users discover related content.

## Prompt
Analyze my content inventory and design an internal linking strategy that maximizes SEO value and user experience.

**Website:** {WEBSITE}
**Content Inventory (list of pages):** {CONTENT_LIST}
**Most Important Pages (revenue/conversion pages):** {PRIORITY_PAGES}
**Current Internal Linking:** {CURRENT_STATE}

### 1. Link Architecture Analysis
- Which pages have the most authority (receive the most backlinks)?
- Which priority pages are under-linked internally?
- Identify "orphan pages" — pages with no internal links pointing to them
- Identify "link hoarding" pages — pages that receive many links but don't pass authority onward

### 2. Strategic Linking Plan
For each priority page:
- Which 5–10 existing pages should link TO this page?
- Suggested anchor text for each link (varied, natural)
- Where in each source page to place the link (intro, body, CTA section)

### 3. Contextual Linking Opportunities
- For each page in the content inventory, suggest 2–3 contextual link insertions
- Format: "In [Page A], add a link to [Page B] with anchor text '[suggested text]' in the paragraph about [topic]"

### 4. Navigation & Hub Pages
- Recommend hub/resource pages that act as linking hubs for each topic cluster
- Suggest breadcrumb structure improvements
- Footer and sidebar link recommendations

### 5. Linking Rules & Guidelines
- Maximum links per page recommendation
- Anchor text variation rules (exact match, partial match, branded, generic)
- Follow vs. nofollow guidance for internal links
- How to handle links in boilerplate elements (header, footer, sidebar)

## Variables
- {WEBSITE} – Your website
- {CONTENT_LIST} – List of pages with titles and URLs
- {PRIORITY_PAGES} – The pages you most want to rank (e.g., product pages, service pages, key landing pages)
- {CURRENT_STATE} – Description of current internal linking (e.g., "random, no strategy", "basic nav links only")

## Notes
- Internal links are one of the most underutilized SEO levers. They're free, entirely in your control, and take effect within weeks.
- Link FROM your highest-authority pages TO your highest-priority pages. Authority flows through internal links.
- Anchor text matters for internal links — use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text (unlike external links where you want variation).
