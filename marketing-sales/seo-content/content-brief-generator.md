## Purpose
Generate a comprehensive content brief that a writer can follow to produce a high-quality, SEO-optimized article without additional guidance.

## Prompt
Create a detailed content brief for a writer. The brief should be so thorough that the writer can produce a publish-ready article without any follow-up questions.

**Article Topic:** {TOPIC}
**Target Keyword:** {TARGET_KEYWORD}
**Target URL Slug:** {URL_SLUG}
**Writer Skill Level:** {WRITER_LEVEL}
**Deadline:** {DEADLINE}

### Content Brief:

**1. Article Overview**
- Working title (2–3 options)
- Target word count
- Target audience persona (who is reading this and why)
- Primary goal of the article (rank for keyword, generate leads, educate)
- Tone and voice guidelines

**2. SEO Requirements**
- Primary keyword and required usage count
- Secondary keywords (5–8) to include naturally
- Title tag and meta description
- URL slug recommendation
- Internal links to include (suggest 3–5 existing pages)
- External links guidance (what types of sources to cite)

**3. Search Intent Analysis**
- What the searcher is actually looking for
- What questions they have at this stage
- What action they should take after reading

**4. Competitive Analysis**
- Summary of what the top 3 ranking articles cover
- What they do well
- What they're missing (your opportunity to differentiate)

**5. Detailed Outline**
- Every H2 and H3 heading
- Bullet points describing what each section should cover
- Suggested examples, data, or stories to include
- Callout boxes, quotes, or visual element suggestions

**6. Do's and Don'ts**
- Specific instructions on tone, formatting, and style
- Words/phrases to use and avoid
- Common mistakes to watch out for in this topic

**7. Visual/Media Requirements**
- How many images needed
- Chart or infographic suggestions
- Screenshot requirements
- Alt text guidelines

**8. CTA and Conversion**
- What CTA to include and where to place it
- Lead magnet or next step to promote

## Variables
- {TOPIC} – Article topic
- {TARGET_KEYWORD} – Primary SEO keyword
- {URL_SLUG} – Suggested URL path
- {WRITER_LEVEL} – Writer experience (e.g., "junior freelancer", "senior in-house", "subject matter expert")
- {DEADLINE} – When the draft is due

## Notes
- A 30-minute content brief saves 3+ hours of revisions. It's the highest-ROI activity in content marketing.
- Always include the search intent — it's the #1 reason articles fail to rank. A "how-to" keyword needs a tutorial, not a product page.
- Include examples of competitor articles (URLs) so the writer can see what "good" looks like in this space.
