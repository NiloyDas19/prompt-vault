## Purpose
Optimize content specifically for AI-powered search engines (Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT search) that cite and summarize web content.

## Prompt
Restructure and optimize the following content for Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) — ensuring it gets cited by AI search engines like Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT browsing.

**Content Topic:** {TOPIC}
**Target Query:** {TARGET_QUERY}
**Current Content (paste or summarize):** {CURRENT_CONTENT}
**Content Goal:** {GOAL}

### 1. Direct Answer Blocks
For the top 5 queries related to this topic:
- Write a **concise, authoritative answer** (40–60 words) immediately under a question-formatted heading
- Structure: `## [Question]` → direct answer paragraph → supporting detail
- These should be self-contained — an AI can extract and cite them without additional context

### 2. Content Structure for AI Citability
Restructure the content using:
- **Clear question-based H2 headers** (match how people ask AI assistants)
- **Definition-style opening sentences** for each section ("X is a...")
- **Comparison tables** where appropriate (AI loves structured data)
- **Numbered lists and step-by-step processes** (easy to extract)
- **Statistical claims with sources** cited inline (builds trust for AI citation)

### 3. Entity Optimization
- Identify key entities in this topic (people, tools, concepts, companies)
- Ensure each entity is defined on first mention
- Create clear relationships between entities ("X is a type of Y used for Z")

### 4. Source Authority Signals
- How to add E-E-A-T signals that AI systems trust
- Author expertise markers
- Citation format that AI engines prefer
- Freshness signals (current year data, "updated [date]")

### 5. Multi-Format Answers
For the primary query, provide the answer in:
- Paragraph format (for AI Overviews)
- Bullet list format (for featured snippets)
- Table format (for comparison queries)
- Step-by-step format (for "how to" queries)

## Variables
- {TOPIC} – Content topic
- {TARGET_QUERY} – The primary question people ask AI about this topic
- {CURRENT_CONTENT} – Existing content to restructure
- {GOAL} – What you want (e.g., "get cited by Perplexity", "appear in Google AI Overviews")

## Notes
- AEO is the next frontier of SEO. AI search engines are rapidly growing, and content that's structured for easy citation will win.
- AI engines prefer content that is: concise, well-structured, factually accurate, and from authoritative sources.
- Unlike traditional SEO, AEO rewards direct answers — don't bury the answer after 500 words of preamble.
