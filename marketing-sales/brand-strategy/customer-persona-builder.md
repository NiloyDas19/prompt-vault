## Purpose
Build detailed, actionable customer personas that go beyond demographics to reveal psychographics, motivations, objections, and media consumption habits.

## Prompt
Create 3 detailed customer personas for my business. Go deep — I need personas that my entire team can reference when making marketing, product, and sales decisions.

**Business:** {BUSINESS}
**Product/Service:** {PRODUCT}
**Industry:** {INDUSTRY}
**Current Customer Base (describe):** {CURRENT_CUSTOMERS}
**Business Model:** {BUSINESS_MODEL}

For each persona, provide:

### Demographics
- Name (fictional but memorable)
- Age range, gender, location
- Job title, company size, income range
- Education level

### Psychographics
- Values and beliefs
- Lifestyle and interests
- Personality type (e.g., analytical, creative, driven, cautious)
- What they read, watch, and listen to
- Social media platforms they use and how

### Professional Life
- Daily responsibilities and challenges
- How they're measured/evaluated at work
- Tools and software they currently use
- Industry trends affecting them

### Buying Behavior
- How they discover new products (search, social, word of mouth, events)
- How they evaluate options (reviews, demos, free trials, peer recommendations)
- Who else is involved in the buying decision
- Typical budget authority and procurement process
- Deal breakers and must-haves

### Pain Points & Goals
- Top 3 frustrations in their work/life that your product addresses
- Top 3 goals they're trying to achieve
- What does "success" look like for them in 12 months?
- What keeps them up at night?

### Messaging Guidance
- What language resonates with this persona (actual phrases they'd use)
- What messaging turns them off (words and tones to avoid)
- Their primary objection to your product
- The single most compelling thing you could say to them
- The content format they prefer (blog, video, podcast, whitepaper)

### The "Day in the Life" Narrative
Write a 100-word narrative of a typical day for this persona, weaving in their frustrations and the moment they'd encounter your product.

## Variables
- {BUSINESS} – Your business name
- {PRODUCT} – What you sell
- {INDUSTRY} – Your industry
- {CURRENT_CUSTOMERS} – Description of who currently buys from you
- {BUSINESS_MODEL} – How you sell (B2B, B2C, SaaS, e-commerce, etc.)

## Notes
- Personas should be based on real customer data when possible. Interview 5–10 customers and feed their actual language into this prompt.
- The "Day in the Life" narrative is the most underrated section — it builds empathy across your entire team.
- Review personas quarterly. Your best customers 2 years ago may not be your best customers today.
