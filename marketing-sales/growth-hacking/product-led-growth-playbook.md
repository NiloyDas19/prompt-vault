## Purpose
Design a product-led growth strategy where the product itself drives acquisition, conversion, and expansion — reducing reliance on sales teams.

## Prompt
Help me build a product-led growth (PLG) strategy. I want the product to be our primary growth engine.

**Product:** {PRODUCT}
**Current Growth Model:** {CURRENT_MODEL}
**Free Tier / Trial:** {FREE_OFFERING}
**Current Conversion Rate (free to paid):** {CONVERSION_RATE}
**Target User:** {USER_PERSONA}
**Target Buyer (if different):** {BUYER_PERSONA}

### 1. PLG Readiness Assessment
- Is your product suited for PLG? (checklist of requirements)
- What needs to change to enable self-serve adoption?
- Bottleneck analysis: where does self-serve break down?

### 2. Freemium/Trial Strategy
- What to give away free (generous enough to demonstrate value)
- What to gate behind the paywall (compelling enough to upgrade)
- Free vs. trial vs. freemium: which model fits best and why
- Trial length recommendation (7, 14, or 30 days) with reasoning

### 3. Self-Serve Conversion Engine
- In-app upgrade prompts: when, where, and how to nudge (without annoying)
- Usage-based triggers: "You've hit 80% of your free limit"
- Feature discovery: how to show users what they're missing on the free plan
- Pricing page optimization for self-serve conversion

### 4. Product Qualified Leads (PQLs)
- Define what a PQL looks like for your product
- Behavioral criteria: what actions indicate purchase readiness?
- When to hand off from product-led to sales-assisted
- How to operationalize PQL scoring

### 5. Expansion Revenue
- Strategies for seat expansion (getting more users within an account)
- Usage-based upselling opportunities
- Feature upselling: how to introduce premium features organically
- Timing: when to suggest expansion (after value milestones, not arbitrarily)

## Variables
- {PRODUCT} – Your product
- {CURRENT_MODEL} – How you currently sell (e.g., "sales-led", "marketing-led", "hybrid")
- {FREE_OFFERING} – Current free tier or trial details
- {CONVERSION_RATE} – Free-to-paid conversion rate
- {USER_PERSONA} – The person who uses the product daily
- {BUYER_PERSONA} – The person who approves the purchase (may be different from user)

## Notes
- PLG doesn't mean "no sales team." The best PLG companies have sales teams that focus on enterprise deals and expansion — not initial acquisition.
- The key principle: let users experience value BEFORE asking for money. If your free tier doesn't wow people, no amount of sales effort will save you.
- PLG works best when the user and the buyer are the same person, or when the user can champion the purchase internally.
