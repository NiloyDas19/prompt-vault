## Purpose
Generate prioritized A/B test ideas for landing pages that can measurably increase conversion rates.

## Prompt
Generate 15 high-impact A/B test ideas for my landing page. Prioritize by expected impact.

**Landing Page URL:** {LANDING_PAGE_URL}
**Page Goal:** {PAGE_GOAL}
**Current Conversion Rate:** {CURRENT_CR}
**Traffic Volume:** {MONTHLY_TRAFFIC}
**Target Audience:** {AUDIENCE}
**Current Page Description:** {PAGE_DESCRIPTION}

For each test idea, provide:

| # | Element | Control | Variant | Hypothesis | Expected Impact | Effort |
|---|---------|---------|---------|-----------|-----------------|--------|

### High-Priority Tests (test first):
1. **Headline** — Test benefit-driven vs. curiosity-driven
2. **CTA Button** — Text, color, size, and placement
3. **Social Proof** — Adding/changing testimonials, logos, or numbers
4. **Hero Image/Video** — Product screenshot vs. lifestyle image vs. video
5. **Form Length** — Reduce fields vs. add qualifying fields

### Medium-Priority Tests:
6. **Page Length** — Long-form vs. short-form
7. **Pricing Display** — Monthly vs. annual, with vs. without competitor comparison
8. **Trust Signals** — Guarantee badges, security logos, review stars
9. **Headline + Subheadline Combination** — Different value prop angles
10. **Mobile Layout** — Mobile-specific optimizations

### Advanced Tests:
11. **Personalization** — Different content for different traffic sources
12. **Exit Intent** — Popup vs. no popup, different offers
13. **Live Chat** — Widget vs. no widget, proactive vs. reactive
14. **Video** — Auto-play demo vs. testimonial vs. no video
15. **Urgency Elements** — Countdown timers, stock indicators, limited-time badges

### Testing Methodology:
- Minimum sample size calculator (based on current traffic and conversion rate)
- How long each test needs to run for statistical significance
- How to avoid common A/B testing mistakes (peeking, stopping too early, testing too many things)

## Variables
- {LANDING_PAGE_URL} – Your landing page URL
- {PAGE_GOAL} – What the page should achieve (e.g., "sign up for free trial", "book a demo", "purchase")
- {CURRENT_CR} – Current conversion rate
- {MONTHLY_TRAFFIC} – Monthly visitors to this page
- {AUDIENCE} – Who visits the page
- {PAGE_DESCRIPTION} – Brief description of the current page layout and content

## Notes
- Test the biggest swings first: headline, CTA, and social proof changes will have more impact than button color changes.
- With less than 10,000 monthly visitors, you need to test radical changes to see statistical significance. Subtle tweaks require massive traffic.
- Always let tests run for at least 2 full business cycles (2 weeks minimum) to account for day-of-week effects.
