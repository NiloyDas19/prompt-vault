## Purpose
Design or optimize a user onboarding flow that gets new users to their "aha moment" as fast as possible, reducing churn and increasing activation rates.

## Prompt
Help me design (or improve) my user onboarding flow. The goal is to get new users to their first "aha moment" as quickly as possible.

**Product:** {PRODUCT}
**Current Onboarding (describe):** {CURRENT_ONBOARDING}
**Activation Metric:** {ACTIVATION_METRIC}
**Current Activation Rate:** {ACTIVATION_RATE}
**Average Time to Activate:** {TIME_TO_ACTIVATE}
**Biggest Drop-Off Point:** {DROP_OFF_POINT}
**User Persona:** {PERSONA}

### 1. Aha Moment Definition
- What is the single action that correlates most strongly with long-term retention?
- How quickly should users reach it? (target time)
- What percentage of new users currently reach it?

### 2. Onboarding Flow Redesign
Design a step-by-step flow:
- **Step 1:** Welcome screen — what to show, what NOT to show
- **Step 2–N:** Each subsequent step toward the aha moment
- For each step: what the user does, what the UI shows, what copy to use, what to skip/defer
- **Completed state:** How to celebrate when they reach the aha moment

### 3. Friction Audit
- What steps in the current flow are unnecessary before the aha moment?
- What information are we asking for too early?
- Where can we use progressive profiling (ask later) instead of upfront forms?
- What can be skipped or defaulted?

### 4. Engagement Triggers
- Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 email/notification sequence for users who stall
- In-app prompts and tooltips for key features
- Re-engagement for users who signed up but never activated

### 5. Onboarding Checklist
Design an in-app onboarding checklist:
- 5–7 steps from signup to activation
- Progress indicator (progress bar, checkmarks)
- Reward or celebration upon completion
- Copy for each checklist item (action-oriented, not feature-oriented)

### 6. Measurement Plan
- KPIs to track: signup-to-activation rate, time-to-activation, onboarding completion rate
- Cohort analysis recommendations
- A/B tests to run on the onboarding flow

## Variables
- {PRODUCT} – Your product
- {CURRENT_ONBOARDING} – Describe your current onboarding (or "none — users are dropped into the app")
- {ACTIVATION_METRIC} – The action that defines "activated" (e.g., "created first project", "sent first invoice", "invited a team member")
- {ACTIVATION_RATE} – Current % of signups who activate
- {TIME_TO_ACTIVATE} – How long it currently takes
- {DROP_OFF_POINT} – Where most users abandon the onboarding
- {PERSONA} – Who your typical new user is

## Notes
- Every additional step in onboarding loses 20–30% of users. Be ruthless about cutting unnecessary steps.
- The best onboarding doesn't feel like onboarding — it feels like using the product. Get users DOING things immediately.
- Defer account setup (profile photo, company details, preferences) until AFTER the aha moment. Nobody cares about their profile picture when they haven't seen your product's value yet.
