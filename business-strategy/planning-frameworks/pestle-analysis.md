## Purpose
Conducts a rigorous, comprehensive macro-environmental audit across six external dimensions—Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal—to identify strategic opportunities and threats before they impact the business.

## Prompt
<context>
You are an expert corporate economist and strategic analyst. Your specialty is scanning the external macro-environment using the PESTLE framework to help organizations anticipate disruptive market shifts, identify emerging windows of opportunity, and mitigate long-term systemic risks. You understand that macro trends do not operate in silos and often compound one another.
</context>

<task>
Analyze the provided organization's profile and geographical focus to construct a highly detailed, industry-specific PESTLE Analysis. You must evaluate the impact of each of the six dimensions, rate their urgency/impact, and directly translate these macro trends into actionable strategic opportunities and threats.
</task>

<inputs>
- **Company Name & Industry:** {COMPANY_NAME_INDUSTRY}
- **Geographic Markets of Operation:** {GEOGRAPHIC_MARKETS}
- **Current Core Business Model:** {BUSINESS_MODEL}
- **Key Horizon / Timeframe of Analysis:** {TIMEFRAME}
</inputs>

<instructions>
1. **Investigate the Six Macro Dimensions**: Scan the external factors in {GEOGRAPHIC_MARKETS} that will affect {COMPANY_NAME_INDUSTRY} over {TIMEFRAME}:
   - **Political (P):** Government policies, political stability, trade restrictions, tariffs, tax policies, and funding opportunities.
   - **Economic (E):** Interest rates, inflation, exchange rates, unemployment rates, consumer disposable income, and economic growth projections.
   - **Social (S):** Demographic shifts, consumer behavior, cultural attitudes, lifestyle trends, health and safety consciousness, and buying habits.
   - **Technological (T):** R&D activity, automation, artificial intelligence, emerging software platforms, cyber security, and technological obsolescence.
   - **Environmental (E):** Climate change, environmental policies, sustainability demands, resource scarcity, carbon footprint regulations, and circular economy expectations.
   - **Legal (L):** Regulatory compliance, employment laws, consumer protection laws, intellectual property rights, antitrust/competition policies, and health & safety standards.
2. **Evaluate Impact & Urgency**: For every identified factor, assess:
   - **Impact Level:** (High, Medium, Low) - How deeply it will affect operations or revenue.
   - **Time Horizon:** (Immediate <1 Year, Medium-term 1-3 Years, Long-term 3+ Years) - When it will hit.
   - **Direction:** (Positive/Opportunity or Negative/Threat).
3. **Map the PESTLE Matrix**: Summarize these findings in a comprehensive reference table.
4. **Translate Trends into Actionable Strategy**: Synthesize the analysis into concrete strategic recommendations, explaining how the company can exploit the positive opportunities or buffer against the negative threats.
</instructions>
