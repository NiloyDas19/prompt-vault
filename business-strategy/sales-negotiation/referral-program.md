## Purpose
Designs a structured customer referral and advocate marketing program to leverage happy customers as active lead generation assets.

## Prompt
```markdown
You are an expert growth marketer and customer advocacy strategist. Your goal is to design a high-converting B2B Referral & Advocate Marketing Program for {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE}. 

Happy customers are the most credible source of new business. You will build a system that identifies these key advocates, incentivizes them ethically, structures a friction-free referral engine, and automates outreach to turn client satisfaction into a predictable organic pipeline.

<context>
- Our Product or Service: {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE}
- Our Target Advocate Profile (e.g., power users, executives, system administrators): {HAPPY_CUSTOMER_PROFILE}
- Referral Rewards & Incentives (for both the advocate and the referred prospect): {ADVOCATE_INCENTIVES}
- Referral Submission Mechanism (e.g., custom link, form, intro email): {REFERRAL_MECHANISM}
- Primary Program Metrics to Track: {KPI_TRACKING_METRICS}
</context>

<instructions>
Develop the complete Advocate Marketing & Referral Program by executing these structural parts:

1. **Advocate Segmentation & Identification Plan**:
   - Outline a clear system to automatically flag and segment potential advocates using NPS data, usage milestones, customer success reviews, and public positive mentions (e.g., G2, LinkedIn).
   - Define the profile of the "Perfect Advocate" based on {HAPPY_CUSTOMER_PROFILE}.

2. **Incentive & Motivation Matrix**:
   - Detail the incentive structure using the variables in {ADVOCATE_INCENTIVES}.
   - Address both intrinsic motivations (career advancement, professional status, community access) and extrinsic motivations (gift cards, service discounts, charity donations) to ensure high participation without compromising professional ethics.

3. **Frictionless Referral Flow & Mechanism**:
   - Design the exact workflow a customer takes to make a referral using {REFERRAL_MECHANISM}.
   - Identify and eliminate potential friction points (e.g., login requirements, complex forms) to make referring as simple as three clicks.

4. **Multi-Channel Advocate Engagement Scripts**:
   - Write highly engaging, non-spammy email and in-app templates to recruit advocates into the program.
   - Write the exact "Intro/Hand-off Email Template" that advocates can copy and paste to introduce {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE} to their peers.

5. **Operational Execution & Tracking Blueprint**:
   - Detail how the marketing and sales teams will track performance using {KPI_TRACKING_METRICS}.
   - Outline the post-referral sales workflow: how referred leads are routed, marked as "referred sources," and prioritized for rapid outreach.
</instructions>

<style_and_tone>
- Tone: Highly engaging, motivating, professional, warm, and structured.
- Pacing: Encouraging and easy to follow, making the program design feel exciting and highly achievable.
- Formatting: Use tables, clear bulleted workflows, and beautifully formatted, copy-pasteable email templates.
</style_and_tone>

## 4. High-Converting Recruitment & Intro Templates

### Template A: CSM Personal Outreach to Happy Customer (Recruitment)
```email
Subject: A quick favor / Supporting peers in your network
Body:
Hi [Customer Name],

It's been fantastic watching your team achieve [Specific Goal/Outcome] with {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE} over the last few months. 

Because you've seen such strong results with [Specific Feature], I wanted to see if you have peers in other organizations who are currently struggling with [Pain Point]. 

We recently launched an advocate program to make it easy to share our resources. If you introduce us to a peer who could benefit from this, we'll thank you both with **[Advocate Reward]** and give them **[Prospect Incentive]** to make the evaluation easy for them.

Would you be open to introducing us to 1 or 2 people in your network who are navigating this right now?

Best,
[CSM/Account Manager Name]
```

### Template B: Copy-Paste Email for the Advocate to Send to their Peer
```email
Subject: Intro to [My Name] at {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE} (re: [Pain Point])
Body:
Hi [Peer Name],

I wanted to introduce you to [My Name], who runs partnership teams at {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE}. 

Over at our company, we were struggling with [Pain Point] and we've been using their solution to [Insert key positive outcome/metric]. 

I know you've been working on solving [Peer's current project/pain] at [Peer's Company], and I thought their approach would be highly relevant for you. 

I've cc'd [My Name] here. They've agreed to waive [Setup fee / provide a specific trial incentive] for anyone in my network if you want to take a quick look. I'll let you two take it from here!

Best,
[Advocate Name]
```

## Variables
- {PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE} – The name of the product or service you are building the program for.
- {HAPPY_CUSTOMER_PROFILE} – The exact job titles and professional characteristics of your most active and satisfied customers.
- {ADVOCATE_INCENTIVES} – The specific dual-sided rewards or incentives you will offer.
- {REFERRAL_MECHANISM} – The technical or manual process for submitting and tracking referrals.
- {KPI_TRACKING_METRICS} – The core data points and goals you want to monitor.

## Notes
- **Ethics first in B2B**: In many enterprise environments, employees cannot accept personal gifts due to compliance rules. Always offer a "Charity Donation" option (e.g., donating the reward value to a non-profit of their choice) to ensure compliance.
- **Timing is everything**: Ask for referrals immediately following a "moment of delight"—such as a highly successful quarterly review, a solved support ticket, or immediately after a contract renewal.
- **Under-promise, over-deliver**: Deliver rewards quickly. A delayed reward ruins the advocate experience and prevents subsequent referrals.
- **Model Recommendation**: Best used with any advanced LLM (e.g., GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet) to write highly customized and natural outreach templates based on your target demographic.
