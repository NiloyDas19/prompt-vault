## Purpose
Generate tactical, armored space marines or futuristic soldiers for sci-fi universes.

## Prompt
Sci-fi space marine soldier concept art, {GENDER} wearing {ARMOR_TYPE} combat armor, {ARMOR_DETAILS} painted in {ARMOR_COLORS}, equipped with {WEAPONRY}, {HELMET_STATUS}, {POSE_AND_ACTION}, {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT}, {BATTLE_DAMAGE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE}, Halo / Mass Effect aesthetic, highly detailed mechanical design

## Variables
- {GENDER} – Gender
- {ARMOR_TYPE} – Armor (e.g., "heavy mechanized power armor", "lightweight tactical stealth suit")
- {ARMOR_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "carbon fiber plating and glowing energy shields", "ammo pouches and survival gear")
- {ARMOR_COLORS} – Paint (e.g., "matte olive drab", "stark white and orange", "urban digital camo")
- {WEAPONRY} – Weapons (e.g., "a heavy plasma rifle", "dual energy pistols", "a glowing energy blade")
- {HELMET_STATUS} – Helmet (e.g., "wearing a full enclosed helmet with a gold visor", "helmet removed holding it under their arm")
- {POSE_AND_ACTION} – Action (e.g., "aiming down sights", "running through explosions", "standing at attention")
- {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} – Setting (e.g., "a dusty alien battlefield", "the metallic corridors of a starship")
- {BATTLE_DAMAGE} – Condition (e.g., "pristine and clean", "heavy battle damage, scorched and scratched")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "harsh alien sun", "strobe lights and laser fire")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "sci-fi soldier concept art, space marine" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (fantasy, swords, magic, casual clothes, contemporary military)

## Notes
- Adding "callsigns and decals on armor" helps make the armor look grounded and realistic.
