## Purpose
Generate adorable, expressive 3D character renders resembling modern animated feature films.

## Prompt
3D character render of a {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION}, {EXPRESSION} expression with large expressive eyes, wearing {OUTFIT}, {ACTION_OR_POSE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} studio lighting, {BACKGROUND_STYLE}, Pixar and Disney 3D animation style, Unreal Engine 5, Octane Render, highly detailed textures, cute and charming

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION} – Who (e.g., "cute fluffy orange monster", "brave little girl with freckles", "grumpy old wizard")
- {EXPRESSION} – Face (e.g., "wide-eyed wonder", "mischievous grin", "confused squint")
- {OUTFIT} – Clothes (e.g., "tiny yellow raincoat", "oversized wizard robes", "miniature spacesuit")
- {ACTION_OR_POSE} – Action (e.g., "holding a glowing firefly", "sneaking on tiptoes", "jumping for joy")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft warm cinematic", "bright colorful")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "soft blurred magical forest", "clean pastel studio background")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "3D animated movie character, Pixar style" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (2d, drawing, painting, realistic human, scary, creepy)

## Notes
- "Large expressive eyes" and "soft cinematic lighting" are the keys to nailing the modern 3D animation studio look.
