## Purpose
Generate intimidating, powerful antagonist or "final boss" characters for games, stories, or tabletop campaigns.

## Prompt
Intimidating villain character concept art of a {VILLAIN_TYPE}, {PHYSICAL_APPEARANCE}, wearing {SINISTER_ATTIRE}, {EVIL_MAGIC_OR_WEAPON}, {POSE_AND_EXPRESSION}, {VILLAIN_LAIR_BACKGROUND}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} dramatic underlighting, {COLOR_THEME} color theme, dark fantasy aesthetic, ominous and powerful, highly detailed boss design

## Variables
- {VILLAIN_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "dark lord", "corrupted king", "demonic overlord", "vampire matriarch")
- {PHYSICAL_APPEARANCE} – Look (e.g., "towering figure with pale skin and glowing red eyes", "faceless behind an iron mask")
- {SINISTER_ATTIRE} – Clothes (e.g., "jagged black obsidian armor with a tattered cape", "elegant bloody crimson velvet robes")
- {EVIL_MAGIC_OR_WEAPON} – Power/Weapon (e.g., "wielding a massive executioner's sword", "dark shadow magic swirling around their hands")
- {POSE_AND_EXPRESSION} – Action (e.g., "looking down arrogantly from a throne", "slowly drawing a weapon", "a cruel sneer")
- {VILLAIN_LAIR_BACKGROUND} – Setting (e.g., "a ruined throne room", "a fiery hellscape", "a dark gothic cathedral")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "harsh dramatic underlighting", "blood-red rim lighting")
- {COLOR_THEME} – Colors (e.g., "monochrome black and red", "sickly green and purple")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 350`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "dark villain character concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (cute, hero, bright, happy, smiling, pastel)

## Notes
- "Underlighting" (lighting from below) is a classic cinematic trick to make characters look sinister and menacing.
