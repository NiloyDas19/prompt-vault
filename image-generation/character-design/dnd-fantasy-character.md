## Purpose
Generate high-quality concept art of fantasy roleplaying characters (like Dungeons & Dragons), perfect for character sheets or campaign visuals.

## Prompt
Fantasy character concept art of a {RACE} {CLASS}, {GENDER}, {PHYSICAL_FEATURES}, wearing {ARMOR_AND_CLOTHING}, wielding {WEAPONS_AND_PROPS}, {ACTION_OR_POSE}, {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT}, {LIGHTING_STYLE}, detailed digital painting, D&D character art, RPG character sheet aesthetic, Greg Rutkowski style

## Variables
- {RACE} – Species (e.g., "Tiefling", "Half-Orc", "High Elf", "Dwarven")
- {CLASS} – Role (e.g., "Paladin", "Rogue", "Necromancer", "Bard")
- {GENDER} – Gender presentation (e.g., "male", "female", "androgynous")
- {PHYSICAL_FEATURES} – Features (e.g., "glowing purple eyes and curved horns", "scarred face with a braided beard")
- {ARMOR_AND_CLOTHING} – Clothes (e.g., "heavy plate armor with gold filigree", "dark leather rogue gear and a hooded cloak")
- {WEAPONS_AND_PROPS} – Props (e.g., "a massive glowing battleaxe", "a spellbook and a glowing arcane orb")
- {ACTION_OR_POSE} – Pose (e.g., "casting a spell with one hand", "standing heroically resting on their sword")
- {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} – Setting (e.g., "a dimly lit tavern", "a mystical forest", "neutral grey studio background")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dramatic rim lighting from magic", "natural dappled sunlight")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "fantasy character concept art, digital painting" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (sci-fi, modern, photography, guns, cars, ugly, bad anatomy)

## Notes
- "Neutral grey studio background" is best if you want to cut the character out to place on a character sheet.
