## Purpose
Generate dynamic, muscular superhero or supervillain character designs in a modern comic book art style.

## Prompt
Comic book character design of a {HERO_OR_VILLAIN_TYPE}, wearing a {COSTUME_DESIGN} superhero suit, {SUPERPOWER_EFFECT} manifesting from their hands, {DYNAMIC_POSE}, {FACIAL_FEATURES_OR_MASK}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} dramatic lighting, {BACKGROUND}, modern Marvel or DC comic book illustration style, Jim Lee / Alex Ross influence, highly detailed ink and color, heroic proportions

## Variables
- {HERO_OR_VILLAIN_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "speedster superhero", "cosmic entity supervillain", "street-level vigilante")
- {COSTUME_DESIGN} – Suit (e.g., "sleek aerodynamic red and gold armored", "dark tactical stealth", "spandex with a glowing emblem")
- {SUPERPOWER_EFFECT} – Power (e.g., "crackling blue lightning", "swirling dark magic", "glowing fire")
- {DYNAMIC_POSE} – Action (e.g., "landing in a three-point superhero landing", "flying towards the camera", "standing heroically on a gargoyle")
- {FACIAL_FEATURES_OR_MASK} – Face (e.g., "a full tactical helmet", "a domino mask and determined jaw", "glowing solid white eyes")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "high contrast chiaroscuro", "city neon lighting reflecting on armor")
- {BACKGROUND} – Setting (e.g., "rainy Gotham-style cityscape", "deep space", "shattered concrete")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "comic book illustration, superhero character" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (photorealistic, 3d, anime, manga, boring pose, civilian clothes)

## Notes
- Mentioning "heroic proportions" helps ensure the exaggerated muscular/athletic build typical of comic art.
