## Purpose
Generate gritty, high-tech cyberpunk character designs featuring cybernetic enhancements and streetwear.

## Prompt
Cyberpunk character concept art of a {CHARACTER_ROLE}, {PHYSICAL_FEATURES} with visible {CYBERNETICS}, wearing {CYBERPUNK_FASHION}, holding {TECH_WEAPON_OR_PROP}, {POSE_EXPRESSION}, {BACKGROUND_CITY}, {NEON_LIGHTING} neon lighting reflecting on their gear, highly detailed digital painting, Blade Runner / Cyberpunk 2077 aesthetic

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_ROLE} – Role (e.g., "netrunner hacker", "corporate mercenary", "street samurai")
- {PHYSICAL_FEATURES} – Look (e.g., "shaved head with glowing tattoos", "half-shaved asymmetrical hair")
- {CYBERNETICS} – Chrome (e.g., "chrome synthetic arms and glowing optical implants", "a metallic jaw replacement")
- {CYBERPUNK_FASHION} – Clothes (e.g., "an oversized glowing collar jacket and techwear pants", "a transparent vinyl trench coat")
- {TECH_WEAPON_OR_PROP} – Prop (e.g., "a glowing katana", "a holographic hacking deck", "a heavy smart-pistol")
- {POSE_EXPRESSION} – Pose (e.g., "leaning against a wall smoking", "plugging a cable into their neck")
- {BACKGROUND_CITY} – Where (e.g., "a rainy neon-lit alleyway", "a cluttered hacker den")
- {NEON_LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "cyan and magenta", "sickly yellow and green")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "cyberpunk character concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (fantasy, medieval, steampunk, daylight, natural, clean)

## Notes
- To make it more "high-society" cyberpunk, use terms like "corporate elite, sleek white chrome, sterile, pristine."
