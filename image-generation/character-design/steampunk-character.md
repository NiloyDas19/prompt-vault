## Purpose
Generate intricate, Victorian-inspired retro-futuristic steampunk character concepts.

## Prompt
Steampunk character concept art of a {CHARACTER_ROLE}, wearing {CLOTHING_STYLE} with {BRASS_LEATHER_DETAILS}, equipped with a {STEAMPUNK_GADGET}, {POSE_EXPRESSION}, {BACKGROUND_SETTING}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting, highly detailed character design, Victorian retro-futurism aesthetic, rich textures of brass and leather

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_ROLE} – Role (e.g., "mad inventor", "airship pirate captain", "clockwork assassin")
- {CLOTHING_STYLE} – Clothes (e.g., "a tailored Victorian tailcoat and top hat", "a leather corset and ruffled skirt")
- {BRASS_LEATHER_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "brass goggles, gear-shaped buttons, and heavy leather boots")
- {STEAMPUNK_GADGET} – Prop (e.g., "a complex brass mechanical arm", "a steam-powered rifle", "a glowing aether-lantern")
- {POSE_EXPRESSION} – Pose (e.g., "smirking confidently", "tinkering with a pocket watch")
- {BACKGROUND_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a smoggy industrial London street", "the deck of a flying zeppelin")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "warm gaslight illumination", "smoky diffused sunlight")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 350`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "steampunk character concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (modern, digital tech, neon, cyber, fantasy magic)

## Notes
- Emphasize materials like "burnished brass, polished mahogany, and worn leather" for the best texture generation.
