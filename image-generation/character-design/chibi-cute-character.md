## Purpose
Generate ultra-cute, big-headed "chibi" characters perfect for stickers, emojis, or casual game avatars.

## Prompt
Cute chibi style character of a {SUBJECT}, {CHIBI_PROPORTIONS}, {EXPRESSION} expression, wearing {CLOTHING}, doing {ACTION_OR_POSE}, {BACKGROUND}, kawaii aesthetic, flat vector illustration, pastel colors, thick clean outlines, perfect for a sticker design

## Variables
- {SUBJECT} – Who (e.g., "little witch", "ninja frog", "grumpy cat in a spacesuit")
- {CHIBI_PROPORTIONS} – Proportions (e.g., "massive head and tiny body", "round and squishy")
- {EXPRESSION} – Face (e.g., "sparkling eyes and a huge smile", "pouting with puffed cheeks", "sleeping peacefully")
- {CLOTHING} – Clothes (e.g., "an oversized hoodie", "a tiny knight's armor")
- {ACTION_OR_POSE} – Action (e.g., "holding a giant strawberry", "waving happily", "hugging a plushie")
- {BACKGROUND} – Background (e.g., "isolated on a solid white background", "surrounded by floating sparkles and stars")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --niji 6` (Niji is strongly recommended for chibi)
**DALL-E 3:** Add "cute kawaii chibi character, vector sticker" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (realistic, scary, detailed, 3d render, tall proportions)

## Notes
- To make it look like a physical sticker, add: "white die-cut border around the character, subtle drop shadow."
