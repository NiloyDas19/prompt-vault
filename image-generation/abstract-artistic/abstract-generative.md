## Purpose
Generate visually stunning abstract artwork driven by mathematical patterns, color theory, and generative principles.

## Prompt
Abstract generative digital artwork featuring {GEOMETRIC_FORMS} geometric or organic forms in {ARRANGEMENT_PATTERN} arrangement, {COLOR_SYSTEM} color system using {SPECIFIC_COLORS} that {COLOR_BEHAVIOR} across the composition, {TEXTURE_QUALITY} texture quality with {SURFACE_DETAIL} surface detail, {DEPTH_ILLUSION} depth illusion created through {DEPTH_TECHNIQUE}, {MOVEMENT_FEEL} sense of movement or energy, {MATHEMATICAL_INFLUENCE} mathematical or natural influence (fractals, Fibonacci, flow fields), {ARTISTIC_STYLE} artistic style, {OUTPUT_FEEL} overall feeling, fine art print quality, {ARTIST_REFERENCE} computational art influence

## Variables
- {GEOMETRIC_FORMS} – Shapes (e.g., "interlocking circles and arcs", "flowing organic blob forms", "sharp angular crystalline")
- {ARRANGEMENT_PATTERN} – How arranged (e.g., "radially symmetrical", "chaotic organic", "grid-based systematic")
- {COLOR_SYSTEM} – Color approach (e.g., "analogous harmony", "complementary tension", "monochromatic gradient")
- {SPECIFIC_COLORS} – Colors (e.g., "deep navy to electric cyan", "warm coral to gold", "black, white, and a single accent red")
- {COLOR_BEHAVIOR} – How colors work (e.g., "shift subtly through hue rotation", "blend through gradient transition", "contrast sharply")
- {TEXTURE_QUALITY} – Texture (e.g., "smooth and polished", "grained and tactile", "layered transparent")
- {SURFACE_DETAIL} – Surface (e.g., "visible brush-like strokes", "perfect mathematical precision", "noise grain")
- {DEPTH_ILLUSION} – 3D feel (e.g., "strong 3D depth", "flat 2D graphic", "subtle layered depth")
- {DEPTH_TECHNIQUE} – How depth works (e.g., "scale variation", "overlapping translucent layers", "shadow and light")
- {MOVEMENT_FEEL} – Motion (e.g., "frozen in motion like a slow shutter", "static and crystallized", "flowing and liquid")
- {MATHEMATICAL_INFLUENCE} – Math (e.g., "Voronoi diagram", "Mandelbrot fractal", "Perlin noise flow field")
- {ARTISTIC_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "minimalist", "maximalist complex", "organic natural", "mechanical precise")
- {OUTPUT_FEEL} – Impression (e.g., "meditative and calm", "explosive energy", "mysterious depth")
- {ARTIST_REFERENCE} – Reference (e.g., "Vera Molnar", "Casey Reas", "Tyler Hobbs Fidenza")
