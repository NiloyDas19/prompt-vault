## Purpose
Generate mind-bending optical illusions, impossible geometry, and striking Op Art.

## Prompt
Abstract geometric Op Art featuring {ILLUSION_TYPE}, {SHAPE_PATTERNS} arranged in a {COMPOSITION_STYLE} composition, {COLOR_CONTRAST} creating a sense of {VISUAL_EFFECT}, mathematically precise, sharp vector illustration style, M.C. Escher / Victor Vasarely influence, trippy optical illusion, high contrast

## Variables
- {ILLUSION_TYPE} – Illusion (e.g., "impossible Penrose stairs", "an infinite tunnel", "warping 3D spheres on a flat plane")
- {SHAPE_PATTERNS} – Shapes (e.g., "concentric repeating squares", "interlocking impossible triangles", "distorted checkerboard")
- {COMPOSITION_STYLE} – Layout (e.g., "perfectly symmetrical", "spiraling into the center", "tessellated seamless")
- {COLOR_CONTRAST} – Colors (e.g., "stark black and white", "vibrant complementary colors: orange and cyan")
- {VISUAL_EFFECT} – Effect (e.g., "false motion and vibration", "infinite depth", "impossible perspective")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "optical illusion, geometric Op Art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (organic, messy, paint strokes, soft, blurry, uneven lines)

## Notes
- Black and white creates the strongest vibrating optical illusions.
