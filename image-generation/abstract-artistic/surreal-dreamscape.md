## Purpose
Generate dreamlike, impossible scenes inspired by surrealism and the subconscious mind.

## Prompt
Surrealist painting of {IMPOSSIBLE_SCENE}, featuring {JUXTAPOSED_ELEMENTS}, {SCALE_DISTORTION} scale distortion, located in a {DREAM_LANDSCAPE}, {LIGHTING_ATMOSPHERE}, painted in the style of {SURREALIST_ARTIST}, symbolic and psychological, melting reality, highly detailed fine art

## Variables
- {IMPOSSIBLE_SCENE} – Scene (e.g., "a sea made of clouds where ships sail on the sky", "a doorway opening into outer space")
- {JUXTAPOSED_ELEMENTS} – Clash (e.g., "a giant eyeball resting in a teacup", "clockwork gears turning inside a sliced apple")
- {SCALE_DISTORTION} – Scale (e.g., "tiny elephants walking on long stilt legs", "a massive moon resting on the ground")
- {DREAM_LANDSCAPE} – Setting (e.g., "a barren infinite desert", "a room with walls made of water", "a checkerboard plain")
- {LIGHTING_ATMOSPHERE} – Light (e.g., "harsh midday sun creating long dark shadows", "eerie twilight blue")
- {SURREALIST_ARTIST} – Reference (e.g., "Salvador Dalí", "René Magritte", "Zdzisław Beksiński", "Leonora Carrington")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 500 --weird 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "surrealist fine art painting, dreamlike" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (realistic, mundane, normal scale, photography, logical)

## Notes
- The "weird" parameter in Midjourney is especially effective for surrealism.
