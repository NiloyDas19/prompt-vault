## Purpose
Generate infinitely complex, mathematically inspired fractal art and Mandelbrot sets.

## Prompt
3D mathematical fractal art, {FRACTAL_TYPE} formula, infinitely complex repeating {GEOMETRIC_SHAPES} shapes, rendered in {MATERIAL_STYLE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting casting intricate shadows within the structure, {COLOR_THEME} color theme, deep zoom perspective into the fractal, hyper-detailed, Mandelbulb 3D aesthetic, cosmic and mind-bending

## Variables
- {FRACTAL_TYPE} – Fractal (e.g., "Mandelbulb", "Julia set", "Sierpinski triangle", "abstract geometric")
- {GEOMETRIC_SHAPES} – Shapes (e.g., "spiraling organic tendrils", "sharp crystalline spires", "interlocking gears")
- {MATERIAL_STYLE} – Material (e.g., "translucent glowing glass", "iridescent bismuth crystal", "matte black rubber and gold")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dramatic volumetric lighting", "glowing from within", "soft ambient occlusion")
- {COLOR_THEME} – Colors (e.g., "neon psychedelic rainbow", "monochrome silver", "fire and ice (orange and cyan)")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 800`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "3D fractal art, infinitely complex" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (blurry, simple, plain, low detail, human, animal)

## Notes
- Combining fractals with materials (like "fractal made of carved wood") yields incredible surreal results.
