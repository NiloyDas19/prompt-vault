## Purpose
Generate beautiful, poetic double exposure images blending a portrait silhouette with a landscape or concept.

## Prompt
Artistic double exposure photography, a silhouette profile of {PRIMARY_SUBJECT} seamlessly blended with {SECONDARY_SUBJECT}, {BLEND_DETAILS} visible within the silhouette, set against a {BACKGROUND_COLOR} background, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, {ATMOSPHERE}, poetic and surreal, True Detective intro aesthetic, masterful in-camera multiple exposure effect

## Variables
- {PRIMARY_SUBJECT} – Silhouette shape (e.g., "a young woman's side profile", "a howling wolf", "a man in a suit")
- {SECONDARY_SUBJECT} – Internal image (e.g., "a dense foggy pine forest", "a bustling New York cityscape", "crashing ocean waves")
- {BLEND_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "tree branches extending out from the hair", "buildings growing out of the shoulders")
- {BACKGROUND_COLOR} – Background (e.g., "pure white negative space", "soft pale grey", "stark black")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "monochrome black and white", "muted earthy tones", "vibrant synthwave colors")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "melancholy and introspective", "wild and natural", "urban and chaotic")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "double exposure photography, silhouette blend" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Use double-exposure LoRA. Negative: (messy, confusing, separated images, full color background)

## Notes
- The starker the background (pure white or pure black), the better the double exposure effect usually looks.
