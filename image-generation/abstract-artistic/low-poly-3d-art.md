## Purpose
Generate charming, minimalist low-poly 3D artwork popular in indie games and modern illustration.

## Prompt
Low poly 3D render of {SUBJECT}, constructed entirely of flat geometric triangular polygons, {ENVIRONMENT_SETTING}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} casting sharp distinct shadows on the flat facets, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, {ATMOSPHERE}, minimalist geometric design, Cinema4D / Blender aesthetic, isometric perspective, clean and crisp

## Variables
- {SUBJECT} – What (e.g., "a cute red fox sitting", "a classic sports car", "a towering mountain peak")
- {ENVIRONMENT_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "floating on a small island chunk", "in a low poly pine forest", "on a solid studio background")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "bright global illumination", "warm sunset directional light", "neon synthwave lighting")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "vibrant flat pastel colors", "monochrome white and grey", "rich jewel tones")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "calm and peaceful", "playful and cute", "stark and abstract")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "low poly 3D render, faceted geometric" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (smooth, high poly, realistic, curved, detailed, organic, textures)

## Notes
- "Isometric perspective" works incredibly well with low-poly floating islands or dioramas.
