## Purpose
Generate stunning long-exposure photography featuring artistic light trails and glowing shapes.

## Prompt
Long exposure light painting photography in a {LOCATION_SETTING}, {LIGHT_SHAPE} drawn in the air using {LIGHT_SOURCE_COLOR} light, {INTERACTION} interacting with the environment, {AMBIENT_LIGHT} ambient lighting, {ATMOSPHERE}, long shutter speed effect, perfectly sharp background with smooth glowing light trails, magical night photography

## Variables
- {LOCATION_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "dark forest at midnight", "abandoned warehouse", "empty city street")
- {LIGHT_SHAPE} – Shapes (e.g., "a glowing wireframe car", "spiraling geometric orbs", "abstract flowing ribbon shapes")
- {LIGHT_SOURCE_COLOR} – Colors (e.g., "bright neon blue and pink", "warm sparks of steel wool", "rainbow RGB")
- {INTERACTION} – Action (e.g., "illuminating the trees around it", "reflecting perfectly on a wet street", "silhouetting a dark figure")
- {AMBIENT_LIGHT} – Ambient (e.g., "dim starlight", "distant city glow", "pitch black")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "mysterious and magical", "high-energy urban", "eerie")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "long exposure light painting photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (daylight, short exposure, bright, sun, people moving)

## Notes
- Steel wool spinning creates amazing showers of orange sparks: "steel wool long exposure spinning sparks."
