## Purpose
Generate striking glitch art that uses digital corruption, data moshing, and error aesthetics as intentional artistic expression.

## Prompt
Glitch art digital artwork of {SUBJECT_MATTER}, {GLITCH_TYPE} glitch effects corrupting the image with {CORRUPTION_PATTERN} corruption patterns, {COLOR_SHIFT} chromatic aberration with {CHANNEL_SEPARATION} RGB channel separation in {OFFSET_DIRECTION} direction, {SCAN_LINE_TYPE} horizontal or vertical scan line distortions, {PIXEL_SORT} pixel sorting creating {SORT_DIRECTION} streaks of {SORTED_COLOR} color, {DATA_MOSHING} data moshing effects blending {BLEND_SUBJECT} into the scene, {INTACT_ELEMENT} remaining intact and recognizable contrasting with the corruption, {DIGITAL_TEXTURE} digital noise texture, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette dominated by {DOMINANT_HUE}, post-internet aesthetic, intentional digital destruction as art

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_MATTER} – What's being glitched (e.g., "a portrait face", "a landscape", "a corporate logo")
- {GLITCH_TYPE} – Glitch style (e.g., "JPEG compression artifact extreme", "VHS tape distortion", "CRT TV malfunction")
- {CORRUPTION_PATTERN} – Pattern (e.g., "horizontal block tearing", "scattered pixel displacement", "frozen frame artifacts")
- {COLOR_SHIFT} – Color glitch (e.g., "heavy chromatic aberration", "RGB split offset", "inverse color regions")
- {CHANNEL_SEPARATION} – Amount (e.g., "extreme 30px", "subtle 5px", "wild 100px")
- {OFFSET_DIRECTION} – Direction (e.g., "horizontal", "diagonal", "random directions")
- {SCAN_LINE_TYPE} – Lines (e.g., "thick horizontal", "thin multiple", "irregular broken")
- {PIXEL_SORT} – Sort effect (e.g., "heavy pixel sorting", "selective column sorting", "no pixel sort")
- {SORT_DIRECTION} – Sort direction (e.g., "vertical downward", "horizontal rightward", "diagonal")
- {SORTED_COLOR} – Color (e.g., "bright saturated", "dark shadow", "highlight white")
- {DATA_MOSHING} – Blend (e.g., "strong data moshing", "subtle bleed", "no data mosh")
- {BLEND_SUBJECT} – What bleeds in (e.g., "another image", "pure noise", "pattern")
- {INTACT_ELEMENT} – What stays clear (e.g., "the eyes", "the center of composition", "nothing, total chaos")
- {DIGITAL_TEXTURE} – Noise (e.g., "heavy noise grain", "compression blocking", "smooth with glitch only")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "electric cyan and magenta", "monochrome black and white", "vaporwave pastels")
- {DOMINANT_HUE} – Main color

## Modifiers
