## Purpose
Generate striking concept art of giant mechas, combat drones, or futuristic androids.

## Prompt
Sci-fi mecha concept art of a {MECHA_TYPE}, {ARMOR_STYLE} armor plating with {PAINT_SCHEME}, {WEAPONRY} integrated weapons, {GLOWING_ELEMENTS} emitting {GLOW_COLOR} light, {POSE_OR_ACTION}, standing in a {ENVIRONMENT}, {BATTLE_DAMAGE} wear and tear, {LIGHTING} dramatic lighting, highly detailed mechanical joints and hydraulics, Gundam / Titanfall aesthetic, 8k resolution 3D render

## Variables
- {MECHA_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "heavy artillery bipedal mech", "agile scout drone", "humanoid cybernetic android")
- {ARMOR_STYLE} – Armor (e.g., "angular stealth geometry", "curved organic plating", "blocky industrial")
- {PAINT_SCHEME} – Paint (e.g., "matte military green with hazard stripes", "pristine white and orange", "worn crimson")
- {WEAPONRY} – Weapons (e.g., "shoulder-mounted missile pods", "a massive plasma sword", "gatling arms")
- {GLOWING_ELEMENTS} – Lights (e.g., "optic sensors and reactor core", "energy vents")
- {GLOW_COLOR} – Color (e.g., "electric cyan", "menacing red")
- {POSE_OR_ACTION} – Action (e.g., "bracing to fire", "standing idle in a maintenance bay", "walking through a ruined city")
- {ENVIRONMENT} – Setting (e.g., "rainy cyberpunk street", "dusty Martian canyon", "bright hangar")
- {BATTLE_DAMAGE} – Condition (e.g., "heavy battle damage, scorch marks and exposed wiring", "factory new")
- {LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "cinematic rim lighting", "harsh spotlights")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "mecha robot concept art, highly detailed" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (human face, flesh, fantasy armor, organic, magical)
