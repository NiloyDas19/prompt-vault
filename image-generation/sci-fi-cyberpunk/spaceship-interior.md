## Purpose
Generate detailed, immersive interiors of sci-fi spacecraft, ranging from gritty industrial haulers to sleek alien vessels.

## Prompt
Sci-fi environment concept art of a {SHIP_ROOM}, {INTERIOR_STYLE} interior design, featuring {CONSOLES_TECH} illuminated by {SCREEN_GLOW}, {WINDOW_VIEW} visible through the {WINDOW_STYLE} windows, {LIGHTING_STYLE} ambient lighting, {CLUTTER_DETAILS} environmental storytelling details, highly detailed panels and bulkheads, cinematic composition, Star Wars / Star Citizen aesthetic

## Variables
- {SHIP_ROOM} – Room (e.g., "pilot's cockpit", "engineering reactor room", "crew mess hall", "cryo-sleep bay")
- {INTERIOR_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "grimy industrial utilitarian", "sleek Apple-like futuristic", "dark alien organic")
- {CONSOLES_TECH} – Tech (e.g., "complex arrays of glowing buttons and joysticks", "holographic touch interfaces")
- {SCREEN_GLOW} – Screens (e.g., "cool blue tactical readouts", "warning red flashing alerts")
- {WINDOW_VIEW} – Outside (e.g., "hyperspace tunnel streaks", "a massive blue planet", "the vastness of deep space")
- {WINDOW_STYLE} – Windows (e.g., "heavily armored slit", "massive geodesic dome", "wide panoramic glass")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dim emergency lighting", "bright sterile white", "moody ambient rim lights")
- {CLUTTER_DETAILS} – Clutter (e.g., "floating coffee cups and zero-g harnesses", "exposed wiring and duct tape repairs")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "spaceship interior concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (earth, outdoor, sky, natural light, modern airplane, contemporary)

## Notes
- Adjust the "clutter" to dictate the genre: clean for Star Trek style, dirty and broken for Star Wars / Expanse style.
