## Purpose
Generate a dense, atmospheric cyberpunk cityscape filled with neon lights, futuristic architecture, and gritty urban detail.

## Prompt
Cyberpunk cityscape of {CITY_SECTION}, towering {ARCHITECTURE_STYLE} megastructures reaching into a {SKY_CONDITION} sky, illuminated by {NEON_COLORS} neon signs reflecting on {WET_SURFACES} wet surfaces, {STREET_LEVEL_DETAIL} at street level, {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} heavy atmosphere, {FLYING_VEHICLES} in the air above, shot from {PERSPECTIVE}, hyper-detailed matte painting, cinematic lighting, {ARTIST_INFLUENCE} influence, Blade Runner aesthetic, dark synthwave mood, incredibly detailed urban decay meets high tech

## Variables
- {CITY_SECTION} – Area (e.g., "a multi-level street market", "a high-altitude corporate sector", "a dark alleyway in the slums")
- {ARCHITECTURE_STYLE} – Buildings (e.g., "brutalist concrete and glass", "cramped Kowloon-style stacked housing", "sleek chrome corporate")
- {SKY_CONDITION} – Sky (e.g., "smog-choked orange", "constant pouring rain", "pitch black")
- {NEON_COLORS} – Neon (e.g., "cyan and magenta", "acid green and hot pink", "stark white and warning red")
- {WET_SURFACES} – Reflections (e.g., "rain-slicked asphalt", "puddled metal grating")
- {STREET_LEVEL_DETAIL} – Detail (e.g., "crowds with glowing umbrellas", "noodle stands and steam", "abandoned tech scrap")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} – Air (e.g., "thick fog and steam from vents", "holographic advertisements projecting through smog")
- {FLYING_VEHICLES} – Vehicles (e.g., "spinner police cars with blue flashing lights", "massive cargo haulers", "no vehicles")
- {PERSPECTIVE} – Camera (e.g., "low angle looking up at the towering canyon of buildings", "high angle looking down into the abyss")
- {ARTIST_INFLUENCE} – Reference (e.g., "Syd Mead", "Josan Gonzalez", "Katsuhiro Otomo")
