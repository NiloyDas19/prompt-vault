## Purpose
Generate surreal, breathtaking landscapes of unexplored extraterrestrial worlds.

## Prompt
Epic extraterrestrial landscape of an alien planet, featuring {GEOLOGICAL_FEATURES} made of {UNUSUAL_MATERIAL}, {FLORA_FAUNA} strange alien flora, {SKY_DESCRIPTION} sky with {CELESTIAL_BODIES} visible in the atmosphere, {LIGHTING} lighting from a {STAR_TYPE} star, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {SCALE_ELEMENT} in the distance for scale, sci-fi concept art, hyper-realistic, No Man's Sky / Avatar aesthetic

## Variables
- {GEOLOGICAL_FEATURES} – Geology (e.g., "towering spiral rock formations", "deep glowing crystal canyons")
- {UNUSUAL_MATERIAL} – Material (e.g., "purple obsidian", "translucent glowing minerals", "black sand")
- {FLORA_FAUNA} – Plants (e.g., "giant bioluminescent mushroom trees", "floating jellyfish-like plants")
- {SKY_DESCRIPTION} – Sky (e.g., "toxic green swirling clouds", "deep violet clear")
- {CELESTIAL_BODIES} – Sky objects (e.g., "three massive shattered moons", "a close-orbiting gas giant")
- {LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "harsh high-contrast shadows", "soft ethereal glow")
- {STAR_TYPE} – Sun (e.g., "red dwarf", "binary blue suns")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Air (e.g., "thick rolling neon fog", "crystal clear vacuum")
- {SCALE_ELEMENT} – Scale (e.g., "a tiny crashed scout ship", "a lone astronaut explorer silhouette")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 500`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "alien planet landscape concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (Earth-like, green grass, blue sky, normal clouds, human buildings)

## Notes
- Try making the colors as un-Earthlike as possible for a stronger alien effect.
