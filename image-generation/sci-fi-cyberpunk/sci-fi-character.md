## Purpose
Generate a high-quality sci-fi character design, perfect for game concept art, RPG characters, or story illustrations.

## Prompt
Sci-fi character concept art of {CHARACTER_ROLE}, wearing {ARMOR_OR_CLOTHING_STYLE} featuring {SPECIFIC_TECH_DETAIL}, {WEAPON_OR_PROP} in hand, {POSE} pose showing their personality, {LIGHTING_STYLE} rim lighting highlighting the materials, {BACKGROUND_TYPE} background, character design sheet aesthetic, highly detailed rendering of {MATERIAL_TYPES}, {ARTIST_INFLUENCE} style, AAA game character concept art quality

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_ROLE} – Who (e.g., "a veteran space marine", "a rogue asteroid miner", "an elite corporate assassin", "an alien diplomat")
- {ARMOR_OR_CLOTHING_STYLE} – Clothes (e.g., "heavy powered exo-armor", "sleek stealth suit with holographic tactical displays", "grimy industrial pressure suit")
- {SPECIFIC_TECH_DETAIL} – Detail (e.g., "glowing blue energy conduits", "carbon fiber plating", "a scarred cybernetic prosthetic arm")
- {WEAPON_OR_PROP} – Prop (e.g., "a massive railgun", "a glowing data-pad", "a plasma sword")
- {POSE} – Action (e.g., "confident standing contrapposto", "crouched in tactical readiness", "casual leaning")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dramatic split lighting", "neutral studio", "colored neon rim lighting")
- {BACKGROUND_TYPE} – Background (e.g., "neutral dark grey studio background", "blurred spaceship interior", "cyberpunk street out of focus")
- {MATERIAL_TYPES} – Textures (e.g., "matte metal, scratched ceramics, and worn leather", "glossy carbon fiber and glowing neon")
- {ARTIST_INFLUENCE} – Reference (e.g., "Yoji Shinkawa", "Mass Effect concept art style", "Destiny character design")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "sci-fi character concept art, full body" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (fantasy, medieval, magic, civilian clothes, cropped, bad anatomy)

## Notes
- For alien races: describe the non-human traits specifically (e.g., "reptilian scales, four eyes, digitigrade legs")
- For turnaround sheet: add "character turnaround, multiple views, front, side, back, T-pose, orthographic"
