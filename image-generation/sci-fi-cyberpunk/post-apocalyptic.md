## Purpose
Generate a gripping post-apocalyptic scene showing ruined cities, wasteland survival, and the aftermath of societal collapse.

## Prompt
Post-apocalyptic concept art of {RUINED_LOCATION}, nature reclaiming the ruins with {OVERGROWTH_TYPE}, a lone {SURVIVOR_DESCRIPTION} wandering through the wasteland carrying {SURVIVAL_GEAR}, the atmosphere is {ATMOSPHERE_CONDITION} with {WEATHER_LIGHTING} lighting, rusted {ABANDONED_TECH} abandoned in the foreground, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette emphasizing the desolation, highly detailed matte painting, environmental storytelling, The Last of Us / Fallout aesthetic

## Variables
- {RUINED_LOCATION} – Place (e.g., "a flooded Times Square", "a collapsed highway overpass", "a suburban neighborhood buried in sand")
- {OVERGROWTH_TYPE} – Reclaiming nature (e.g., "lush green vines and moss", "dry dead brush", "strange glowing alien fungus", "none, just desert")
- {SURVIVOR_DESCRIPTION} – Character (e.g., "scavenger in a gas mask and tattered cloak", "traveler with a robotic dog companion")
- {SURVIVAL_GEAR} – Gear (e.g., "a heavily modified rifle and heavy backpack", "makeshift armor made of stop signs")
- {ATMOSPHERE_CONDITION} – Air (e.g., "thick with radioactive dust", "crisp and clean after humanity's end", "oppressive heat haze")
- {WEATHER_LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "golden hour cutting through the ruins", "overcast flat lighting", "toxic green moonlight")
- {ABANDONED_TECH} – Tech (e.g., "rusting husks of cars", "a crashed commercial airliner", "remnants of a giant war mech")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "muted browns and grays", "overgrown vibrant greens", "harsh sepia tones")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 350`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "post-apocalyptic concept art, ruined world" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (clean, new, populated, modern city, happy, bright, polished)

## Notes
- For Mad Max style: use "desert wasteland, vehicular combat, rust and spikes, high contrast harsh sun"
- For Last of Us style: use "overgrown urban ruins, lush green reclaiming concrete, melancholic beauty"
