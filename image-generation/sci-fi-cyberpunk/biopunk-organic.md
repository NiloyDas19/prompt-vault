## Purpose
Generate eerie, fascinating biopunk imagery where technology and biology merge seamlessly into organic machines.

## Prompt
Biopunk concept art of {BIOPUNK_SUBJECT}, a seamless fusion of flesh, bone, and machinery, {ORGANIC_DETAILS} pulsing with {FLUID_COLOR} fluid, {TECH_DETAILS} integrated directly into the organic structure, {TEXTURE_DESCRIPTION} textures, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting casting unsettling shadows, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} background, H.R. Giger inspired biomechanical aesthetic crossed with modern sci-fi, hyper-detailed macabre beauty

## Variables
- {BIOPUNK_SUBJECT} – Main focus (e.g., "a living organic spaceship interior", "a genetically engineered bio-weapon soldier", "a biological computer terminal")
- {ORGANIC_DETAILS} – Bio parts (e.g., "sinewy muscle tissue and chitinous plating", "translucent skin over pulsing organs", "veins connecting to wires")
- {FLUID_COLOR} – Liquid (e.g., "bioluminescent green", "black oil-like", "sickly yellow")
- {TECH_DETAILS} – Tech parts (e.g., "glass tubes and metal spinal implants", "carbon fiber woven into bone")
- {TEXTURE_DESCRIPTION} – Textures (e.g., "wet, glistening, and alien", "dry bone-like calcification and rusted metal")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "harsh clinical operating room light", "dim cavernous shadows", "underwater-like dappled light")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "claustrophobic and eerie", "fascinating but repulsive", "alien and cold")
- {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} – Setting (e.g., "a vat-grown laboratory", "the dark interior of a hive ship", "fleshy cavern walls")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 500 --weird 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "biopunk concept art, biomechanical" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (clean metal, normal tech, standard cyberpunk, cute, bright, happy)

## Notes
- For Scorn/Giger aesthetic: use "H.R. Giger style, monochromatic bone and grey, ribbed structures, phallic/yonic imagery"
- For Cronenberg body-horror: use "Cronenberg body horror, fleshy technology, grotesque mutation, visceral"
