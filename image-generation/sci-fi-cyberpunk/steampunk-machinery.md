## Purpose
Generate intricate, Victorian-era retro-futuristic steampunk machinery, gadgets, and vehicles.

## Prompt
Detailed steampunk concept art of a {STEAMPUNK_OBJECT}, constructed from {MATERIALS}, featuring intricate {MECHANICAL_DETAILS}, powered by {POWER_SOURCE}, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere with {STEAM_EFFECTS}, {BACKGROUND}, rich {COLOR_PALETTE}, macro photography / concept art style, highly detailed brass and copper textures, Victorian retro-futurism

## Variables
- {STEAMPUNK_OBJECT} – Object (e.g., "mechanical pocket watch", "steam-powered locomotive airship", "brass diving suit")
- {MATERIALS} – Materials (e.g., "polished brass, oxidized copper, mahogany wood, and thick glass")
- {MECHANICAL_DETAILS} – Mechanics (e.g., "exposed gears, pistons, pressure gauges, and leather belts")
- {POWER_SOURCE} – Power (e.g., "a glowing aether crystal", "a miniature coal furnace")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "grimy industrial", "elegant aristocratic")
- {STEAM_EFFECTS} – Steam (e.g., "venting pressurized steam", "glowing smoke")
- {BACKGROUND} – Background (e.g., "resting on a velvet-lined mahogany desk", "flying through Victorian London fog")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "warm amber, bronze, and deep forest green")
