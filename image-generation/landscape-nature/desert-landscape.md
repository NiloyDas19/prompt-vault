## Purpose
Generate a striking desert landscape that captures the stark beauty, scale, and geometric perfection of desert environments.

## Prompt
{DESERT_TYPE} desert landscape featuring {MAIN_SUBJECT}, {TIME_OF_DAY} light creating long dramatic shadows that reveal the {TEXTURE_DETAIL} texture of the terrain, {SKY_COLOR} sky spanning vast overhead, {ATMOSPHERE} heat shimmer or air quality, shot with {LENS} lens capturing the epic scale, rich {COLOR_PALETTE} tones dominating the scene, {HUMAN_OR_WILDLIFE_ELEMENT} providing scale reference, National Geographic photography, hyperrealistic, awe-inspiring emptiness

## Variables
- {DESERT_TYPE} – Desert type (e.g., "Saharan sand dune", "American Southwest red rock", "Atacama salt flat")
- {MAIN_SUBJECT} – Central focus (e.g., "towering sand dunes with sharp ridge lines", "cracked salt flats stretching to infinity")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Light timing (e.g., "low-angle dawn", "harsh noon", "golden hour sunset")
- {TEXTURE_DETAIL} – Surface texture (e.g., "rippled wind-carved", "cracked polygon", "smooth flowing")
- {SKY_COLOR} – Sky (e.g., "deep cobalt blue", "fiery orange and pink", "star-filled night")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Air quality (e.g., "shimmering heat haze", "crystal clear", "fine dust haze")
- {LENS} – Focal length (e.g., "24mm wide-angle", "200mm telephoto compressing the dunes")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Dominant colors (e.g., "warm amber and sienna", "cool lavender and pink")
- {HUMAN_OR_WILDLIFE_ELEMENT} – Scale element (e.g., "a lone camel caravan", "a solitary figure", "no human presence")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "desert landscape photography, arid beauty" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (green, water, trees, urban, buildings, overcast)

## Notes
- For abstract dune patterns: use aerial or drone perspective, "shot from directly above"
- For surreal take: add "Salvador Dali surrealism, melting elements, impossible scale"
