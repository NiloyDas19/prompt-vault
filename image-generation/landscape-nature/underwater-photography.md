## Purpose
Generate stunning underwater photography revealing the hidden beauty of the ocean's interior world.

## Prompt
Professional underwater ocean photography of {UNDERWATER_SUBJECT}, {LIGHT_RAYS} god rays of sunlight penetrating from the surface above creating {WATER_COLOR} ethereal blue-green water atmosphere, {MARINE_LIFE} visible in {DISTANCE_RELATIONSHIP}, visibility is {VISIBILITY}, shot with wide-angle dome port 16mm lens, {DEPTH} depth, capturing both the surface light from above and the subject below simultaneously, National Geographic marine photography, breathtaking clarity, otherworldly beauty

## Variables
- {UNDERWATER_SUBJECT} – Main subject (e.g., "a coral reef ecosystem teeming with tropical fish", "a humpback whale", "an ancient shipwreck covered in coral")
- {LIGHT_RAYS} – Light (e.g., "dramatic", "soft filtered", "shafts of bright")
- {WATER_COLOR} – Water tones (e.g., "deep sapphire", "tropical turquoise", "murky blue-green")
- {MARINE_LIFE} – Surrounding life (e.g., "schools of silver fish", "manta rays", "sharks circling")
- {DISTANCE_RELATIONSHIP} – Composition (e.g., "surrounding the main subject", "in the far background")
- {VISIBILITY} – Water clarity (e.g., "crystal clear 30m visibility", "slightly hazy with particles")
- {DEPTH} – Depth feel (e.g., "shallow reef with the surface visible above", "deep open ocean")
