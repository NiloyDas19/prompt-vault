## Purpose
Generate an atmospheric, ethereal forest scene filled with light, mist, and a sense of ancient magic.

## Prompt
Mystical ancient {FOREST_TYPE} forest, shafts of {LIGHT_COLOR} light breaking through the {CANOPY_DESCRIPTION} canopy and illuminating floating dust particles, {GROUND_COVER} covering the forest floor, morning mist curling around {TREE_DESCRIPTION} tree trunks, {MAGICAL_ELEMENT} adding an otherworldly quality, {MOOD} atmosphere, wide environmental shot, ultra-detailed botanical accuracy, f/8 landscape photography, Tolkien-esque beauty, National Geographic quality

## Variables
- {FOREST_TYPE} – Type of forest (e.g., "temperate rainforest", "ancient redwood", "beech woodland")
- {LIGHT_COLOR} – Light color (e.g., "golden", "emerald green", "cool blue")
- {CANOPY_DESCRIPTION} – Tree tops (e.g., "dense moss-covered", "autumn-golden", "towering cathedral-like")
- {GROUND_COVER} – Forest floor (e.g., "thick green moss and ferns", "autumn fallen leaves", "wildflowers and clover")
- {TREE_DESCRIPTION} – Tree trunks (e.g., "enormous ancient", "silver birch", "gnarled twisted")
- {MAGICAL_ELEMENT} – Fantasy element (e.g., "bioluminescent mushrooms", "floating fireflies", "shimmering fairy lights")
- {MOOD} – Overall atmosphere (e.g., "serene and peaceful", "mysterious and ancient")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:2 --v 6.1 --stylize 350`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "ethereal nature photography" at the start for best results
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (urban, buildings, people, plastic, cartoon, artificial)

## Notes
- For horror forest: replace magical elements with "dead trees, fog, pale moonlight, dark and threatening"
- For fantasy book cover: add "fantasy illustration style, painterly, epic scope, Greg Rutkowski influence"
