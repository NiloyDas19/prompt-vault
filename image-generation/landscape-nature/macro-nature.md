## Purpose
Generate stunning extreme macro photography that reveals the hidden detail and beauty of small natural subjects.

## Prompt
Extreme macro photography of {MACRO_SUBJECT}, photographed at {MAGNIFICATION}x magnification, {WATER_OR_DEW} droplets catching light like crystals, {LIGHTING_STYLE} light raking across the surface revealing {TEXTURE_DETAILS} micro-textures, {COLOR_SCHEME} color palette, razor-thin depth of field isolating the subject from a beautifully blurred {BACKGROUND_COLORS} bokeh background, hyper-detailed, scientific beauty, award-winning macro photography, Canon MP-E 65mm or Laowa 25mm macro lens quality

## Variables
- {MACRO_SUBJECT} – What is photographed (e.g., "a dewdrop-covered spider web strand", "the surface of a monarch butterfly wing", "a bee covered in pollen")
- {MAGNIFICATION} – Scale (e.g., "5", "10", "3")
- {WATER_OR_DEW} – Moisture (e.g., "morning dew", "rain", "mist", "no moisture")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft diffused", "dramatic side", "ring flash", "backlit translucent")
- {TEXTURE_DETAILS} – What textures are revealed (e.g., "intricate scale patterns", "fine hair-like structures")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "jewel-tone greens and golds", "soft pink and cream")
- {BACKGROUND_COLORS} – Background blur colors (e.g., "soft green forest", "warm golden", "cool blue sky")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:3 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "extreme macro photography, scientific detail" at beginning
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (wide shot, landscape, humans, blurry subject, soft, undefined)

## Notes
- For abstract macro: focus on patterns and colors: "abstract macro photograph emphasizing pattern and texture only"
- For scientific illustration: add "field guide illustration quality, botanical art, accurate detail"
