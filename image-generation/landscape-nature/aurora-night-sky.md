## Purpose
Generate a stunning astrophotography or aurora borealis scene with a dramatic foreground landscape.

## Prompt
Breathtaking {AURORA_OR_NIGHT_SKY} photography of {SKY_PHENOMENON} erupting across the sky in {LIGHT_COLORS}, reflecting in {REFLECTION_SURFACE} below, {FOREGROUND_LANDSCAPE} silhouetted against the light-painted sky, {STARS_DETAIL} stars visible through the display, long exposure astrophotography, hyper-detailed sky with visible nebulosity, {MOON_STATE}, the landscape below bathed in {AMBIENT_LIGHT_COLOR} ambient glow, magical and awe-inspiring, award-winning nature photography

## Variables
- {AURORA_OR_NIGHT_SKY} – Type (e.g., "Aurora Borealis", "Milky Way", "meteor shower")
- {SKY_PHENOMENON} – The celestial display (e.g., "swirling green and purple aurora curtains", "the Milky Way galactic core")
- {LIGHT_COLORS} – Colors (e.g., "electric green and magenta", "silver and violet", "golden and white")
- {REFLECTION_SURFACE} – What reflects the sky (e.g., "a perfectly still frozen lake", "wet beach sand")
- {FOREGROUND_LANDSCAPE} – Land below (e.g., "snow-covered pine trees", "Viking-age wooden church")
- {STARS_DETAIL} – Star visibility (e.g., "thousands of individual stars", "the Milky Way band")
- {MOON_STATE} – Moon (e.g., "crescent moon on the horizon", "moonless dark sky")
- {AMBIENT_LIGHT_COLOR} – Ground light (e.g., "ethereal green", "cold blue", "warm amber from distant village lights")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "astrophotography, long exposure, night sky" explicitly
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (daytime, overexposed, artificial lights, buildings, people, fake)

## Notes
- For pure Milky Way: remove aurora and focus on "the galactic core arching over {LANDSCAPE}"
- For star trails: add "circular star trails around Polaris, 4-hour long exposure, star trail photography"
