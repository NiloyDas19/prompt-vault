## Purpose
Generate a spectacular weather photography image — storms, lightning, tornados, or other dramatic meteorological events.

## Prompt
Dramatic weather photography featuring {WEATHER_PHENOMENON} over {LANDSCAPE_SETTING}, {STORM_COLORS} storm clouds {CLOUD_DESCRIPTION}, {LIGHTNING_OR_EFFECT} electrical energy cutting through the sky, the landscape below showing {LANDSCAPE_REACTION}, {TIME_OF_DAY} light mixing with the storm's own illumination, wide-angle panoramic shot capturing the full scope of the event, storm chaser photography, National Geographic weather coverage quality, raw elemental power

## Variables
- {WEATHER_PHENOMENON} – Storm type (e.g., "a massive supercell thunderstorm", "a rope tornado", "a blizzard whiteout")
- {LANDSCAPE_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "flat Great Plains farmland", "coastal beach", "mountain valley")
- {STORM_COLORS} – Sky colors (e.g., "sickly green and purple", "deep charcoal and silver", "electric blue")
- {CLOUD_DESCRIPTION} – Cloud detail (e.g., "rotating wall cloud", "anvil-shaped cumulonimbus", "shelf cloud rolling forward")
- {LIGHTNING_OR_EFFECT} – Energy (e.g., "multiple lightning bolts", "single dramatic bolt", "no lightning, just power")
- {LANDSCAPE_REACTION} – Ground effect (e.g., "crops bending in the wind", "debris flying", "perfectly still eerie calm")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – When (e.g., "twilight blue hour", "golden hour before the storm", "night with only lightning illumination")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "storm photography, dramatic weather" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (calm, sunny, clear, peaceful, flat, boring)

## Notes
- For black and white: add "black and white film, Ansel Adams drama, high contrast silver gelatin print"
- For abstract weather: "abstract swirling storm from directly above, satellite view, spiral cloud formation"
