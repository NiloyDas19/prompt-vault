## Purpose
Generate a dramatic, cinematic cityscape photograph with stunning light, reflections, and urban energy.

## Prompt
Cinematic {CITY_TYPE} cityscape of {SPECIFIC_VIEW}, {TIME_OF_DAY} creating {LIGHT_EFFECT} across the skyline, {WET_OR_DRY} streets reflecting {REFLECTION_COLORS} light, {ATMOSPHERIC_CONDITION} in the air, shot from {VANTAGE_POINT}, {LENS_FOCAL_LENGTH} lens, city lights and {TRAFFIC_OR_MOVEMENT} creating dynamic energy, {COLOR_GRADE} color grade, architectural photography meets fine art, hyper-detailed urban textures, award-winning cityscape photography

## Variables
- {CITY_TYPE} – City style (e.g., "futuristic Asian metropolis", "classic European", "gritty New York", "neon-lit Tokyo")
- {SPECIFIC_VIEW} – What's shown (e.g., "skyscrapers reflecting in the harbor", "a busy night market street")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – When (e.g., "blue hour", "rainy night", "golden hour sunset")
- {LIGHT_EFFECT} – How light falls (e.g., "warm orange glow", "cool blue cast", "neon rainbow reflections")
- {WET_OR_DRY} – Street condition (e.g., "rain-slicked", "dry", "flooded with puddles")
- {REFLECTION_COLORS} – Reflection colors (e.g., "neon pink and blue", "warm yellow and orange")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_CONDITION} – Air (e.g., "light fog", "clear crisp", "heavy smog haze")
- {VANTAGE_POINT} – Camera position (e.g., "elevated rooftop perspective", "street level looking up", "aerial drone shot")
- {LENS_FOCAL_LENGTH} – Lens (e.g., "24mm wide", "70mm medium", "200mm compressed telephoto")
- {TRAFFIC_OR_MOVEMENT} – Motion (e.g., "long-exposure car light trails", "blurred pedestrians", "still and empty")
- {COLOR_GRADE} – Color treatment (e.g., "teal and orange", "cool blue", "warm amber")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "architectural cityscape photography" at beginning
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (nature, countryside, flat, boring, overexposed)

## Notes
- For Hong Kong aesthetic: add "dense neon signage, layered urban density, rain, Blade Runner atmosphere"
- For minimal Scandinavian: add "clean architecture, muted tones, winter light, negative space, design aesthetic"
