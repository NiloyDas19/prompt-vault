## Purpose
Generate a powerful, visually stunning ocean or seascape photograph with dramatic light and water movement.

## Prompt
Dramatic ocean seascape of {SPECIFIC_SCENE}, {WATER_STATE} ocean with {WATER_COLOR} water, {SKY_CONDITION} sky above, {TIME_OF_DAY} light painting the scene with {LIGHT_QUALITY}, {FOREGROUND_ROCKS_OR_SHORE} in the foreground, long exposure effect blurring the water into silky smooth motion, {MOOD} mood, ultra-sharp detail on the rocks contrasting with silky water, professional seascape photography, Ansel Adams meets modern color photography

## Variables
- {SPECIFIC_SCENE} – What the scene is (e.g., "a rugged cliffside with crashing waves", "a deserted tropical beach at low tide")
- {WATER_STATE} – Water movement (e.g., "powerful storm-churned", "gentle rippling", "glassy calm")
- {WATER_COLOR} – Color of water (e.g., "deep sapphire blue", "emerald green", "turquoise tropical")
- {SKY_CONDITION} – Sky (e.g., "stormy purple-grey clouds", "pastel pink and gold sunrise", "fiery red sunset")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – When (e.g., "blue hour", "sunrise", "golden hour")
- {LIGHT_QUALITY} – Light description (e.g., "dramatic warm", "cool diffused", "moody low")
- {FOREGROUND_ROCKS_OR_SHORE} – Foreground (e.g., "tide pools and barnacle-covered rocks", "wet black volcanic rocks")
- {MOOD} – Emotional quality (e.g., "powerful and awe-inspiring", "peaceful and meditative")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Specify "long exposure seascape photography" explicitly
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (people, boats, buildings, cartoonish, flat, murky)

## Notes
- For tropical paradise: add "white sand beach, palm trees, crystal clear water, Maldives aesthetic"
- For black and white drama: specify "monochrome, silver gelatin print quality, Ansel Adams style"
