## Purpose
Generate a breathtaking, epic mountain landscape photograph with dramatic lighting and scale.

## Prompt
Epic mountain landscape photograph of {MOUNTAIN_SCENE}, {TIME_OF_DAY} with {SKY_DRAMA} sky, {FOREGROUND_ELEMENT} in the foreground providing scale and depth, {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} filling the valleys, dramatic volumetric lighting, shot with 24mm wide-angle lens, deep focus f/11, rich detail across the entire frame from foreground to background, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, National Geographic / Ansel Adams inspired, awe-inspiring scale

## Variables
- {MOUNTAIN_SCENE} – The specific scene (e.g., "snow-capped jagged peaks reflected in a crystal alpine lake", "volcanic mountains shrouded in clouds")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – When (e.g., "blue hour just before dawn", "dramatic storm clearing at sunset", "golden hour alpenglow")
- {SKY_DRAMA} – Sky description (e.g., "fiery red and orange", "dark storm clouds with god rays breaking through")
- {FOREGROUND_ELEMENT} – Foreground detail (e.g., "a lone pine tree", "wildflowers in bloom", "a wooden hiking trail sign")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} – Weather/atmosphere (e.g., "morning mist", "low-hanging clouds", "smoke from distant fires")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "cool blues and purples", "warm ambers and reds", "earthy greens and grays")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "landscape photography, majestic scale" at the beginning
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (people, buildings, power lines, flat, boring, overexposed)

## Notes
- For Milky Way landscape: replace lighting with "Milky Way galaxy arching overhead, astrophotography, long exposure, star trails"
- For infrared look: add "infrared photography, white foliage, dark dramatic sky, surreal landscape"
