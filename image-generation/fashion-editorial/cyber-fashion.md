## Purpose
Generate sleek, functional, futuristic techwear and cyberpunk fashion photography.

## Prompt
Techwear fashion photography featuring a model wearing {TECHWEAR_OUTFIT}, made of {FABRIC_MATERIALS}, equipped with {FUNCTIONAL_ACCESSORIES}, posing in a {CYBER_LOCATION}, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting catching the reflective elements of the clothing, {COLOR_PALETTE}, futuristic streetwear lookbook, Acronym / Y-3 aesthetic, highly detailed

## Variables
- {TECHWEAR_OUTFIT} – Outfit (e.g., "an oversized waterproof poncho and articulated cargo pants", "a sleek tactical harness over a fitted bodysuit")
- {FABRIC_MATERIALS} – Fabric (e.g., "matte black Gore-Tex and ripstop nylon", "iridescent waterproof fabric", "carbon fiber weave")
- {FUNCTIONAL_ACCESSORIES} – Accessories (e.g., "multiple matte black buckles, straps, and a half-face respirator mask", "a modular chest rig and waterproof backpack")
- {CYBER_LOCATION} – Where (e.g., "a rain-slicked neon alley", "a sterile white laboratory corridor", "a brutalist subway station")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "dystopian survival", "covert operative", "high-tech urban")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "neon rim lighting", "harsh security floodlights", "moody ambient city light")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "all-black monochromatic", "black with stark white and neon orange accents", "military olive and grey")
