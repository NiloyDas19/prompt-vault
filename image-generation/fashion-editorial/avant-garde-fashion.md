## Purpose
Generate striking, experimental haute couture imagery that pushes the boundaries of traditional fashion silhouettes.

## Prompt
Avant-garde haute couture fashion editorial featuring a model wearing {GARMENT_DESCRIPTION}, constructed from {UNCONVENTIONAL_MATERIALS}, the silhouette is {SILHOUETTE_SHAPE}, {STYLING_DETAILS}, set against a {BACKGROUND_SETTING}, {LIGHTING_STYLE}, high fashion magazine photography, Iris Van Herpen / Alexander McQueen influence, hyper-detailed, surreal fashion concept

## Variables
- {GARMENT_DESCRIPTION} – Garment (e.g., "a massive sculptural gown", "a structured exoskeleton suit", "an inflating geometric coat")
- {UNCONVENTIONAL_MATERIALS} – Materials (e.g., "shattered glass and liquid chrome", "laser-cut silicone and 3D printed resin", "folded origami paper")
- {SILHOUETTE_SHAPE} – Silhouette (e.g., "impossibly wide and architectural", "sharp and aggressive", "fluid and gravity-defying")
- {STYLING_DETAILS} – Styling (e.g., "face obscured by a crystal mask", "hair styled into severe geometric shapes")
- {BACKGROUND_SETTING} – Background (e.g., "a stark white seamless studio", "an endless mirror room", "a brutalist concrete bunker")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "clinical high-key lighting", "dramatic spotlight isolating the texture", "colored gel lights")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 500`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "avant-garde fashion photography, experimental couture" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (casual wear, normal clothes, street style, boring, flat)

## Notes
- Focus on the materials to make it truly avant-garde (e.g., "dress made entirely of frozen smoke").
