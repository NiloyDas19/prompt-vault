## Purpose
Generate moody, intellectual, and vintage-inspired Dark Academia fashion imagery.

## Prompt
Dark academia fashion photography of {SUBJECT} wearing {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION}, {FABRIC_TEXTURES}, styling includes {ACCESSORIES}, reading a book in {SETTING}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting through {WINDOW_TYPE}, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, vintage intellectual aesthetic, 35mm film photography feel, moody and scholarly

## Variables
- {SUBJECT} – Who (e.g., "a young scholar", "a brooding literature student", "two friends sharing notes")
- {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION} – Clothes (e.g., "a tailored tweed blazer over a turtleneck and plaid trousers", "an oversized cable-knit sweater and a pleated skirt")
- {FABRIC_TEXTURES} – Fabrics (e.g., "heavy wool, corduroy, and worn leather", "crisp cotton and cashmere")
- {ACCESSORIES} – Accessories (e.g., "a vintage leather satchel and wire-rimmed glasses", "a pocket watch and a silk tie")
- {SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a dusty gothic university library", "a rainy Parisian cafe", "a wood-paneled study with leather armchairs")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "warm light from a green banker's lamp", "gloomy overcast light")
- {WINDOW_TYPE} – Windows (e.g., "rain-streaked stained glass windows", "large gothic arches")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "melancholic and studious", "mysterious and secretive")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "rich browns, forest greens, and burgundy", "sepia and charcoal grey")
