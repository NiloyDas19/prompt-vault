## Purpose
Generate a striking high-fashion editorial image suitable for magazine spreads, lookbooks, or campaign photography.

## Prompt
High fashion editorial photograph featuring {MODEL_DESCRIPTION} wearing {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION} from {STYLE_DIRECTION} design direction, photographed in {LOCATION_TYPE} setting of {SPECIFIC_LOCATION}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} dramatic editorial lighting creating {MOOD}, {CAMERA_ANGLE} composition, {STYLING_ELEMENTS} hair and makeup styling, {MOVEMENT_OR_POSE} dynamic {POSE_QUALITY} pose, {COLOR_STORY} color story dominating the frame, {PHOTOGRAPHER_STYLE} photographic influence, {MAGAZINE} editorial quality, unapologetically high fashion

## Variables
- {MODEL_DESCRIPTION} – Model (e.g., "a statuesque model with a shaved head", "a model with sharp angular features")
- {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION} – Clothes (e.g., "an oversized structured black coat with sharp shoulders", "a bias-cut liquid satin gown")
- {STYLE_DIRECTION} – Design direction (e.g., "Japanese minimalist", "maximalist Baroque excess", "romantic deconstructed")
- {LOCATION_TYPE} – Setting type (e.g., "architectural", "natural", "industrial", "studio")
- {SPECIFIC_LOCATION} – Exact setting (e.g., "a brutalist concrete building", "a salt flat", "a rose garden")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "harsh midday sun creating graphic shadows", "soft studio beauty light", "natural golden hour")
- {MOOD} – Mood (e.g., "fierce and powerful", "ethereal and otherworldly", "melancholy and introspective")
- {CAMERA_ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "full-length low-angle", "three-quarter profile", "extreme close-up on garment detail")
- {STYLING_ELEMENTS} – Styling (e.g., "severe slicked-back hair, bold red lip", "bare minimal makeup, wet hair")
- {MOVEMENT_OR_POSE} – Action (e.g., "fabric caught mid-movement in the wind", "rigid architectural stillness")
- {POSE_QUALITY} – Pose type
- {COLOR_STORY} – Color (e.g., "monochromatic all-black", "tonal earth palette", "bold complementary color clash")
- {PHOTOGRAPHER_STYLE} – Reference (e.g., "Steven Meisel", "Helmut Newton", "Nick Knight")
- {MAGAZINE} – Publication (e.g., "Vogue Italia", "Another Magazine", "LOVE Magazine")

## Modifiers
