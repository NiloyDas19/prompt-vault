## Purpose
Generate classic, elegant fashion imagery reminiscent of mid-century fashion magazine covers.

## Prompt
Classic {DECADE} fashion magazine cover photography of an elegant woman wearing {FASHION_ITEM}, styled with {ACCESSORIES}, {MAKEUP_HAIR} hair and makeup, {POSE_EXPRESSION}, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} background, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting, {PHOTOGRAPHER_INFLUENCE} style, {COLOR_GRADE} color grade, iconic and timeless, editorial fashion perfection

## Variables
- {DECADE} – Era (e.g., "1950s", "1960s", "1930s", "1970s")
- {FASHION_ITEM} – Garment (e.g., "a massive Christian Dior New Look tulle gown", "a sleek 1960s mod shift dress", "a tailored 1940s tweed suit")
- {ACCESSORIES} – Accessories (e.g., "white silk gloves and a wide-brimmed hat", "oversized round sunglasses and bold geometric earrings")
- {MAKEUP_HAIR} – Styling (e.g., "perfect red lips and victory rolls", "heavy Twiggy eyeliner and a pixie cut")
- {POSE_EXPRESSION} – Pose (e.g., "looking haughtily over her shoulder", "a bright open-mouthed laugh", "poised and statuesque")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "a solid pastel studio backdrop", "the streets of 1950s Paris", "a luxurious vintage car")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft glamour portrait lighting", "high contrast black and white", "vibrant Kodachrome flash")
- {PHOTOGRAPHER_INFLUENCE} – Reference (e.g., "Richard Avedon", "Irving Penn", "Cecil Beaton")
- {COLOR_GRADE} – Colors (e.g., "rich vintage Technicolor", "classic silver gelatin black and white", "faded pastel")
