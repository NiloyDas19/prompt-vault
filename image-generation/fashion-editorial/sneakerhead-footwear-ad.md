## Purpose
Generate high-impact, dynamic commercial photography for sneakers and footwear.

## Prompt
Dynamic footwear advertising photography of {SNEAKER_DESCRIPTION}, {PLACEMENT_OR_ACTION}, {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT}, {LIGHTING_EFFECTS} lighting highlighting the {MATERIAL_DETAILS} materials, {DYNAMIC_ELEMENTS} adding energy to the frame, {COLOR_SCHEME} color scheme matching the shoe, extreme sharp focus on the shoe, Nike / Adidas campaign aesthetic, hyper-detailed commercial product photography

## Variables
- {SNEAKER_DESCRIPTION} – Shoe (e.g., "a futuristic chunky streetwear sneaker", "a classic high-top basketball shoe in red and black", "a sleek running shoe with translucent soles")
- {PLACEMENT_OR_ACTION} – Action (e.g., "frozen mid-air in a dynamic jump", "resting on a piece of shattered concrete", "being worn by a model walking on water")
- {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} – Setting (e.g., "a clean studio backdrop with neon accents", "a gritty urban basketball court", "an abstract floating geometric space")
- {LIGHTING_EFFECTS} – Light (e.g., "dramatic edge lighting and colored gels", "bright punchy studio strobe", "moody under-lighting")
- {MATERIAL_DETAILS} – Textures (e.g., "woven knit texture and glossy plastic", "premium tumbled leather and suede")
- {DYNAMIC_ELEMENTS} – Energy (e.g., "water splashing around the sole", "smoke and sparks in the background", "motion blur on the background, shoe perfectly sharp")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "monochrome black with electric blue accents", "vibrant neon pop art colors", "earth tones")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "sneaker commercial photography, dynamic footwear ad" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (distorted shoe shape, bad logo, messy, amateur, feet, barefoot)

## Notes
- AI often struggles with real brand logos (Nike swoosh, etc.). Focus on describing the shoe's aesthetic rather than requesting a specific real brand logo for better results.
