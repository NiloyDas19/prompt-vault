## Purpose
Generate clean, highly commercial minimalist fashion photography focusing entirely on the garments and silhouette.

## Prompt
Minimalist fashion lookbook photography of a model wearing {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION}, {GARMENT_FIT}, {STYLING}, shot against a {STUDIO_BACKDROP}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting casting {SHADOW_TYPE} shadows, {POSE}, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, clean Scandinavian design aesthetic, COS / Jil Sander influence, high-end e-commerce editorial

## Variables
- {OUTFIT_DESCRIPTION} – Outfit (e.g., "an oversized unstructured beige wool coat over wide-leg trousers", "a crisp white poplin shirt with asymmetrical buttons")
- {GARMENT_FIT} – Fit (e.g., "draped and voluminous", "architectural and stiff", "perfectly tailored")
- {STYLING} – Detail (e.g., "no accessories, barefoot", "styled with minimal leather mules")
- {STUDIO_BACKDROP} – Background (e.g., "pure white seamless cyclorama", "warm neutral beige paper", "soft grey concrete wall")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft even diffused lighting", "single harsh directional light")
- {SHADOW_TYPE} – Shadows (e.g., "no shadows", "sharp graphic shadows on the background")
- {POSE} – Pose (e.g., "relaxed and effortless standing", "subtle movement walking forward", "seated on a simple wooden block")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "monochrome neutrals: taupe, ivory, sand", "stark black and white", "navy and cream")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "minimalist fashion lookbook photography, clean background" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (cluttered background, busy patterns, loud colors, over-accessorized, messy)

## Notes
- To emphasize the fabric quality, add: "sharp focus on the fabric texture and drape."
