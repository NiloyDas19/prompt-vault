## Purpose
Generate dreamy, magical fashion photography that blurs the line between high fashion and fantasy illustration.

## Prompt
Ethereal fantasy fashion photography of a {SUBJECT} wearing a {MAGICAL_GARMENT} made of {MAGICAL_MATERIAL}, {ACCESSORIES}, located in {ENCHANTED_SETTING}, {LIGHTING} lighting with {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECTS}, {POSE_AND_MOVEMENT}, {COLOR_MOOD} color mood, Pre-Raphaelite painting meets Vogue editorial, Tim Walker style, dreamy and otherworldly, highly detailed

## Variables
- {SUBJECT} – Who (e.g., "an elven princess", "a forest nymph", "a dark sorceress model")
- {MAGICAL_GARMENT} – Garment (e.g., "a flowing translucent gown that seems to float", "a structured dress mimicking butterfly wings")
- {MAGICAL_MATERIAL} – Material (e.g., "woven starlight and spider silk", "shimmering iridescent organza", "dyed smoke")
- {ACCESSORIES} – Accessories (e.g., "a crown of actual floating glowing crystals", "intricate silver filigree armor over the dress")
- {ENCHANTED_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a moonlit magical forest", "standing on a mirror-like lake at dawn", "a ruined gothic chapel overgrown with white roses")
- {LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "soft glowing bioluminescence", "ethereal moonlight", "golden hour dappled light")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECTS} – Effects (e.g., "surrounded by floating glowing embers", "thick ground mist rolling around her feet")
- {POSE_AND_MOVEMENT} – Action (e.g., "fabric billowing impossibly around her", "gazing fiercely into the camera", "reaching toward the light")
- {COLOR_MOOD} – Colors (e.g., "soft pastel pinks and lavenders", "deep jewel tones: emerald and sapphire", "ghostly silver and white")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 450`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "fantasy fashion photography, ethereal and magical" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (modern city, casual clothes, ugly, mundane, boring, plastic)
