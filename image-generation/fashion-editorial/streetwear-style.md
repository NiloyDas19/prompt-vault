## Purpose
Generate authentic, cool streetwear fashion photography with urban settings and editorial energy.

## Prompt
Streetwear fashion photography of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} wearing {OUTFIT_DETAILS}, photographed in {URBAN_SETTING} urban environment, {WEATHER_AND_TIME} conditions creating {ATMOSPHERE}, {PHOTOGRAPHY_STYLE} street photography approach, {SHOT_TYPE} shot type, {LIGHTING_CONDITION} natural available light, raw authentic energy, {BRAND_AESTHETIC} brand aesthetic, {CULTURAL_REFERENCE} cultural influence, shot on {CAMERA_TYPE} camera, {COLOR_GRADE} color grade, effortlessly cool, {PUBLICATION_STYLE} editorial quality

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Who (e.g., "a young man with bleached hair", "a skater in baggy clothes", "two friends in matching fits")
- {OUTFIT_DETAILS} – Clothes (e.g., "oversized vintage band tee, wide-leg cargo pants, chunky sneakers", "a puffer jacket, track pants")
- {URBAN_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a graffiti-covered underpass", "a rooftop overlooking the city", "a basketball court")
- {WEATHER_AND_TIME} – Conditions (e.g., "rainy night with reflections", "sunny afternoon with harsh shadows")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "energetic and alive", "cool and nonchalant", "tense and focused")
- {PHOTOGRAPHY_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "documentary candid", "editorial posed", "portrait with environment")
- {SHOT_TYPE} – Framing (e.g., "full-length", "waist-up half body", "dynamic action")
- {LIGHTING_CONDITION} – Light (e.g., "harsh noon sun", "overcast diffused", "city lights at night")
- {BRAND_AESTHETIC} – Brand (e.g., "Supreme meets Comme des Garçons", "Nike Sportswear campaign")
- {CULTURAL_REFERENCE} – Culture (e.g., "New York skate culture", "Tokyo Harajuku fashion", "London grime scene")
- {CAMERA_TYPE} – Camera look (e.g., "35mm film", "digital SLR", "disposable film camera")
- {COLOR_GRADE} – Grade (e.g., "washed-out film look", "punchy contrasty", "neutral clean")
- {PUBLICATION_STYLE} – Pub (e.g., "Highsnobiety", "Hypebeast", "Dazed & Confused")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "streetwear fashion photography, urban authentic" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (formal, corporate, high fashion runway, overly styled, plastic)

## Notes
- For lookbook: add "clean background, consistent lighting, product-forward, e-commerce lookbook style"
- For culture documentary: add "photojournalism style, decisive moment, authentic subculture, documentary"
