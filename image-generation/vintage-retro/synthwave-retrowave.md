## Purpose
Generate vibrant 1980s-inspired synthwave or retrowave artwork with neon grids, chromatic gradients, and retro-futurist aesthetics.

## Prompt
Synthwave retrowave {SCENE_TYPE} scene featuring {MAIN_SUBJECT}, {NEON_GRID} glowing {GRID_COLOR} perspective grid stretching to the horizon, {SKY_TYPE} sky in {GRADIENT_COLORS} gradient from {TOP_COLOR} at top to {HORIZON_COLOR} at horizon, {CELESTIAL_BODY} enormous {CELESTIAL_BODY_TYPE} on the horizon, {NEON_ACCENT_ELEMENTS} neon accent elements creating depth, {CHROME_ELEMENT} chrome or metallic reflective surface, {SCAN_LINES} scanline overlay texture, {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} retro 80s typography aesthetic, {ARTISTIC_REFERENCE} artistic influence, saturated beyond reality, 1984 retro-futurism, Outrun aesthetic

## Variables
- {SCENE_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "landscape", "cityscape", "character portrait", "vehicle")
- {MAIN_SUBJECT} – Subject (e.g., "a neon-lit sports car", "a lone silhouetted figure", "a robot samurai")
- {NEON_GRID} – Grid presence (e.g., "a geometric flat", "a curved checkerboard", "parallel speed lines")
- {GRID_COLOR} – Grid color (e.g., "hot pink", "electric purple", "cyan")
- {SKY_TYPE} – Sky (e.g., "clear twilight", "starfield", "cloud-streaked")
- {GRADIENT_COLORS} – Sky gradient
- {TOP_COLOR} – Top of sky (e.g., "deep purple", "midnight blue", "black")
- {HORIZON_COLOR} – Horizon (e.g., "hot pink", "orange", "magenta")
- {CELESTIAL_BODY} – Size (e.g., "enormous", "massive")
- {CELESTIAL_BODY_TYPE} – What (e.g., "sun with horizontal speed lines", "retro striped planet")
- {NEON_ACCENT_ELEMENTS} – Accents (e.g., "palm trees outlined in neon", "neon wireframe pyramids", "laser grid lines")
- {CHROME_ELEMENT} – Chrome (e.g., "a chrome car hood reflecting the grid", "mirrored floor")
- {SCAN_LINES} – Texture (e.g., "subtle CRT scanlines", "heavy VHS scanlines", "no scanlines")
- {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} – Type (e.g., "chrome 3D extruded lettering", "neon tube font", "pixel font")
- {ARTISTIC_REFERENCE} – Reference (e.g., "Kavinsky album art", "Carpenter Brut", "Drive film poster")

## Modifiers
