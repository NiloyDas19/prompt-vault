## Purpose
Generate vibrant, graphic 1960s Pop Art illustrations resembling comic books or Warhol prints.

## Prompt
Classic 1960s Pop Art illustration of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, rendered in {ART_STYLE} style, bold {LINE_WORK} black outlines, {COLOR_PALETTE} saturated flat colors, {TEXTURE_EFFECT} Ben-Day dots texture visible, {COMPOSITION_FOCUS} composition, {TEXT_BUBBLE} element, graphic design, vintage comic book aesthetic, Roy Lichtenstein / Andy Warhol influence, striking high contrast

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Subject (e.g., "a crying woman on the telephone", "a retro Campbell's soup can", "an explosion of an airplane")
- {ART_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "vintage comic book panel", "silkscreen print", "mass commercial advertising")
- {LINE_WORK} – Outlines (e.g., "thick graphic", "variable comic inking")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "primary colors: bright yellow, cyan, and red", "neon silkscreen colors")
- {TEXTURE_EFFECT} – Texture (e.g., "halftone Ben-Day dots shading", "misaligned CMYK print registration", "flat matte paint")
- {COMPOSITION_FOCUS} – Layout (e.g., "extreme close-up on the eyes", "centered iconic object", "dynamic diagonal action")
- {TEXT_BUBBLE} – Text (e.g., "a thought bubble with dramatic dots", "bold action text like 'BAM!'", "no text")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "Pop Art style illustration, vintage comic, Ben-Day dots" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (photorealistic, 3D render, gradients, soft shading, muted colors)

## Notes
- Emphasize the physical print medium: "cheap comic book paper texture, slightly yellowed edges, off-register color printing."
