## Purpose
Generate nostalgic, colorful mid-century Americana scenes, focusing on classic diners, drive-ins, and 1950s car culture.

## Prompt
Classic 1950s Americana photography of {SCENE_FOCUS}, located at a {LOCATION_TYPE}, featuring {ICONIC_ELEMENTS}, {TIME_OF_DAY} with {LIGHTING_STYLE}, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette emphasizing the era, {PEOPLE_DETAILS} in period clothing, Kodak Kodachrome film aesthetic, vintage postcard feel, Norman Rockwell meets Edward Hopper

## Variables
- {SCENE_FOCUS} – Focus (e.g., "a gleaming cherry-red classic car parked outside", "a waitress pouring coffee at the counter", "teenagers sharing a milkshake")
- {LOCATION_TYPE} – Where (e.g., "a neon-lit chrome diner", "a Route 66 gas station", "a drive-in movie theater")
- {ICONIC_ELEMENTS} – Era details (e.g., "checkerboard floors, red vinyl booths, and a jukebox", "huge tailfins on cars, glowing neon signage")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Time (e.g., "warm summer sunset", "late night under buzzing neon", "bright sunny afternoon")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "glowing neon reflecting on wet pavement", "bright sunny high-contrast", "cinematic moody diner lighting")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "pastel pinks, mint greens, and chrome", "rich Kodachrome reds and yellows")
- {PEOPLE_DETAILS} – People (e.g., "greasers in leather jackets and girls in poodle skirts", "a lone traveler at the counter", "empty, no people")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --style raw`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "1950s Americana vintage photography, Kodachrome" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (modern cars, smartphones, contemporary clothes, grim, dirty, cyberpunk)

## Notes
- For an eerie "liminal space" vibe, remove the people and set it at night: "empty diner at 3 AM, Edward Hopper Nighthawks vibe, lonely."
