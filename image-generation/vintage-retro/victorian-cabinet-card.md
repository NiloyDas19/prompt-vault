## Purpose
Generate authentic-looking 19th-century Victorian cabinet card or tintype photography with period-accurate styling and photographic artifacts.

## Prompt
Authentic 1890s Victorian {PHOTO_TYPE} portrait of {SUBJECT}, wearing {CLOTHING_STYLE}, posing {POSE_STYLE} in a vintage photography studio, {STUDIO_PROPS} in the background, {EXPRESSION} expression typical of long-exposure early photography, {LIGHTING_STYLE} harsh early flash powder or natural studio lighting, {PHOTO_DEFECTS} photographic artifacts and aging, sepia tone, antique historical photograph aesthetic

## Variables
- {PHOTO_TYPE} – Format (e.g., "cabinet card", "tintype", "daguerreotype", "albumen print")
- {SUBJECT} – Who (e.g., "a stern bearded gentleman", "a family with three children", "a pair of sisters")
- {CLOTHING_STYLE} – Clothes (e.g., "a high-collared lace dress and cameo brooch", "a three-piece tweed suit and bowler hat")
- {POSE_STYLE} – Pose (e.g., "stiffly seated in an ornate chair", "standing with one hand resting on a pedestal")
- {STUDIO_PROPS} – Props (e.g., "a painted romantic landscape backdrop", "heavy velvet drapes and a taxidermy bird")
- {EXPRESSION} – Face (e.g., "unsmiling and formal", "a rare subtle smile", "hauntingly blank staring past the lens")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft diffused skylight", "harsh high-contrast lighting")
- {PHOTO_DEFECTS} – Damage (e.g., "scratched emulsion, faded edges, and chemical stains", "silver mirroring on the edges", "near pristine")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "authentic 19th-century historical photograph, not an illustration" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (color, modern clothes, smiling, digital, sharp, contemporary)

## Notes
- People in the 1800s rarely smiled in photos due to long exposure times. Specify "unsmiling, stoic, long-exposure stillness" for authenticity.
