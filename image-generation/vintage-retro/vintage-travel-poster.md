## Purpose
Generate a vintage-style travel poster in the classic WPA or golden age of travel illustration style.

## Prompt
Vintage {ERA} era travel poster for {DESTINATION}, featuring {ICONIC_LANDMARK_OR_SCENE} as the central illustration in {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} style, {COLOR_REDUCTION} limited color palette using {SPECIFIC_COLORS}, {PERSPECTIVE_TYPE} perspective emphasizing the {DESTINATION_QUALITY}, {SKY_TREATMENT} stylized sky treatment, bold {TYPOGRAPHY_POSITION} text area suggesting "{DESTINATION}" as the headline, {BORDER_TREATMENT} decorative border, {AIRLINE_OR_AGENCY} branding element, flat simplified illustration with {DETAIL_LEVEL} of detail, vintage ink printing aesthetic, inspiring wanderlust

## Variables
- {ERA} – Style period (e.g., "1930s WPA", "1950s airline", "1960s mod", "1920s Belle Epoque")
- {DESTINATION} – Where (e.g., "Kyoto, Japan", "Rio de Janeiro", "Iceland", "Egyptian Pyramids")
- {ICONIC_LANDMARK_OR_SCENE} – What's shown (e.g., "Mount Fuji with cherry blossoms", "the Northern Lights over glaciers")
- {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "bold flat graphic", "painterly realist", "geometric abstract", "silhouette")
- {COLOR_REDUCTION} – Palette limit (e.g., "3-color", "5-color", "full rich color")
- {SPECIFIC_COLORS} – Colors (e.g., "deep teal, ivory, and burnt orange", "navy, red, and cream")
- {PERSPECTIVE_TYPE} – View (e.g., "dramatic low-angle looking up", "classic straight-on", "bird's-eye aerial")
- {DESTINATION_QUALITY} – What makes it special (e.g., "grandeur and scale", "exotic mysticism", "natural wonder")
- {SKY_TREATMENT} – Sky (e.g., "stepped gradient bands", "simple solid color block", "stylized cloud shapes")
- {TYPOGRAPHY_POSITION} – Text area (e.g., "top third", "bottom banner", "centered overlay")
- {BORDER_TREATMENT} – Border (e.g., "Art Deco geometric border", "no border full bleed", "thin simple frame")
- {AIRLINE_OR_AGENCY} – Fake brand (e.g., "Air Moderne, Paris", "Pacific Express")
- {DETAIL_LEVEL} – Detail (e.g., "high", "medium", "minimal graphic")

## Modifiers
