## Purpose
Generate antique scientific botanical or biological illustrations, perfect for wall art or vintage design.

## Prompt
Vintage 18th-century scientific botanical illustration of {PLANT_OR_SUBJECT}, {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} style on {PAPER_TYPE}, showing {ANATOMICAL_DETAILS}, {COLOR_TREATMENT} coloring, {LAYOUT_STYLE} layout, elegant {CALLIGRAPHY} calligraphy labels, highly detailed, Ernst Haeckel / Pierre-Joseph Redouté influence, natural history museum archive aesthetic

## Variables
- {PLANT_OR_SUBJECT} – What (e.g., "a rare Orchid species", "a deadly nightshade plant", "a collection of bioluminescent fungi", "a cross-section of a nautilus shell")
- {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "stipple engraving", "watercolor and ink wash", "lithograph print")
- {PAPER_TYPE} – Paper (e.g., "aged parchment", "foxed and yellowed vellum", "antique cream rag paper")
- {ANATOMICAL_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "the full plant, root system, seeds, and cross-section of the flower", "various stages of the blooming lifecycle")
- {COLOR_TREATMENT} – Color (e.g., "delicate hand-tinted watercolors", "monochromatic black ink", "faded earthy pigments")
- {LAYOUT_STYLE} – Layout (e.g., "arranged symmetrically", "scattered scientific diagram", "single elegant central stem")
- {CALLIGRAPHY} – Text (e.g., "faded Latin copperplate script", "spidery handwritten notes in brown ink")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "antique botanical scientific illustration, vintage parchment" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (photography, 3D, modern, bright white paper, messy, cartoon)

## Notes
- To create a fantasy version, use real-sounding descriptions for fake plants: "vintage botanical illustration of a glowing Mandrake root, fantasy flora."
