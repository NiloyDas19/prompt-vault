## Purpose
Generate raw, authentic 1990s grunge, alternative, or lo-fi photography resembling disposable cameras or early digital.

## Prompt
Raw 1990s {CAMERA_TYPE} photograph of {SUBJECT}, {CLOTHING_STYLE} 90s alternative fashion, {LOCATION_SETTING} setting, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting, {FILM_AESTHETIC} lo-fi film aesthetic, {COLOR_CAST} color cast, candid documentary feel, grunge aesthetic, youth culture, unpolished and authentic, flash reflection, 1994 zine photography style

## Variables
- {CAMERA_TYPE} – Camera (e.g., "disposable camera flash", "Polaroid 600", "cheap point-and-shoot film")
- {SUBJECT} – Who (e.g., "a teenage garage band practicing", "friends hanging out on a ratty couch", "a skateboarder")
- {CLOTHING_STYLE} – Clothes (e.g., "flannel shirts, ripped denim, and combat boots", "oversized graphic tees and chain wallets", "slip dresses over baby tees")
- {LOCATION_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a messy teenager's bedroom with band posters", "a dimly lit dive bar", "a suburban parking lot at night")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "harsh direct on-camera flash creating red-eye", "dim moody ambient light")
- {FILM_AESTHETIC} – Film look (e.g., "heavy grain, slight blur, light leaks", "overexposed washed-out film")
- {COLOR_CAST} – Colors (e.g., "green fluorescent cast", "warm sickly yellow", "high contrast muted colors")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:3 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 50`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "1990s candid disposable camera photo" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Use disposable camera LoRA. Negative: (professional, studio lighting, bokeh, sharp, modern fashion)

## Notes
- To emphasize the era, include specific 90s details: "CDs scattered on the floor, a chunky CRT television, a landline phone."
