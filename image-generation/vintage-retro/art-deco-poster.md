## Purpose
Generate a stunning Art Deco style poster with geometric elegance, bold typography feel, and the glamour of the 1920s-30s.

## Prompt
Authentic Art Deco style poster featuring {POSTER_SUBJECT}, {GEOMETRIC_FRAME} geometric border and architectural framing, {SYMMETRY_TYPE} symmetrical composition, {STYLIZED_FIGURE} stylized figure or subject rendered in Art Deco fashion, {COLOR_SCHEME} color scheme using {SPECIFIC_COLORS} with {METALLIC_ACCENT} metallic accent, {SUNBURST_OR_RADIAL} radiating or sunburst background element, {DECORATIVE_MOTIFS} decorative motifs (zigzags, chevrons, lotus, sunrise), bold {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} typography aesthetic, {DESTINATION_OR_EVENT} travel poster or event feel, 1925-1935 aesthetic, Cassandre or Erte artistic influence

## Variables
- {POSTER_SUBJECT} – Subject (e.g., "an ocean liner", "a jazz club", "a glamorous city skyline")
- {GEOMETRIC_FRAME} – Frame (e.g., "stepped ziggurat", "angular fan", "chevron border")
- {SYMMETRY_TYPE} – Symmetry (e.g., "perfect bilateral", "near-symmetrical with focal point")
- {STYLIZED_FIGURE} – Figure (e.g., "a stylized flapper dancer", "a streamlined locomotive", "no figure, object only")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "black, cream, and gold", "navy, crimson, and silver")
- {SPECIFIC_COLORS} – Exact palette
- {METALLIC_ACCENT} – Metal (e.g., "gold leaf", "silver", "bronze", "copper")
- {SUNBURST_OR_RADIAL} – Background element (e.g., "golden sunburst rays", "diagonal speed lines")
- {DECORATIVE_MOTIFS} – Ornaments to include
- {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} – Font feel (e.g., "bold slab serif", "thin elegant sans-serif", "stylized letterforms")
- {DESTINATION_OR_EVENT} – Purpose (e.g., "transatlantic cruise advertisement", "New Year's Eve gala")
