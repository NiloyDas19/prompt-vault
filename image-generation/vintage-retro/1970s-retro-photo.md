## Purpose
Generate an authentically styled photograph that looks like it was shot and developed in the 1970s with period-accurate colors, grain, and subject matter.

## Prompt
Authentic 1970s era photograph of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} in {SETTING}, shot on {FILM_CAMERA} film camera with {FILM_TYPE} film stock, {WARM_TONE} warm color cast with {FADED_QUALITY} faded saturation, visible film grain, {SEVENTIES_COLORS} era-specific color palette, {CLOTHING_AND_HAIRSTYLE} period-accurate clothing and hairstyles, {LIGHT_TYPE} available light, {PHYSICAL_DEFECTS} period-authentic photo imperfections, found photograph aesthetic, {OCCASION} moment captured, nostalgia-inducing, Kodachrome color science

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Who/what (e.g., "a family at a backyard barbecue", "teenagers at a roller skating rink")
- {SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a wood-paneled suburban kitchen", "a sun-drenched California beach")
- {FILM_CAMERA} – Camera (e.g., "Kodak Instamatic", "Polaroid SX-70", "Canon AE-1")
- {FILM_TYPE} – Film (e.g., "Kodachrome 64", "Ektachrome", "Tri-X black and white")
- {WARM_TONE} – Color cast (e.g., "warm amber", "peachy skin tones", "yellow-green cast from tungsten")
- {FADED_QUALITY} – Fade (e.g., "slightly faded and washed", "heavily faded like old found photo")
- {SEVENTIES_COLORS} – Era colors (e.g., "harvest gold, avocado green, burnt orange", "psychedelic pattern fabrics")
- {CLOTHING_AND_HAIRSTYLE} – Fashion (e.g., "flared denim, paisley print, feathered Farrah Fawcett hair")
- {LIGHT_TYPE} – Light (e.g., "harsh direct flash", "warm tungsten lamp", "harsh midday sun")
- {PHYSICAL_DEFECTS} – Imperfections (e.g., "slight vignette, red eye, light leak on edge", "film scratches")
- {OCCASION} – Moment (e.g., "Christmas morning", "a summer vacation", "a birthday party")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6.1`
**DALL-E 3:** Explicitly write "this is a real photograph taken in the 1970s" to trigger period accuracy
**Stable Diffusion:** Use analog-photo LoRA. Negative: (digital, modern, sharp, contemporary clothing, HDR, clean)

## Notes
- For Polaroid: change aspect ratio to 1:1, add "square Polaroid border, underexposed slightly, polaroid color cast"
- For black and white: "black and white 1970s newspaper photography, high grain, documentary style"
