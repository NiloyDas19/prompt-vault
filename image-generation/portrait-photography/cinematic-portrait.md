## Purpose
Generate a dramatic, cinematic-quality portrait that looks like a still from a prestige film.

## Prompt
Cinematic film still portrait of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, {EMOTION_OR_EXPRESSION}, shot on 85mm lens at f/1.8, shallow depth of field, soft natural window light with a hint of {ACCENT_LIGHT_COLOR} rim light, {COLOR_GRADE} color grading, film grain, hyper-detailed skin texture, bokeh background of {BACKGROUND_SETTING}, professional photography, editorial quality

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Who is in the portrait (e.g., "a weathered fisherman in his 60s", "a young woman with freckles and auburn hair")
- {EMOTION_OR_EXPRESSION} – Their expression/emotion (e.g., "gazing wistfully into the distance", "mid-laugh with eyes crinkling")
- {ACCENT_LIGHT_COLOR} – Rim light color (e.g., golden, blue, warm orange, cool white)
- {COLOR_GRADE} – Color grading style (e.g., teal and orange, muted desaturated, warm golden)
- {BACKGROUND_SETTING} – What's blurred in the background (e.g., city lights, lush forest, dark studio)

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 150
**DALL-E 3:** Works as written; add "Ultra-realistic, professional quality" at the start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (deformed face, bad anatomy, plastic skin, blurry, low quality, watermark, text)

## Notes
- For dramatic mood: replace window light with "dramatic Rembrandt lighting"
- For fashion editorial: add "Vogue magazine cover style, perfect makeup, fashion editorial"
- For environmental portraits: replace bokeh background with a sharp background of a specific location
