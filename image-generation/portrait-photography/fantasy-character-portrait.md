## Purpose
Generate a richly detailed fantasy character portrait suitable for book covers, game art, or concept design.

## Prompt
Epic fantasy character portrait of {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION}, {ATTIRE_AND_ARMOR} with intricate hand-painted details, {LIGHTING} dramatic fantasy lighting casting {LIGHT_COLOR} tones, highly detailed face with {DISTINCTIVE_FEATURE}, painterly art style blending realism and fantasy, {ARTIST_STYLE} artistic influence, richly textured background of {BACKGROUND_LORE}, digital painting, 8K resolution, stunning composition, award-winning concept art

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION} – Who the character is (e.g., "a fierce elven warrior queen", "an ancient weathered wizard with piercing blue eyes")
- {ATTIRE_AND_ARMOR} – Their clothing/armor (e.g., "ornate golden plate armor", "tattered dark robes with glowing runes")
- {LIGHTING} – Lighting type (e.g., "ethereal backlit", "firelight", "magical bioluminescent")
- {LIGHT_COLOR} – Light color cast (e.g., emerald green, deep purple, warm gold, electric blue)
- {DISTINCTIVE_FEATURE} – A defining facial detail (e.g., "a scar across the left cheek", "glowing silver eyes", "intricate face paint")
- {ARTIST_STYLE} – Art style reference (e.g., "Greg Rutkowski", "Artgerm", "WLOP", "Craig Mullins")
- {BACKGROUND_LORE} – Background setting (e.g., "a crumbling ancient castle", "an enchanted misty forest", "a celestial sky realm")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 2:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 400
**DALL-E 3:** Add "Digital fantasy art, epic quality" at the beginning
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (modern clothing, photo, ugly, deformed, bad anatomy, blurry)

## Notes
- For a softer style: add "oil painting, Pre-Raphaelite influence, soft brush strokes"
- For a darker character: add "gothic, ominous, menacing aura, dark fantasy"
