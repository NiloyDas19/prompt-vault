## Purpose
Generate a dramatic, shadow-heavy noir portrait evoking mystery, tension, and classic film noir aesthetics.

## Prompt
Film noir portrait of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, deep chiaroscuro lighting with {SHADOW_PLAY} dramatic shadows, a single {LIGHT_SOURCE} cutting through the darkness illuminating part of the face, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, wisps of smoke or mist, {SETTING} setting visible through venetian blind shadows or a rain-streaked window, high contrast black and white or {DESATURATED_COLOR} desaturated tones, cinematic composition, 1940s noir aesthetic, shot on 35mm film, tension and mystery

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Character (e.g., "a femme fatale with scarlet lips", "a weathered private detective in a fedora")
- {SHADOW_PLAY} – Shadow detail (e.g., "venetian blind stripe patterns", "harsh side-lit", "half face in complete darkness")
- {LIGHT_SOURCE} – What creates the light (e.g., "desk lamp", "neon sign", "street lamp through a window", "match flame")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood descriptor (e.g., "tense and paranoid", "melancholy and introspective", "dangerous seduction")
- {SETTING} – Background location (e.g., "a rain-soaked city alley", "a smoky jazz club", "a dimly lit office")
- {DESATURATED_COLOR} – Optional color version (e.g., "cold blue", "warm amber", "deep green tint")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 2:3 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 250
**DALL-E 3:** Add "classic film noir style, 1940s aesthetic" at the start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (bright, colorful, happy, daylight, modern, cheerful)

## Notes
- For color noir: add "neo-noir neon-lit cityscape, vibrant colors contrasting deep shadows, Blade Runner influence"
- For graphic novel look: add "graphic novel panels, ink and shadow, Frank Miller Sin City aesthetic"
