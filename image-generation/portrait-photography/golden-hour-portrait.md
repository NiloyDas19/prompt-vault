## Purpose
Generate a warm, glowing portrait taken outdoors during the magical golden hour just after sunrise or before sunset.

## Prompt
{SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} photographed during golden hour, warm amber and honey sunlight backlighting the subject creating a glowing halo effect, long shadows stretching across {ENVIRONMENT}, lens flare kissing the edge of frame, shot on 50mm lens, f/2.0, skin tones glowing warm amber, dust particles visible in the light, National Geographic photography style, {MOOD} mood, highly detailed, photorealistic

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Subject description (e.g., "A shepherd standing on a hillside", "A child running through a wheat field")
- {ENVIRONMENT} – The setting (e.g., "golden wheat fields", "desert dunes", "a rural village path")
- {MOOD} – The overall mood (e.g., nostalgic, joyful, contemplative, serene)

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6.1
**DALL-E 3:** Add "photographed at golden hour, warm light" to reinforce timing
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (blue tones, cold light, artificial light, overexposed, flat lighting)

## Notes
- For a cinematic widescreen feel: change aspect ratio to 16:9 and add "anamorphic lens, epic landscape vista"
- For lifestyle photography: add "candid moment, authentic expression, lifestyle photography brand campaign"
