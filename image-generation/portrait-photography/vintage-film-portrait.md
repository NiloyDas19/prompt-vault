## Purpose
Generate a portrait that authentically replicates the look and feel of classic film photography from a specific decade.

## Prompt
{ERA} era portrait photograph of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, authentic {FILM_STOCK} film stock emulation, {ERA_SPECIFIC_COLORS} color palette, slight grain and film imperfections, {ERA_SPECIFIC_CLOTHING} clothing and hairstyle true to the period, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting, analog photography quality, archival print feel, found photograph aesthetic, historical authenticity

## Variables
- {ERA} – Time period (e.g., "1940s noir", "1960s mod", "1970s psychedelic", "1980s neon", "1950s Americana")
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Who (e.g., "a glamorous Hollywood starlet", "a factory worker on lunch break", "teenagers at a drive-in")
- {FILM_STOCK} – Film stock (e.g., "Kodak Portra 400", "Fuji Velvia", "Kodachrome 64", "Ilford HP5 black and white")
- {ERA_SPECIFIC_COLORS} – Color palette (e.g., "warm sepia and amber", "faded pastels", "rich saturated Kodachrome colors")
- {ERA_SPECIFIC_CLOTHING} – Fashion of the era
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light quality (e.g., "natural available light", "harsh flashbulb", "soft studio tungsten")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 3:4 --style raw --v 6.1
**DALL-E 3:** Begin with "Authentic vintage photograph," and specify the decade explicitly
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (digital, modern, sharp, oversaturated, HDR, contemporary)

## Notes
- For photobooth aesthetic: add "3:4 portrait, photobooth strip, 1960s style, sequential poses"
- For magazine cover: add "LIFE magazine cover, period-accurate typography, retro layout"
