## Purpose
Generate a warm, authentic lifestyle portrait of a family or child that feels genuine and emotionally resonant.

## Prompt
Authentic lifestyle photography of {SUBJECTS} in {NATURAL_SETTING}, {GENUINE_MOMENT} candid genuine moment, warm natural light — {LIGHT_QUALITY}, colors are {COLOR_PALETTE} and soft, environment is {SURROUNDINGS}, photographed with a 50mm lens f/2.8, slight warm grade, documentary family photography style, no posing or stiffness, emotionally resonant, professional lifestyle brand photography quality

## Variables
- {SUBJECTS} – Who's in the photo (e.g., "a mother and toddler", "three siblings of different ages", "a family of four")
- {NATURAL_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "their kitchen making breakfast", "a backyard during a summer afternoon", "a cozy living room on a rainy day")
- {GENUINE_MOMENT} – What's happening (e.g., "laughing together over a shared joke", "a child hugging a parent from behind", "reading books together on the floor")
- {LIGHT_QUALITY} – Light description (e.g., "morning window light", "dappled afternoon shade", "warm evening sunlight through open doors")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "warm earth tones", "muted pastels", "natural neutrals")
- {SURROUNDINGS} – Background details (e.g., "slightly cluttered and lived-in", "minimal and clean", "lush with plants and books")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6.1
**DALL-E 3:** Works well as written; avoid over-specifying to preserve authenticity
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (posed, stiff, studio, artificial, fake smile, plastic)

## Notes
- For holiday greeting cards: add "festive winter setting, warm Christmas tree lights, holiday aesthetic"
- For brand campaigns: add "lifestyle brand photography, aspirational but authentic, {BRAND_STYLE} aesthetic"
