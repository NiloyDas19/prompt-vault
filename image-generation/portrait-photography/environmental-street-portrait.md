## Purpose
Generate a documentary-style portrait of a person within their natural urban or street environment.

## Prompt
Documentary street portrait of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} in {LOCATION}, candid authentic expression, available light photography, {TIME_OF_DAY} light, environmental storytelling, shot on 35mm lens f/2.8, slight grain, gritty urban atmosphere, the background in sharp enough focus to read the environment, honest and unposed, photojournalism style, award-winning documentary photography

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Who (e.g., "an elderly street vendor selling flowers", "a young skateboarder with paint-stained hands")
- {LOCATION} – Where (e.g., "a busy New York subway platform", "a narrow alley in Istanbul", "a colorful market in Marrakech")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Light quality (e.g., "overcast midday", "rainy afternoon", "blue hour twilight", "harsh noon")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 3:4 --style raw --v 6.1
**DALL-E 3:** Add "documentary photography style, authentic" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (staged, artificial, studio light, smooth, plastic)

## Notes
- Inspired by Vivian Maier: add "Vivian Maier street photography style, black and white, reflective surfaces"
- For color documentary: add "Magnum Photos style, rich saturated colors, decisive moment"
