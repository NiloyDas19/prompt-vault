## Purpose

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Subject (e.g., "a bald man with a fierce gaze", "a tattooed woman with bold makeup")
- {LIGHTING_SETUP} – Lighting technique (e.g., "Rembrandt", "butterfly", "split", "loop", "flat beauty")
