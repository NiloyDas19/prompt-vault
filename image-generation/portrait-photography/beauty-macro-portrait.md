## Purpose
Generate a close-up beauty or fashion editorial portrait with perfect studio lighting and high-end aesthetics.

## Prompt
High fashion beauty editorial portrait, extreme close-up of {SUBJECT_FEATURES}, flawless skin with visible pores and micro-texture, {MAKEUP_STYLE} makeup, {HAIR_STYLE} hair, {KEY_LIGHT} studio lighting, catchlights in eyes, sharp focus on eyes and lips, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, Vogue or Harper's Bazaar editorial quality, beauty advertising photography, shallow depth of field, ultra high resolution medium format camera

## Variables
- {SUBJECT_FEATURES} – Face description (e.g., "a woman with high cheekbones and full lips", "a model with striking amber eyes")
- {MAKEUP_STYLE} – Makeup look (e.g., "bold red lip and cat eye", "dewy no-makeup makeup", "avant-garde geometric eye art", "glittery holographic")
- {HAIR_STYLE} – Hair (e.g., "sleek platinum blunt bob", "wild natural afro", "wet look slicked back")
- {KEY_LIGHT} – Main light (e.g., "large softbox beauty dish", "ring light", "natural window", "high-contrast Rembrandt")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Dominant colors (e.g., "warm golds and bronzes", "cool icy blues and silvers", "jewel tones")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 300
**DALL-E 3:** Add "beauty editorial photography, professional quality" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (bad teeth, ugly, deformed eyes, blurry, low quality, plastic skin)

## Notes
- For skincare campaign: add "glass skin, luminous, hydrated, skincare advertisement"
- For avant-garde: add "experimental, Alexander McQueen aesthetic, otherworldly, artistic"
