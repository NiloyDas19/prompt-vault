## Purpose
Generate atmospheric environment concept art for games, films, or world-building — establishing the look and feel of a location.

## Prompt
Environment concept art for a {GENRE} {ENVIRONMENT_TYPE}, {ESTABLISHING_SHOT_DESCRIPTION}, {TIME_OF_DAY_LIGHTING} lighting casting {SHADOW_DIRECTION} shadows across the scene, {ARCHITECTURAL_OR_NATURAL_FEATURES} dominating the composition, {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} filling the air, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette establishing the world's visual identity, {HUMAN_SCALE_ELEMENT} providing scale, painterly digital art style, {MOOD} mood, {INSPIRED_BY} visual influence, suitable for game engine environment reference, professional entertainment art

## Variables
- {GENRE} – Setting genre (e.g., "post-apocalyptic sci-fi", "high fantasy", "dark gothic horror", "cozy cottagecore")
- {ENVIRONMENT_TYPE} – Location type (e.g., "ancient ruined city", "alien jungle world", "underground cavern civilization", "floating sky islands")
- {ESTABLISHING_SHOT_DESCRIPTION} – Composition (e.g., "wide establishing shot showing the full cityscape", "dramatic low-angle looking up at the towering gate")
- {TIME_OF_DAY_LIGHTING} – Light source (e.g., "twin suns at midday", "moonlit night", "perpetual twilight", "bioluminescent night")
- {SHADOW_DIRECTION} – Shadow quality (e.g., "long dramatic", "dappled forest", "harsh noon", "soft diffused")
- {ARCHITECTURAL_OR_NATURAL_FEATURES} – Main visual elements (e.g., "crumbling gothic spires", "enormous mushroom trees", "crystal formations", "ancient stone terraces")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_EFFECT} – Air quality (e.g., "thick mist", "floating spores", "smoke and embers", "shimmering heat", "snow flurries")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "muted grey and rust", "vibrant teal and gold", "deep purple and electric blue")
- {HUMAN_SCALE_ELEMENT} – Scale reference (e.g., "a tiny figure on a path", "a small boat", "a lantern in a doorway")
- {MOOD} – Atmosphere (e.g., "ominous and foreboding", "wondrous and inviting", "desolate and lonely", "epic and grand")
- {INSPIRED_BY} – Reference (e.g., "Dark Souls", "Studio Ghibli", "Horizon Zero Dawn", "Disco Elysium")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 450
**DALL-E 3:** Add "environment concept art, entertainment design quality"
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (portrait, character only, modern photography, realistic photo, low quality)

## Notes
- For isometric view: add "isometric perspective, game map view, top-down 45-degree angle"
- For key art: add "key art piece, dramatic hero composition, marketing image quality"
- 16:9 aspect ratio matches standard game engine viewport and presentation slides
