## Purpose
Generate visually striking surrealist compositions that merge the impossible with photorealistic rendering.

## Prompt
Hyperrealistic surrealist composition featuring {IMPOSSIBLE_SCENARIO}, seamlessly blending {ELEMENT_A} with {ELEMENT_B} in {PHYSICALLY_IMPOSSIBLE_WAY}, {SCALE_DISTORTION} scale distortion creating {SCALE_EFFECT}, {LIGHTING_REALITY} realistic lighting that makes the impossible feel tangible, {DREAMLIKE_DETAIL} dreamlike detail hidden in {LOCATION_IN_IMAGE}, {COLOR_SURREAL} color palette straddling reality and dream, inspired by {SURREALIST_REFERENCE}, photorealistic rendering making the impossible look undeniably real, thought-provoking and visually unforgettable

## Variables
- {IMPOSSIBLE_SCENARIO} – The central impossible concept (e.g., "a giant clock melting over a desert rock formation", "a doorway opening from a city street directly into a starfield")
- {ELEMENT_A} – First element to combine (e.g., "ocean waves", "a Victorian house", "a human eye")
- {ELEMENT_B} – Second element (e.g., "storm clouds", "tree roots", "galaxies")
- {PHYSICALLY_IMPOSSIBLE_WAY} – How they merge (e.g., "pouring upward into the sky", "growing through each other", "becoming one seamless object")
- {SCALE_DISTORTION} – Size manipulation (e.g., "a mountain-sized butterfly", "ant-sized skyscrapers", "a hand-sized planet")
- {SCALE_EFFECT} – What the scale does (e.g., "overwhelming and disorienting wonder", "intimate cosmic beauty", "disturbing uncanny valley")
- {LIGHTING_REALITY} – Light quality (e.g., "golden hour physically correct", "impossible multiple simultaneous suns", "light from an unseen source")
- {DREAMLIKE_DETAIL} – Hidden element (e.g., "tiny figures in the distance", "an impossible staircase", "text that can't quite be read")
- {LOCATION_IN_IMAGE} – Where the detail is (e.g., "in the bottom corner", "reflected in a window", "barely visible in the shadows")
- {COLOR_SURREAL} – Colors (e.g., "hyper-saturated impossible colors", "muted dream-like pastels", "monochrome with one vivid accent")
- {SURREALIST_REFERENCE} – Artist influence (e.g., "Salvador Dali", "René Magritte", "Giorgio de Chirico", "Zdzisław Beksiński")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 4:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 750 --weird 1000
**DALL-E 3:** Add "surrealist photorealistic composition, impossible dream" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (simple, obvious, expected, mundane, unimaginative, low quality)

## Notes
- For Magritte bowler hat style: add "René Magritte style, bowler hat, everyday objects made strange, Belgian surrealism"
- For Beksiński horror surrealism: add "dark dystopian dreamscape, decaying architecture, lonely figure, oppressive atmosphere"
- Midjourney's `--weird` parameter (up to 3000) is ideal for pushing surrealist unpredictability
