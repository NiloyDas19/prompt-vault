## Purpose
Generate a beautiful watercolor or gouache-style illustration with authentic paint textures and soft, luminous color blending.

## Prompt
{ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} watercolor illustration of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, {COLOR_HARMONY} color harmony with {DOMINANT_COLORS} dominant colors and {ACCENT_COLORS} accents, {PAPER_TEXTURE} paper texture visible, wet-on-wet blending creating {BLEEDING_EFFECT} soft edges where colors meet, {DRY_BRUSH_DETAIL} dry brush details on {TEXTURED_ELEMENTS}, {COMPOSITION_STYLE} composition, {MOOD_QUALITY} mood, {WHITE_SPACE} negative white space, hand-painted illustration, {BOOK_OR_PRINT_STYLE} quality, authentic media simulation

## Variables
- {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "loose expressive", "tight detailed", "spontaneous gestural", "precise botanical")
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – What's illustrated (e.g., "a fox sleeping under a mushroom", "a coastal village in rain", "a girl reading in a window")
- {COLOR_HARMONY} – Color relationship (e.g., "analogous warm", "complementary", "monochromatic blue", "split-complementary")
- {DOMINANT_COLORS} – Main colors (e.g., "soft terracotta and sage green", "cerulean blue and ivory")
- {ACCENT_COLORS} – Pop colors (e.g., "coral orange", "gold yellow", "crimson red")
- {PAPER_TEXTURE} – Paper (e.g., "rough cold-press", "smooth hot-press", "aged yellowed")
- {BLEEDING_EFFECT} – Paint bleed quality (e.g., "organic blooming", "controlled minimal", "dramatic fluid")
- {DRY_BRUSH_DETAIL} – Brush texture (e.g., "prominent", "subtle", "heavy texture")
- {TEXTURED_ELEMENTS} – What gets texture (e.g., "fur, foliage, fabric", "bark, stone, water")
- {COMPOSITION_STYLE} – Layout (e.g., "centered vignette", "full bleed", "spot illustration on white")
- {MOOD_QUALITY} – Feeling (e.g., "whimsical and gentle", "contemplative and quiet", "joyful and vibrant")
- {WHITE_SPACE} – Negative space use (e.g., "generous white breathing room", "minimal, full of life", "balanced")
- {BOOK_OR_PRINT_STYLE} – Usage (e.g., "children's picture book", "editorial illustration", "greeting card", "fine art print")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 4:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 600
**DALL-E 3:** Add "traditional watercolor painting, hand-painted" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (digital look, sharp lines, photorealistic, 3D render, vector)

## Notes
- For children's book: add "charming, accessible, bright colors, child-friendly, Beatrix Potter influence"
- For botanical illustration: add "scientific botanical art, accurate plant detail, field guide quality"
- Higher stylize value (600) in Midjourney encourages more painterly, less literal interpretations
