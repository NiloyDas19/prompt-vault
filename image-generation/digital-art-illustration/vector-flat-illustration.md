## Purpose
Generate a clean, modern flat or vector-style illustration suitable for UI, marketing, editorial, or brand assets.

## Prompt
{ILLUSTRATION_TYPE} flat vector illustration of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, {STYLE_VARIANT} design style, {COLOR_PALETTE_TYPE} color palette using {SPECIFIC_COLORS} colors, {LINE_STYLE} outlines (if any), {SHAPE_COMPLEXITY} geometric shapes, {SHADOW_TREATMENT} shadow treatment, {TEXTURE_LEVEL} texture level, clean and scalable, {USE_CASE} usage style, {CULTURAL_INFLUENCE} cultural influence, professional graphic design quality, suitable for print and digital

## Variables
- {ILLUSTRATION_TYPE} – Type (e.g., "editorial", "spot", "full scene", "icon set", "infographic")
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – What to illustrate (e.g., "a person working from home with a cat", "a city park scene", "abstract data visualization")
- {STYLE_VARIANT} – Style (e.g., "geometric minimalist", "bold graphic", "isometric", "organic shape", "paper cutout")
- {COLOR_PALETTE_TYPE} – Palette type (e.g., "limited 3-color", "vibrant full", "muted pastel", "high contrast duotone")
- {SPECIFIC_COLORS} – Colors (e.g., "coral, teal, and cream", "midnight blue, gold, and white", "terracotta and sage")
- {LINE_STYLE} – Outlines (e.g., "thick bold outlines", "no outlines", "hairline subtle outlines")
- {SHAPE_COMPLEXITY} – Shape style (e.g., "simple rounded", "complex geometric", "organic irregular")
- {SHADOW_TREATMENT} – Shadows (e.g., "long flat shadows", "no shadows", "subtle soft drop shadow", "cut-out layered shadows")
- {TEXTURE_LEVEL} – Texture (e.g., "completely flat no texture", "subtle grain overlay", "paper texture")
- {USE_CASE} – Purpose (e.g., "tech startup website hero", "magazine editorial", "children's educational", "brand identity")
- {CULTURAL_INFLUENCE} – Inspiration (e.g., "Scandinavian minimalism", "Japanese graphic design", "Latin American bold color", "neutral global")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 4:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 300 --no shadows, gradients, photorealism
**DALL-E 3:** Add "flat vector illustration, graphic design style" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (photorealistic, 3D, shadows, gradients unless specified, complex textures)

## Notes
- For icon set: add "icon set of 6 related icons in consistent style, white background, equally sized"
- For infographic: add "data visualization, charts and graphs, information design, clear hierarchy"
- Use Midjourney's `--no` flag to explicitly suppress unwanted elements like gradients and shadows
