## Purpose
Generate an image that authentically replicates the look and texture of a classical oil painting — from Old Masters to Impressionism.

## Prompt
{PAINTING_ERA} oil painting style depiction of {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION}, {TECHNIQUE} brushwork with visible {PAINT_TEXTURE} paint texture, {LIGHTING_STYLE} Baroque/Old Master lighting, {WARM_COOL_BALANCE} warm-cool color balance, {GLAZING_EFFECT} layered glazing depth, painted on {CANVAS_OR_PANEL} canvas, dark {BACKGROUND_TREATMENT} background treatment, {COMPOSITION_STYLE} classical composition, {ARTIST_REFERENCE} artistic influence, museum quality fine art, signed in the lower right corner in period-appropriate script

## Variables
- {PAINTING_ERA} – Period (e.g., "Dutch Golden Age", "Italian Renaissance", "French Impressionist", "Spanish Baroque", "Victorian academic")
- {SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION} – Subject (e.g., "a merchant at his writing desk surrounded by exotic goods", "a pastoral harvest scene", "a woman reading by candlelight")
- {TECHNIQUE} – Brushwork (e.g., "smooth sfumato", "broken impressionistic", "tight realistic", "expressive alla prima")
- {PAINT_TEXTURE} – Texture (e.g., "impasto thickness", "smooth enamel-like", "visible bristle marks")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "single candle chiaroscuro", "soft diffused northern window", "dramatic Caravaggio", "dappled plein air")
- {WARM_COOL_BALANCE} – Tones (e.g., "warm amber shadows, cool highlight", "cool grey shadows, warm sunlit areas")
- {GLAZING_EFFECT} – Depth (e.g., "rich deep", "luminous translucent", "dark velvety")
- {CANVAS_OR_PANEL} – Support (e.g., "linen canvas", "oak panel", "copper")
- {BACKGROUND_TREATMENT} – Background (e.g., "dark almost black", "subtle landscape", "curtained interior", "classical column")
- {COMPOSITION_STYLE} – Layout (e.g., "pyramidal", "triangular golden ratio", "diagonal", "symmetrical")
- {ARTIST_REFERENCE} – Inspiration (e.g., "Rembrandt van Rijn", "Johannes Vermeer", "John Singer Sargent", "Claude Monet")
