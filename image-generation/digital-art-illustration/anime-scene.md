## Purpose
Generate a high-quality anime-style illustration with detailed character art and atmospheric backgrounds.

## Prompt
{ANIME_STYLE} anime illustration of {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION} {ACTION_POSE} in {SETTING}, {TIME_AND_WEATHER} atmosphere, {COLOR_MOOD} color palette with {ACCENT_COLORS} accent colors, {DETAIL_LEVEL} background detail, expressive anime eyes, dynamic {HAIR_ELEMENT} hair movement, {ART_QUALITY} quality linework, {INSPIRATION} artistic influence, vibrant and emotional, masterpiece illustration quality

## Variables
- {ANIME_STYLE} – Style variant (e.g., "Studio Ghibli inspired soft watercolor", "sharp modern shonen", "90s retro cel-shaded", "painterly seinen")
- {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION} – Character details (e.g., "a silver-haired girl in a sailor uniform", "a battle-scarred warrior with a red cloak")
- {ACTION_POSE} – What they're doing (e.g., "standing at the edge of a cliff looking into the distance", "running through cherry blossoms")
- {SETTING} – Where (e.g., "a sunlit rural Japanese village", "a futuristic neon city", "a mystical floating island")
- {TIME_AND_WEATHER} – Atmosphere (e.g., "golden summer afternoon", "rainy autumn night", "cherry blossom spring")
- {COLOR_MOOD} – Color feeling (e.g., "warm and nostalgic", "cool and melancholy", "vibrant and energetic")
- {ACCENT_COLORS} – Key colors (e.g., "sakura pink and soft blue", "electric cyan and deep purple")
- {DETAIL_LEVEL} – Background (e.g., "highly detailed", "painterly impressionistic", "simple gradient")
- {ART_QUALITY} – Line quality (e.g., "clean", "sketchy", "bold")
- {HAIR_ELEMENT} – Hair descriptor used in the prompt
- {INSPIRATION} – Reference (e.g., "Makoto Shinkai", "Yoshitaka Amano", "CLAMP", "Ilya Kuvshinov")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** --ar 2:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 500 --niji 6
**DALL-E 3:** Add "anime art style, high quality illustration"
**Stable Diffusion:** Model: anything-v5 or counterfeit. Negative: (western cartoon, 3d render, photorealistic, ugly, bad anatomy)

## Notes
- For chibi: add "chibi style, cute proportions, large head, small body, adorable"
- For dark fantasy anime: "dark fantasy manga style, Berserk-inspired, detailed hatching, intense drama"
- 🎨 Midjourney's `--niji 6` flag is specifically tuned for anime and illustration styles — use it for best results
