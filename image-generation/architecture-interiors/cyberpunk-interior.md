## Purpose
Generate gritty, high-tech, neon-lit interior spaces typical of the cyberpunk genre.

## Prompt
Cyberpunk interior design of a {ROOM_TYPE}, cluttered with {TECH_CLUTTER}, illuminated by {NEON_COLORS} neon lights shining through {WINDOW_VIEW}, {FURNITURE_STYLE} furniture with {TECH_INTEGRATION}, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {SURFACE_TEXTURES}, glowing holographic displays, cinematic lighting, Unreal Engine 5 render, Blade Runner aesthetic, highly detailed

## Variables
- {ROOM_TYPE} – Space (e.g., "hacker's tiny apartment", "underground ripperdoc clinic", "high-rise corporate office")
- {TECH_CLUTTER} – Clutter (e.g., "tangled cables, server racks, and empty noodle boxes", "clean minimalist cybernetics")
- {NEON_COLORS} – Neon (e.g., "harsh cyan and magenta", "sickly green and warning red")
- {WINDOW_VIEW} – View (e.g., "rain-streaked windows showing a dense neon city", "blinds casting slatted shadows")
- {FURNITURE_STYLE} – Furniture (e.g., "worn leather and chrome", "futuristic ergonomic sleek")
- {TECH_INTEGRATION} – Integration (e.g., "screens built into every surface", "visible wiring and conduits")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "claustrophobic and gritty", "sterile and corporate", "cozy neon hideout")
- {SURFACE_TEXTURES} – Textures (e.g., "scratched metal and damp concrete", "polished obsidian glass")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 400`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "cyberpunk interior concept art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (daylight, natural, rustic, historical, clean, empty)

## Notes
- Contrast is key in cyberpunk: mix extreme high tech with extreme poverty/grime.
