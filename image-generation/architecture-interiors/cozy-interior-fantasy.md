## Purpose
Generate a warm, inviting fantasy interior space filled with charm, magic, and cozy atmosphere — perfect for book covers, game art, or whimsical illustration.

## Prompt
Warm and inviting illustration of a cozy {FANTASY_SPACE_TYPE} interior, {WARM_LIGHT_SOURCE} casting {WARM_LIGHT_QUALITY} warm {LIGHT_COLOR} light across the space, {ARCHITECTURAL_QUIRK} architectural whimsy, shelves overflowing with {WHIMSICAL_ITEMS}, {COZY_FURNITURE} furniture draped with {TEXTILE_DETAILS}, {MAGICAL_ELEMENT} subtly magical element visible in the scene, {WINDOW_VIEW} through the window, {LIVED_IN_DETAIL} charming lived-in details, {ART_STYLE} illustration style, enchanting cottagecore fantasy aesthetic, Anne of Green Gables meets Studio Ghibli warmth

## Variables
- {FANTASY_SPACE_TYPE} – Space (e.g., "witch's cottage kitchen", "wizard's library tower", "hobbit hole living room")
- {WARM_LIGHT_SOURCE} – Light (e.g., "a crackling stone fireplace", "dozens of floating candles")
- {WARM_LIGHT_QUALITY} – Light quality (e.g., "flickering orange", "soft amber", "golden dancing")
- {LIGHT_COLOR} – Color (e.g., "amber", "honey gold", "rose gold")
- {ARCHITECTURAL_QUIRK} – Unique feature (e.g., "rounded hobbit-hole doors", "a staircase that spirals around a living tree")
- {WHIMSICAL_ITEMS} – Shelf contents (e.g., "potion bottles, spell books, unusual specimens in jars", "jars of preserves, cookbooks, herbs")
- {COZY_FURNITURE} – Furniture (e.g., "a deep wingback armchair", "a low cushioned window seat")
- {TEXTILE_DETAILS} – Fabrics (e.g., "mismatched quilts and knitted blankets", "rich velvet throws")
- {MAGICAL_ELEMENT} – Magic (e.g., "a book opening on its own", "a small glowing sprite")
- {WINDOW_VIEW} – Outside (e.g., "a moonlit enchanted forest", "a mountain valley at sunrise")
- {LIVED_IN_DETAIL} – Charm detail (e.g., "a steaming cup of tea half-drunk", "muddy boots by the door")
- {ART_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "painterly watercolor", "detailed digital illustration", "children's book style")
