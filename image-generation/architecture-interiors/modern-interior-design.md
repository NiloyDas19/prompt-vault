## Purpose
Generate a stunning interior design photography image showcasing a beautifully designed room or living space.

## Prompt
Professional interior design photography of a {ROOM_TYPE} in {DESIGN_STYLE} style, {TIME_OF_DAY} natural light pouring through {WINDOW_TYPE} windows casting {SHADOW_QUALITY} shadows across the space, {MATERIAL_PALETTE} material palette featuring {SPECIFIC_MATERIALS}, {FURNITURE_DESCRIPTION} furniture arrangement, {COLOR_SCHEME} color scheme, {TEXTILE_DETAILS} textiles adding warmth, {PLANT_PRESENCE} greenery present, {DECORATIVE_OBJECTS} as decorative accents, architectural photography, shot with {LENS_TYPE} lens, immaculate staging, {PUBLICATION} editorial quality

## Variables
- {ROOM_TYPE} – Space (e.g., "open-plan living room", "master bedroom", "chef's kitchen")
- {DESIGN_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "Japandi minimalist", "industrial loft", "Scandinavian cozy")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Light timing (e.g., "morning golden", "afternoon diffused", "evening warm lamp")
- {WINDOW_TYPE} – Windows (e.g., "floor-to-ceiling", "arched", "skylight")
- {SHADOW_QUALITY} – Shadow (e.g., "long dramatic diagonal", "soft dappled", "minimal flat")
- {MATERIAL_PALETTE} – Materials (e.g., "concrete, walnut, leather, brass", "linen, oak, marble, rattan")
- {SPECIFIC_MATERIALS} – Key materials
- {FURNITURE_DESCRIPTION} – Furniture (e.g., "a low profile sectional sofa", "a dining table for 8")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "warm neutrals — cream, taupe, caramel", "cool blues and warm wood tones")
- {TEXTILE_DETAILS} – Fabrics (e.g., "thick wool throws", "linen curtains", "Persian rug")
- {PLANT_PRESENCE} – Plants (e.g., "large statement fiddle leaf fig", "abundant plant wall", "no plants")
- {DECORATIVE_OBJECTS} – Decor (e.g., "stack of coffee table books", "sculptural ceramic vases")
- {LENS_TYPE} – Lens (e.g., "24mm wide", "35mm standard", "tilt-shift")
- {PUBLICATION} – Style ref (e.g., "Architectural Digest", "Wallpaper*", "Dezeen")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:3 --style raw --v 6.1`

**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (people, mess, low quality, cluttered, poorly lit, dark)
