## Purpose

## Prompt

## Variables
- {STRUCTURE_TYPE} – Structure (e.g., "single-story pavilion home", "desert oasis residence", "A-frame cabin")
- {ARCHITECT_REFERENCE} – Style (e.g., "Richard Neutra", "Frank Lloyd Wright", "Eichler", "John Lautner")

- {LANDSCAPE_INTEGRATION} – Nature (e.g., "seamlessly integrating with arid desert landscaping", "nestled in lush tropical foliage")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Time (e.g., "twilight blue hour", "harsh midday sun")
- {LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "warm interior lights glowing against the dusk", "sharp geometric shadows")
- {INTERIOR_GLIMPSE} – Interior (e.g., "a sunken conversation pit and vintage Eames chair", "terrazzo floors and walnut panels")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "turquoise, mustard yellow, and warm teak", "stark white, black, and natural greenery")
