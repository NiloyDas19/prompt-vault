## Purpose
Generate optimistic, green, sustainable utopian architecture typical of the solarpunk genre.

## Prompt
Solarpunk architectural concept art of a {CITY_AREA}, featuring {BUILDING_SHAPE} buildings constructed from {SUSTAINABLE_MATERIALS}, integrated seamlessly with {PLANT_INTEGRATION}, {ENERGY_TECH} providing power, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere under a {SKY_CONDITION}, {TRANSPORTATION} moving through the scene, bright and optimistic {COLOR_PALETTE}, high-tech meets nature, highly detailed, architectural rendering, utopian future

## Variables
- {CITY_AREA} – Area (e.g., "sprawling eco-city skyline", "vertical farming residential tower", "community garden plaza")
- {BUILDING_SHAPE} – Shapes (e.g., "organic flowing, biomimetic curves", "geometric terraced", "lotus-inspired")
- {SUSTAINABLE_MATERIALS} – Materials (e.g., "white bioplastics, warm wood, and smart glass", "rammed earth and green steel")
- {PLANT_INTEGRATION} – Nature (e.g., "massive vertical forests and cascading vines", "rooftop gardens and hydroponic trees")
- {ENERGY_TECH} – Power (e.g., "translucent solar panels and sleek wind turbines", "glowing algae bioreactors")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "peaceful, clean, and vibrant", "bustling, optimistic, and bright")
- {SKY_CONDITION} – Sky (e.g., "crystal clear blue sky with fluffy clouds", "golden hour sunset")
- {TRANSPORTATION} – Movement (e.g., "silent magnetic gliders", "pedestrians and bicycles on skybridges")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "vibrant greens, clean whites, and warm woods", "pastel sunset colors with emerald foliage")

## Notes
- Solarpunk is the opposite of cyberpunk: focus on sunlight, plants, community, and clean technology.
