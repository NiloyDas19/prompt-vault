## Purpose
Generate breathtaking fantasy architecture — impossible structures, magical cities, and otherworldly built environments.

## Prompt
Epic fantasy architecture concept art of {FANTASY_STRUCTURE}, {IMPOSSIBLE_ENGINEERING_FEATURE} defying conventional physics, built from {FANTASY_MATERIALS} with intricate {DECORATIVE_STYLE} ornamentation, {WORLD_ENVIRONMENT} surrounding environment, {MAGICAL_LIGHT_SOURCE} illuminating the scene in {LIGHT_COLOR} light, {SCALE_INDICATOR} providing epic scale, {ATMOSPHERIC_MAGIC} magical atmosphere, {CIVILIZATION_STYLE} cultural aesthetic, epic establishing shot composition, painterly digital concept art, {REFERENCE_GAMES_OR_FILM} visual inspiration, awe-inspiring grandeur

## Variables
- {FANTASY_STRUCTURE} – What was built (e.g., "a city built on the inside of a hollow mountain", "a dwarf fortress carved into a living volcano")
- {IMPOSSIBLE_ENGINEERING_FEATURE} – Physics-defying element (e.g., "suspended bridges with no visible support", "entire districts floating on nothing")
- {FANTASY_MATERIALS} – Materials (e.g., "living wood woven with moonsilver", "crystallized starlight and ancient stone")
- {DECORATIVE_STYLE} – Ornamentation (e.g., "Celtic knotwork", "Arabic geometric", "Gothic flying buttresses")
- {WORLD_ENVIRONMENT} – Surroundings (e.g., "amidst a thundercloud", "on a sea of clouds", "in a deep underground cavern")
- {MAGICAL_LIGHT_SOURCE} – Light source (e.g., "a captured sun orb", "bioluminescent crystal veins")
- {LIGHT_COLOR} – Light color (e.g., "warm amber", "cool silver-blue", "emerald green")
- {SCALE_INDICATOR} – Scale reference (e.g., "tiny figures on bridges", "dragons circling the highest spire")
- {ATMOSPHERIC_MAGIC} – Magic in air (e.g., "floating arcane sigils", "golden mana particles", "clouds of colored smoke")
- {CIVILIZATION_STYLE} – Culture (e.g., "ancient Egyptian influence", "Norse Viking brutalism", "Aztec terraced grandeur")
- {REFERENCE_GAMES_OR_FILM} – Reference (e.g., "Minas Tirith from Lord of the Rings", "Kaer Morhen from The Witcher")
