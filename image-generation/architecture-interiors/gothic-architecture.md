## Purpose
Generate striking, detailed images of Gothic and neo-Gothic architectural structures with dramatic lighting.

## Prompt
Extremely detailed architectural photography of a {GOTHIC_STRUCTURE} in authentic Gothic style, featuring {GOTHIC_ELEMENTS}, {TIME_OF_DAY} with {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {LIGHTING_STYLE} highlighting the intricate stonework and {WINDOW_TYPE} windows, {CAMERA_ANGLE} emphasizing the towering verticality, {COLOR_TONE} tones, historical architectural render, masterpiece of masonry, photorealistic, 8k resolution

## Variables
- {GOTHIC_STRUCTURE} – Structure (e.g., "massive cathedral", "vampire castle", "ancient university library")
- {GOTHIC_ELEMENTS} – Elements (e.g., "flying buttresses and gargoyles", "intricate ribbed vaults and pointed arches")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Time (e.g., "midnight under a full moon", "misty dawn", "stormy afternoon")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "eerie and foreboding", "sacred and quiet", "melancholy")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dramatic chiaroscuro", "moonlight casting long shadows", "warm candlelight")
- {WINDOW_TYPE} – Windows (e.g., "elaborate stained glass rose", "tall lancet")
- {CAMERA_ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "low angle looking up at the spires", "interior looking down the nave")
- {COLOR_TONE} – Colors (e.g., "cool blue and grey", "monochromatic silver", "warm sepia")
