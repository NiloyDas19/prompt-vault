## Purpose
Generate a stunning exterior architectural photograph of a building or structure.

## Prompt
Professional architectural photography of {BUILDING_DESCRIPTION} in {ARCHITECTURAL_STYLE} style, {TIME_OF_DAY} light creating {LIGHT_EFFECT} across the {FACADE_MATERIAL} facade, {LANDSCAPE_CONTEXT} surrounding landscape, {SKY_CONDITION} sky above, {PERSPECTIVE_TYPE} perspective from {VANTAGE_POINT}, {REFLECTIVE_SURFACES} catching and reflecting the environment, {HUMAN_SCALE} providing human scale, {SYMMETRY_ASYMMETRY} composition, architectural photography shot on a {LENS} lens, Architectural Digest or Dezeen quality, photorealistic, award-winning

## Variables
- {BUILDING_DESCRIPTION} – Building (e.g., "a cantilevered glass and steel private residence", "a brutalist government building")
- {ARCHITECTURAL_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "Zaha Hadid parametric", "Tadao Ando minimalist concrete", "Frank Lloyd Wright organic")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – When (e.g., "golden hour", "blue hour twilight with interior lights on")
- {LIGHT_EFFECT} – Light effect (e.g., "warm glow", "cool shadows", "dramatic striped shadows from louvers")
- {FACADE_MATERIAL} – Material (e.g., "raw exposed concrete", "weathered corten steel", "glass curtain wall")
- {LANDSCAPE_CONTEXT} – Setting (e.g., "desert landscape", "dense urban street", "lakeside surrounded by pine trees")
- {SKY_CONDITION} – Sky (e.g., "dramatic cloud formation", "clear blue", "overcast grey")
- {PERSPECTIVE_TYPE} – View (e.g., "straight-on elevation", "dramatic low angle worm's eye", "3/4 corner view")
- {VANTAGE_POINT} – Where from (e.g., "street level", "opposite rooftop", "aerial 30m")
- {REFLECTIVE_SURFACES} – Reflections (e.g., "infinity pool", "wet ground", "glass facade")
- {HUMAN_SCALE} – Scale (e.g., "a lone figure in the entrance", "no people", "silhouetted figures")
- {SYMMETRY_ASYMMETRY} – Composition (e.g., "perfect bilateral symmetry", "dynamic asymmetric")
- {LENS} – Focal length (e.g., "24mm wide", "50mm", "17mm ultra-wide")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "architectural photography, professional quality" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (people unless specified, power lines, cars unless atmospheric, cluttered)

## Notes
- For heritage/classical: add "Victorian grandeur, ornate stone facade, classical columns, period-accurate"
- For futuristic: add "speculative future architecture, biophilic design, living walls, solar panels integrated"
