## Purpose
Generate hauntingly beautiful images of abandoned buildings being slowly reclaimed by nature.

## Prompt
Urbex photography of an abandoned {BUILDING_TYPE}, {DECAY_LEVEL} state of decay, nature reclaiming the structure with {PLANT_LIFE}, {LIGHT_SOURCE} shining through {ROOF_CONDITION}, highlighting the {REMAINING_DETAILS} left behind, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere, {COLOR_TONE} tones with vibrant green overgrowth, melancholic beauty, highly detailed architectural ruin, photorealistic

## Variables
- {BUILDING_TYPE} – Building (e.g., "grand Victorian greenhouse", "Soviet-era factory", "luxury hotel lobby")
- {DECAY_LEVEL} – Decay (e.g., "decades of complete neglect", "recently abandoned", "crumbling into dust")
- {PLANT_LIFE} – Overgrowth (e.g., "thick ivy, moss, and ferns", "trees growing through the floorboards")
- {LIGHT_SOURCE} – Light (e.g., "shafts of dusty sunlight", "eerie moonlight", "overcast soft light")
- {ROOF_CONDITION} – Roof (e.g., "a collapsed glass roof", "holes in the ceiling", "shattered skylights")
- {REMAINING_DETAILS} – Remnants (e.g., "a decaying grand piano", "peeling wallpaper and rusted machinery", "faded frescoes")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "peaceful and silent", "eerie and haunted", "sad and nostalgic")
- {COLOR_TONE} – Colors (e.g., "desaturated cool tones with bright moss", "warm golden decay")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:2 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "abandoned building photography, nature reclaiming" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (clean, new, maintained, people, bright, pristine)

## Notes
- Use for post-apocalyptic settings without the sci-fi elements (purely contemporary ruins).
