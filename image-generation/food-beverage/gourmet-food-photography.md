## Purpose
Generate stunning professional food photography with appetizing styling and perfect lighting.

## Prompt
Professional food photography of {DISH_DESCRIPTION}, {PLATING_STYLE} plating on {PLATE_OR_VESSEL} vessel, {GARNISH_AND_PROPS} garnishes and styling props, {LIGHTING_TYPE} food photography lighting creating {SHADOW_QUALITY} shadows and {HIGHLIGHT_EFFECT} highlights on the food, {BACKGROUND_SURFACE} background and surface, {TEXTURE_EMPHASIS} textures prominently featured, {ANGLE} shooting angle, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette making the food look {APPETITE_QUALITY}, steam rising from {STEAM_ELEMENT}, shallow depth of field with sharp focus on {FOCAL_POINT}, editorial food photography quality, {PUBLICATION} magazine aesthetic

## Variables
- {DISH_DESCRIPTION} – The food (e.g., "a perfectly seared dry-aged ribeye with herb butter", "a deconstructed lemon tart with edible flowers")
- {PLATING_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "artistic fine dining", "rustic and abundant", "minimalist Japanese")
- {PLATE_OR_VESSEL} – Container (e.g., "a slate grey ceramic plate", "a cast iron skillet", "a white bone china bowl")
- {GARNISH_AND_PROPS} – Styling (e.g., "fresh herb sprigs, cracked pepper, a swirl of cream")
- {LIGHTING_TYPE} – Light (e.g., "soft diffused window light from the left", "dramatic side lighting")
- {SHADOW_QUALITY} – Shadows (e.g., "soft and natural", "dramatic and moody")
- {HIGHLIGHT_EFFECT} – Highlights (e.g., "glossy specular highlights on sauces", "steam-lit glow")
- {BACKGROUND_SURFACE} – Setting (e.g., "dark slate and linen napkin", "warm wood and marble")
- {TEXTURE_EMPHASIS} – Textures (e.g., "crispy skin contrast with soft interior", "glistening sauce over rough bread")
- {ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "45-degree hero shot", "overhead flat lay", "straight-on at plate level")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "warm earth tones", "cool minimalist")
- {APPETITE_QUALITY} – Appeal (e.g., "irresistibly appetizing", "rich and indulgent")
- {STEAM_ELEMENT} – Steam from what (e.g., "the broth", "the meat", "no steam, served cold")
- {FOCAL_POINT} – Sharp focus on (e.g., "the main protein", "the garnish", "the sauce drizzle")
- {PUBLICATION} – Style ref (e.g., "Bon Appétit", "Kinfolk", "Saveur")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "professional food photography, appetizing" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (unappetizing, cold food look, raw when should be cooked, bad plating)

## Notes
- For drink photography: adapt for "a craft cocktail, condensation on glass, garnish floating, moody bar atmosphere"
- For recipe content: add "ingredients arranged around the dish, recipe book editorial style, overhead"
