## Purpose
Generate bright, vibrant, and appetizing imagery of healthy food, salads, and fresh produce.

## Prompt
Bright and fresh food photography of a {SALAD_TYPE} in a {BOWL_TYPE}, loaded with {KEY_INGREDIENTS}, {DRESSING_ACTION} dressing glistening on the leaves, {COLORS} vibrant color palette emphasizing freshness, {LIGHTING_STYLE} bright natural lighting, {BACKGROUND_PROPS} surrounding the bowl, shot from a {CAMERA_ANGLE}, healthy lifestyle aesthetic, highly appetizing, crisp and sharp details

## Variables
- {SALAD_TYPE} – Dish (e.g., "vibrant Mediterranean salad", "grain bowl", "summer fruit salad")
- {BOWL_TYPE} – Vessel (e.g., "wide shallow ceramic bowl", "large wooden salad bowl")
- {KEY_INGREDIENTS} – Contents (e.g., "crisp greens, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and crumbled feta", "quinoa, roasted chickpeas, and kale")
- {DRESSING_ACTION} – Moisture (e.g., "a light drizzle of olive oil", "lemon vinaigrette")
- {COLORS} – Color mood (e.g., "emerald greens and bright reds", "rainbow colors")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "clean high-key white", "soft dappled sunlight")
- {BACKGROUND_PROPS} – Props (e.g., "a halved lemon, fresh herbs, and a cruet of oil", "clean white marble, minimalist")
- {CAMERA_ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "overhead flat lay", "45-degree angle")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "healthy food photography, fresh ingredients" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (wilted, brown, unappetizing, dark, moody, cooked to mush)

## Notes
- The key to healthy food photography is bright, clean light. Avoid moody/dark lighting unless specifically aiming for a rustic farm-to-table look.
