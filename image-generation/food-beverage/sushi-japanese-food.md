## Purpose
Generate precise, artistic imagery of high-end sushi and Japanese cuisine.

## Prompt
High-end Japanese culinary photography of {SUSHI_TYPE}, delicately plated on {SERVING_WARE}, featuring {FISH_DETAILS} fresh fish with perfect texture, garnished with {GARNISHES}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting highlighting the gloss of the fish and individual grains of rice, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} background, {ACTION_ELEMENT} dynamic element, Zen minimalist aesthetic, Michelin star presentation, extreme sharp focus

## Variables
- {SUSHI_TYPE} – What (e.g., "an omakase nigiri selection", "a premium tuna sashimi platter", "an elaborate dragon roll")
- {SERVING_WARE} – Plate (e.g., "a dark slate slab", "a traditional wooden sushi boat", "minimalist white ceramic")
- {FISH_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "glistening fatty tuna (otoro) with marbling", "translucent white fish")
- {GARNISHES} – Garnish (e.g., "a dab of fresh wasabi, shiso leaf, and gold leaf", "microgreens and ikura (salmon roe)")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "dramatic moody directional", "clean, even, bright studio")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "dark blurred sushi counter", "clean neutral grey", "bamboo mat texture")
- {ACTION_ELEMENT} – Action (e.g., "chopsticks gently lifting a piece", "soy sauce being brushed on by a chef", "perfectly still")
