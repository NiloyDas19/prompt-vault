## Purpose
Generate mouth-watering, indulgent images of cakes, pastries, and sweet desserts.

## Prompt
Indulgent food photography of a {DESSERT_TYPE}, featuring {MAIN_TEXTURES} textures, dripping with {SAUCE_OR_SYRUP}, garnished with {TOPPINGS}, presented on {PLATE_STYLE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting that highlights the {HIGHLIGHT_FEATURE}, {BACKGROUND_PROPS} in the background, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette, incredibly appetizing, editorial pastry magazine quality, close-up macro details

## Variables
- {DESSERT_TYPE} – Dessert (e.g., "multi-layered dark chocolate cake", "delicate French macarons", "a towering ice cream sundae")
- {MAIN_TEXTURES} – Textures (e.g., "rich velvety frosting and moist crumb", "crispy flaky pastry and smooth cream")
- {SAUCE_OR_SYRUP} – Drip (e.g., "warm caramel sauce", "glossy chocolate ganache", "berry coulis")
- {TOPPINGS} – Garnish (e.g., "fresh raspberries and gold leaf", "crushed toasted hazelnuts", "a dusting of cocoa powder")
- {PLATE_STYLE} – Plate (e.g., "an ornate vintage gold-rimmed plate", "a modern matte pastel dish")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft romantic window light", "bright high-contrast pop")
- {HIGHLIGHT_FEATURE} – Focus (e.g., "the glossy shine of the chocolate", "the flaky layers of the croissant")
- {BACKGROUND_PROPS} – Props (e.g., "a silver dessert fork and a cup of tea", "scattered ingredients like vanilla beans")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "rich dark browns and gold", "pastel pinks and mint green")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "decadent dessert photography, appetizing" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (diet, healthy, unappetizing, flat, dry, messy plating)

## Notes
- Adding "a slice taken out, showing the beautiful cross-section layers" is excellent for cakes and pies.
