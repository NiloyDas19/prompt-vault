## Purpose
Generate beautiful, artistic beverage photography with dramatic lighting and expert styling.

## Prompt
Artisan beverage photography of {DRINK_DESCRIPTION} in {GLASSWARE} glassware, {GARNISH_DETAILS} garnish, {CONDENSATION_OR_FROST} condensation or frost on the glass surface, {LIGHTING_SETUP} lighting creating {LIGHT_THROUGH_GLASS} translucency through the liquid, {LIQUID_COLOR} colored liquid catching the light, {BACKGROUND_SETTING} background, {SURFACE} surface it rests on, {PROPS} atmospheric props in the scene, {ICE_ELEMENT} ice element visible, shot at {HEIGHT_PERSPECTIVE} perspective, commercial beverage photography, {BRAND_STYLE} brand aesthetic, perfectly styled

## Variables
- {DRINK_DESCRIPTION} – What the drink is (e.g., "a smoky mezcal Old Fashioned", "an azure butterfly pea flower gin tonic")
- {GLASSWARE} – Glass type (e.g., "a wide crystal rocks glass", "a tall slim highball", "a vintage coupe")
- {GARNISH_DETAILS} – Garnish (e.g., "a dehydrated citrus wheel and rosemary sprig", "edible flower floating on the surface")
- {CONDENSATION_OR_FROST} – Moisture (e.g., "heavy cold condensation droplets", "frosty misted glass", "no condensation")
- {LIGHTING_SETUP} – Lights (e.g., "backlit with warm amber", "dramatic side lit", "soft diffused overhead")
- {LIGHT_THROUGH_GLASS} – Glass light (e.g., "glowing from within", "jewel-like refraction")
- {LIQUID_COLOR} – Drink color (e.g., "deep amber", "electric cobalt blue", "ruby red")
- {BACKGROUND_SETTING} – Background (e.g., "moody dark bar atmosphere", "bright airy summer terrace")
- {SURFACE} – What it sits on (e.g., "dark marble", "a cork coaster on aged oak")
- {PROPS} – Props (e.g., "a cocktail jigger and fresh herbs", "minimal, glass only")
- {ICE_ELEMENT} – Ice (e.g., "a large single crystal clear ice sphere", "crushed pebble ice", "no ice")
- {HEIGHT_PERSPECTIVE} – Angle (e.g., "eye-level with the glass", "slight above-level", "overhead flat lay")
- {BRAND_STYLE} – Brand feel (e.g., "premium spirits luxury", "craft cocktail bar", "wellness tonic")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "professional beverage photography, commercial quality" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (plastic glass, poor lighting, spilled, flat, dull color)

## Notes
- For wine: emphasize "pour in motion, arc of wine into glass, deep red color, sommelier setting"
- For non-alcoholic: add "health and wellness aesthetic, bright and fresh, botanical styling"
