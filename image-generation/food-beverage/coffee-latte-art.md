## Purpose
Generate beautiful, cozy cafe imagery focusing on perfectly poured coffee and latte art.

## Prompt
Close-up food photography of {COFFEE_TYPE} in a {CUP_TYPE}, featuring perfect {LATTE_ART_DESIGN} latte art on top of rich crema, resting on a {TABLE_SURFACE}, {ACTION_ELEMENT} happening in the frame, {LIGHTING} warm morning sunlight casting long shadows, {BACKGROUND_BLUR} blurred in the background, {ATMOSPHERE} cozy cafe atmosphere, highly detailed, Instagram aesthetic

## Variables
- {COFFEE_TYPE} – Drink (e.g., "a flat white", "a dark espresso shot", "a matcha latte")
- {CUP_TYPE} – Vessel (e.g., "a ceramic matte black cup", "a thick diner mug", "a clear double-walled glass")
- {LATTE_ART_DESIGN} – Art (e.g., "rosetta pattern", "swan design", "tulip stack", "no latte art, just rich crema")
- {TABLE_SURFACE} – Table (e.g., "a rustic wooden cafe table", "white marble counter")
- {ACTION_ELEMENT} – Action (e.g., "milk being poured from a pitcher", "steam rising", "a hand holding a spoon")
- {LIGHTING} – Light (e.g., "soft golden hour", "moody rainy day diffused light")
- {BACKGROUND_BLUR} – Bokeh (e.g., "a blurred barista working", "rainy window and city street", "pastries in a display case")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "warm and inviting", "sleek and modern third-wave coffee")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "coffee photography, cafe aesthetic" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (spilled, messy, bad latte art, distorted cup, cold)

## Notes
- For cold brew: change to "iced coffee, condensation on glass, swirling milk and coffee mixing, bright summer light"
