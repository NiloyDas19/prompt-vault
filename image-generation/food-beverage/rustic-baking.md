## Purpose
Generate warm, appetizing imagery of freshly baked bread, pastries, and rustic baked goods.

## Prompt
Food photography of {BAKED_GOODS}, resting on a {SURFACE}, dusted with {DUSTING}, {TEXTURE_DETAIL} crust and {INTERIOR_DETAIL} interior visible, surrounded by {BAKING_INGREDIENTS} ingredients, {LIGHTING_STYLE} natural window light illuminating the steam rising from the hot bread, {WARMTH_LEVEL} warm and cozy atmosphere, {PROP_STYLING} styling, shot with 50mm lens, shallow depth of field, rustic farmhouse aesthetic, highly appetizing

## Variables
- {BAKED_GOODS} – What (e.g., "a crusty artisanal sourdough loaf", "warm cinnamon rolls", "a rustic galette")
- {SURFACE} – Setting (e.g., "a floured wooden cutting board", "a cooling rack over parchment paper")
- {DUSTING} – Topping (e.g., "powdered sugar", "white flour", "coarse sea salt")
- {TEXTURE_DETAIL} – Outside (e.g., "blistered crispy", "flaky golden brown")
- {INTERIOR_DETAIL} – Inside (e.g., "soft airy crumb", "gooey chocolate")
- {BAKING_INGREDIENTS} – Props (e.g., "fresh wheat stalks and a vintage flour scoop", "butter and jam")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "morning", "soft diffused", "dramatic directional")
- {WARMTH_LEVEL} – Mood (e.g., "extremely cozy and inviting", "bright and fresh")
- {PROP_STYLING} – Props (e.g., "a rumpled linen napkin and a bread knife", "minimalist modern ceramic plate")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "rustic food photography, warm appetizing" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (burnt, unappetizing, raw dough, modern kitchen, clinical)

## Notes
- Emphasize "steam rising" to ensure the food looks freshly baked and hot.
