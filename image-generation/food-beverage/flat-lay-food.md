## Purpose
Generate a beautifully styled overhead flat lay food scene for social media, editorial, or recipe content.

## Prompt
Top-down overhead flat lay food photography of {SCENE_CONCEPT}, {COLOR_SCHEME} color scheme creating a cohesive visual story, all elements arranged in {COMPOSITION_STYLE} composition on {SURFACE_TYPE} surface, {HERO_ELEMENT} as the hero dish surrounded by {SUPPORTING_ELEMENTS}, {TEXTURE_VARIETY} texture variety in the arrangement, {LINEN_OR_TEXTILE} textile adding softness, {UTENSILS} styled utensils placed naturally, {FRESH_INGREDIENTS} fresh ingredients scattered artfully, natural {LIGHT_DIRECTION} diffused overhead light, Instagram-worthy food styling, {STYLE_REF} editorial aesthetic, shot directly overhead, every element perfectly placed

## Variables
- {SCENE_CONCEPT} – What the scene is (e.g., "a full Italian pasta dinner spread", "a baker's prep table with croissants and coffee")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "warm earth tones and cream", "fresh green and white")
- {COMPOSITION_STYLE} – Layout (e.g., "radial with hero in center", "organic scattered", "grid-structured")
- {SURFACE_TYPE} – Background (e.g., "aged linen tablecloth", "white marble", "dark slate")
- {HERO_ELEMENT} – Main dish (e.g., "a bowl of pappardelle with truffle", "a fresh baked sourdough loaf")
- {SUPPORTING_ELEMENTS} – Surrounding items (e.g., "coffee cups, fresh flowers, scattered nuts and herbs")
- {TEXTURE_VARIETY} – Textures (e.g., "smooth ceramic, rough bread crust, silky sauce", "glossy and matte contrast")
- {LINEN_OR_TEXTILE} – Fabric (e.g., "a rumpled linen napkin", "a striped cotton cloth", "no textile")
- {UTENSILS} – Cutlery (e.g., "vintage silver fork and knife", "rustic wooden spoon")
- {FRESH_INGREDIENTS} – Raw ingredients (e.g., "cherry tomatoes, basil leaves, cracked pepper", "fresh berries")
- {LIGHT_DIRECTION} – Light from (e.g., "top-left", "centered", "slightly angled")
- {STYLE_REF} – Reference (e.g., "Kinfolk", "Bon Appétit", "Pinterest aesthetic")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6.1`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "overhead flat lay food photography" at start; be very specific about what's included
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (angled shot, side view, empty table, dirty, cluttered without style)
