## Purpose
Generate irresistible, hyper-stylized commercial fast food photography, perfect for advertisements.

## Prompt
Commercial food advertising photography of a massive, juicy {BURGER_TYPE}, {PATTY_DETAILS} patty with {MELTED_CHEESE} melting cheese, stacked perfectly with {TOPPINGS}, resting on a {BUN_TYPE} bun, {SAUCE_DETAILS} sauce dripping slightly, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} background, {LIGHTING_STYLE} studio lighting making every ingredient glisten, {CONDIMENT_SPLASH} dynamic element, hyper-detailed, incredibly appetizing, McDonalds / Shake Shack advertising aesthetic

## Variables
- {BURGER_TYPE} – What (e.g., "double smash burger", "crispy fried chicken sandwich", "classic cheeseburger")
- {PATTY_DETAILS} – Meat (e.g., "perfectly seared crusty", "thick juicy medium-rare")
- {MELTED_CHEESE} – Cheese (e.g., "gooey American", "sharp cheddar", "blue cheese crumbles")
- {TOPPINGS} – Veggies/Add-ons (e.g., "crisp iceberg lettuce, bright red tomatoes, and pickles", "crispy bacon and onion rings")
- {BUN_TYPE} – Bun (e.g., "toasted brioche", "sesame seed", "pretzel")
- {SAUCE_DETAILS} – Sauce (e.g., "special burger sauce", "smoky BBQ")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – BG (e.g., "solid vibrant yellow", "moody dark slate", "blurred restaurant interior")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "bright high-key", "dramatic spotlight")
- {CONDIMENT_SPLASH} – Motion (e.g., "a dynamic splash of ketchup in the background", "no motion, perfectly still")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:3 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "commercial fast food advertising photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (messy, gross, unappetizing, flat, dry, sad, wilted lettuce)

## Notes
- Fast food advertising usually features ingredients stacked with impossible perfection. Use "impossibly perfect stacking" to enhance this.
