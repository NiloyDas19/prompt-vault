## Purpose
Generate professional studio product photography with perfect lighting, materials, and composition for e-commerce, advertising, or brand campaigns.

## Prompt
Professional product photography of {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION}, placed on {SURFACE_MATERIAL} surface with {SURFACE_TEXTURE} texture, {LIGHTING_SETUP} studio lighting creating {SHADOW_TYPE} shadows, {BACKGROUND_TYPE} background in {BACKGROUND_COLOR}, {PROP_ELEMENTS} complementary props adding context and lifestyle feel, {ANGLE} camera angle, shot on {LENS} lens with {DEPTH_OF_FIELD} depth of field, {COLOR_GRADE} color grading, commercial photography quality, {BRAND_TIER} brand tier aesthetics, sharp product focus, zero distortion

## Variables
- {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION} – Product (e.g., "a matte black luxury perfume bottle with gold cap", "a artisan ceramic coffee mug", "a minimalist smartwatch")
- {SURFACE_MATERIAL} – Surface (e.g., "honed white marble", "weathered oak wood", "brushed concrete", "frosted glass", "black acrylic")
- {SURFACE_TEXTURE} – Texture detail (e.g., "smooth with subtle veining", "rough and tactile", "highly polished reflective")
- {LIGHTING_SETUP} – Lights (e.g., "soft diffused side-lighting from a large softbox", "dramatic hard rim light", "natural window light", "beauty dish overhead")
- {SHADOW_TYPE} – Shadows (e.g., "soft natural drop shadow", "clean mirror reflection below", "dramatic hard directional", "no shadow clean")
- {BACKGROUND_TYPE} – BG style (e.g., "seamless studio", "contextual lifestyle setting", "abstract blurred", "gradient")
- {BACKGROUND_COLOR} – Color (e.g., "pure white", "off-white cream", "deep charcoal", "sage green")
- {PROP_ELEMENTS} – Props (e.g., "scattered dried botanicals and pebbles", "a linen cloth fold and leather journal", "no props, product only")
- {ANGLE} – View (e.g., "45-degree hero shot", "straight-on flat lay from above", "close-up 3/4 view")
- {LENS} – Lens (e.g., "85mm macro", "50mm standard", "90mm tilt-shift")
- {DEPTH_OF_FIELD} – Focus (e.g., "sharp throughout", "shallow with soft bokeh on props", "medium")
- {COLOR_GRADE} – Grade (e.g., "clean neutral", "warm lifestyle", "cool minimal", "rich saturated")
- {BRAND_TIER} – Quality level (e.g., "luxury premium", "mass market accessible", "artisan indie", "clinical pharmaceutical")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "professional product photography, studio quality" at start; describe the product in maximum detail
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (bad lighting, shadows on product face, reflections obscuring product, blurry product, distorted shape)

## Notes
- For flat lay: change angle to "directly overhead flat lay" and add "perfectly arranged, top-down composition, editorial styling"
- For Amazon listing: add "pure white background, no props, multiple angles suggested, e-commerce standard"
- For lifestyle campaign: add "model's hand interacting with product, aspirational lifestyle context, brand campaign quality"
