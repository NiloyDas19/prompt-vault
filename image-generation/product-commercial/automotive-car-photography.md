## Purpose
Generate sleek, high-end automotive commercial photography suitable for car advertisements and magazines.

## Prompt
High-end automotive commercial photography of a {CAR_DESCRIPTION}, parked {LOCATION_SETTING}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} highlighting the {PAINT_FINISH} paint, {TIME_OF_DAY}, {CAMERA_ANGLE}, {REFLECTION_DETAILS} reflecting on the bodywork, cinematic atmosphere, sharp focus on the {FOCAL_POINT}, Top Gear magazine aesthetic, photorealistic 8k

## Variables
- {CAR_DESCRIPTION} – Car (e.g., "sleek matte black supercar", "vintage 1960s muscle car", "futuristic electric SUV")
- {LOCATION_SETTING} – Where (e.g., "on a rain-slicked neon city street", "in a stark white minimalist studio", "on a winding mountain road")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "massive overhead softbox lighting", "dramatic low-angle sunset rim lighting")
- {PAINT_FINISH} – Paint (e.g., "glossy metallic", "satin matte", "pearlescent")
- {TIME_OF_DAY} – Time (e.g., "blue hour", "midnight", "golden hour")
- {CAMERA_ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "low aggressive 3/4 front angle", "profile side shot tracking motion", "detail shot of the grille")
- {REFLECTION_DETAILS} – Reflections (e.g., "neon lights", "the sunset sky", "clean studio lines")
- {FOCAL_POINT} – Focus (e.g., "the headlights", "the alloy wheels", "the entire vehicle profile")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "commercial automotive photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (distorted wheels, asymmetrical headlights, bad reflections, traffic, people)

## Notes
- For motion shots: add "panning shot, motion blur on background, wheels spinning, dynamic speed."
