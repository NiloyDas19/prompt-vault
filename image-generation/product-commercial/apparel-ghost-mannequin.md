## Purpose
Generate clean, e-commerce style clothing photography where the garment appears to be worn by an invisible person (ghost mannequin effect).

## Prompt
E-commerce product photography of {GARMENT_DESCRIPTION}, shot using the ghost mannequin technique so the clothing appears filled out by an invisible body, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} pure background, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting highlighting the {FABRIC_TEXTURE} fabric texture, {FIT_STYLE} fit, perfectly steamed and wrinkle-free, fashion catalog aesthetic, highly detailed

## Variables
- {GARMENT_DESCRIPTION} – Garment (e.g., "a structured wool winter coat", "a flowing silk summer dress", "a heavy knit cardigan")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "pure white #FFFFFF", "soft light grey studio")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "even softbox studio lighting", "slight directional lighting for 3D depth")
- {FABRIC_TEXTURE} – Fabric (e.g., "woven wool", "smooth silk", "ribbed cotton")
- {FIT_STYLE} – Fit (e.g., "tailored and sharp", "draped and relaxed", "puffer volume")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "e-commerce ghost mannequin apparel photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (people, faces, hangers, mannequins, hands, messy background)

## Notes
- Ghost mannequin is notoriously hard for AI without a person popping in. If a person appears, reinforce the negative prompts with "invisible person, no face, no hands, empty clothes."
