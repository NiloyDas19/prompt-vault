## Purpose
Generate sparkling, ultra-detailed macro photography for high-end jewelry and watches.

## Prompt
Luxury jewelry macro photography of {JEWELRY_PIECE}, resting on {DISPLAY_SURFACE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} creating intense sparkle and {LIGHT_CAUSTICS} light caustics on the surface, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} background, razor-thin depth of field focusing perfectly on the {FOCAL_DETAIL}, {COLOR_MOOD} color mood, hyper-detailed commercial product photography, Cartier / Tiffany advertising aesthetic

## Variables
- {JEWELRY_PIECE} – Item (e.g., "a diamond engagement ring in platinum", "an intricate gold chronograph watch", "a sapphire pendant necklace")
- {DISPLAY_SURFACE} – Surface (e.g., "dark reflective obsidian", "soft cream velvet", "rough natural slate")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "hard directional pin-lights", "soft wrap-around studio lighting")
- {LIGHT_CAUSTICS} – Light effect (e.g., "rainbow prismatic dispersion", "sharp starry glints")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "dark moody bokeh", "clean high-key white", "subtle brand color")
- {FOCAL_DETAIL} – Focus (e.g., "the central diamond facets", "the watch dial texture")
- {COLOR_MOOD} – Colors (e.g., "cool icy luxury", "warm romantic gold")
