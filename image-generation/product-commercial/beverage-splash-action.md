## Purpose
Generate dynamic, high-speed photography of liquids splashing around beverage products.

## Prompt
High-speed commercial beverage photography of {DRINK_PRODUCT}, surrounded by an explosive dynamic splash of {LIQUID_TYPE}, {ADDITIONAL_ELEMENTS} suspended mid-air in the splash, {BACKGROUND_STYLE} background, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting freezing the motion perfectly crisp, {COLOR_VIBE} color vibe, refreshing and energetic, advertising campaign aesthetic, hyper-realistic physics

## Variables
- {DRINK_PRODUCT} – Product (e.g., "a sleek silver energy drink can", "a frosted glass bottle of beer", "a minimalist iced tea bottle")
- {LIQUID_TYPE} – Splash (e.g., "crystal clear water", "golden amber liquid", "vibrant crimson juice")
- {ADDITIONAL_ELEMENTS} – Extras (e.g., "shattered ice cubes and fresh mint leaves", "slices of lemon and condensation droplets")
- {BACKGROUND_STYLE} – Background (e.g., "a solid dark background for contrast", "a vibrant matching color backdrop", "an out-of-focus tropical beach")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "bright high-key strobes", "dramatic backlit edge lighting to highlight the water")
- {COLOR_VIBE} – Mood (e.g., "icy cold and refreshing", "dark and intense energy")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "high-speed splash photography, beverage ad" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (blurry, messy, distorted bottle shape, bad liquid physics, messy text)

## Notes
- To make the product look icy cold, add: "heavy condensation on the product, frost, freezing cold aesthetic."
