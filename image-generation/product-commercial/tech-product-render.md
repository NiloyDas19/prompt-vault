## Purpose
Generate a sleek, high-tech product visualization for electronics, apps, or digital products that conveys innovation and premium quality.

## Prompt
Premium tech product visualization of {TECH_PRODUCT}, {RENDERING_STYLE} rendering approach, {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} environment background, {MATERIAL_SPEC} surface materials with {FINISH_TYPE} finish, {GLOW_EFFECT} subtle {GLOW_COLOR} glow emanating from {GLOW_SOURCE}, {REFLECTION_TREATMENT} floor or surface reflections below the product, {FLOATING_ELEMENTS} floating UI elements or data visualization around the product, {LIGHTING_RIG} lighting configuration, {CAMERA_ANGLE} camera angle, {DEPTH_TREATMENT} depth treatment, {COLOR_LANGUAGE} brand color language, Apple or {TECH_BRAND} product photography aesthetic, photorealistic 3D visualization

## Variables
- {TECH_PRODUCT} – Product (e.g., "a sleek pair of over-ear headphones", "a minimalist smartwatch", "a thin laptop")
- {RENDERING_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "photorealistic CGI", "3D render with slight stylization", "real photography + CGI hybrid")
- {BACKGROUND_ENVIRONMENT} – BG (e.g., "pure gradient from dark to light", "abstract geometric environment", "lifestyle setting")
- {MATERIAL_SPEC} – Materials (e.g., "space grey aluminum alloy", "midnight black glossy polymer", "white ceramic")
- {FINISH_TYPE} – Surface (e.g., "brushed matte", "mirror polished", "sand-blasted", "soft-touch rubber coat")
- {GLOW_EFFECT} – Glow type (e.g., "edge-lit", "screen emanating", "internal indicator light", "no glow")
- {GLOW_COLOR} – Glow color (e.g., "cool white", "electric blue", "multicolor RGB")
- {GLOW_SOURCE} – Source (e.g., "the active display", "charging port", "status indicator")
- {REFLECTION_TREATMENT} – Reflection (e.g., "perfect mirror reflection below", "subtle glossy surface", "no reflection clean")
- {FLOATING_ELEMENTS} – UI elements (e.g., "semi-transparent spec callouts", "holographic UI elements", "clean, no UI elements")
- {LIGHTING_RIG} – Lights (e.g., "three-point studio", "dramatic backlit silhouette", "soft 270-degree wrap")
- {CAMERA_ANGLE} – Angle (e.g., "low 3/4 hero shot", "straight-on front elevation", "45-degree overhead")
- {DEPTH_TREATMENT} – Depth (e.g., "sharp throughout", "slight atmospheric haze at edges", "strong depth of field")
- {COLOR_LANGUAGE} – Colors (e.g., "minimalist black and silver", "vibrant consumer-friendly colors", "premium dark and gold")
- {TECH_BRAND} – Style reference (e.g., "Samsung", "Sony", "Bang & Olufsen")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6.1 --stylize 200`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "tech product visualization, premium commercial photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (cheap, cluttered, low-res display, bad reflections, distorted product shape)

## Notes
- For app screenshot mockup: add "device mockup, app UI visible on screen, lifestyle context, marketing screenshot"
- For exploded view: add "exploded technical diagram, component breakdown, engineering illustration style"
