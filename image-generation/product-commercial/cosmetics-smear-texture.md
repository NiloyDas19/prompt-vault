## Purpose
Generate highly satisfying, tactile macro shots of cosmetic product textures (smears, dollops, powders).

## Prompt
Abstract commercial beauty photography of {COSMETIC_PRODUCT}, styled as an artistic {TEXTURE_ACTION}, highlighting the {TEXTURE_QUALITY} texture, {COLOR_PALETTE} color palette against a {BACKGROUND_COLOR} background, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting revealing the micro-details, satisfying macro product photography, editorial beauty texture, high-end skincare branding

## Variables
- {COSMETIC_PRODUCT} – Product (e.g., "thick night cream", "crushed velvet eyeshadow powder", "glossy lip tint")
- {TEXTURE_ACTION} – Action (e.g., "a perfect curved smear using a spatula", "a dollop with a sculpted peak", "shattered powder explosion")
- {TEXTURE_QUALITY} – Texture (e.g., "rich buttery opaque", "translucent gel with suspended bubbles", "glittering metallic")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "pastel peach and cream", "stark monochrome black and white", "vibrant fuchsia")
- {BACKGROUND_COLOR} – Background (e.g., "matching monochromatic", "contrasting complementary color", "frosted glass")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "hard light for dramatic shadow", "soft diffused light for smooth gradients")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "cosmetic texture smear photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (messy, gross, unappetizing, packaging, bottles, text)

## Notes
- This prompt generates the texture itself, not the bottle/packaging. Great for background assets or social media posts.
