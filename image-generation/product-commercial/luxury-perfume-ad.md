## Purpose
Generate a high-end luxury fragrance or cosmetics advertising photograph suitable for print or digital campaigns.

## Prompt
Luxury fragrance advertising photograph featuring {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION} perfume or cosmetic product, {HERO_PRESENTATION} presentation style, {LUXURY_MATERIAL} luxury material surface and props including {SPECIFIC_PROPS}, {PETAL_OR_INGREDIENT} {INGREDIENT_TYPE} scattered organically suggesting the scent story, {LIGHTING_SETUP} studio lighting creating {LIGHTING_EFFECT} on the product, {ATMOSPHERE} atmosphere with {COLOR_MOOD} color mood, {TEXTURE_INTERPLAY} texture interplay between {TEXTURE_A} and {TEXTURE_B}, {SCALE_TREATMENT} scale and cropping treatment, {BRAND_TIER} brand tier aesthetic, high fashion advertising photography, {HOUSE_STYLE} maison influence

## Variables
- {PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION} – Product (e.g., "a tall angular crystal flacon with gold stopper", "a round black matte bottle with embossed lettering")
- {HERO_PRESENTATION} – How shown (e.g., "single bottle as sole hero", "trio of bottles in descending size")
- {LUXURY_MATERIAL} – Materials (e.g., "polished black marble", "aged mirror", "silk and hammered gold")
- {SPECIFIC_PROPS} – Props (e.g., "scattered pearls and dried rose petals", "raw crystal geodes")
- {PETAL_OR_INGREDIENT} – Adjective (e.g., "delicate", "abundant", "precisely placed")
- {INGREDIENT_TYPE} – Natural element (e.g., "rose petals", "jasmine flowers", "sea salt crystals")
- {LIGHTING_SETUP} – Lights (e.g., "split hard-soft lighting", "dramatic backlit", "soft wraparound beauty")
- {LIGHTING_EFFECT} – Effect (e.g., "refraction through the glass", "deep jewel-like glow", "glass caustic light patterns")
- {ATMOSPHERE} – Mood (e.g., "mysterious and opulent", "clean minimalist luxury", "romantic sensual")
- {COLOR_MOOD} – Colors (e.g., "deep burgundy and gold", "ice white and silver", "warm amber and cream")
- {TEXTURE_INTERPLAY} – Contrast (e.g., "rough stone against smooth glass", "matte and glossy")
- {TEXTURE_A} – First texture
- {TEXTURE_B} – Second texture
- {SCALE_TREATMENT} – Framing (e.g., "tight close-up on bottle detail", "medium shot with full context")
- {BRAND_TIER} – Tier (e.g., "ultra-luxury niche", "prestige department store", "premium drugstore")
- {HOUSE_STYLE} – Reference (e.g., "Chanel No.5 classic", "Tom Ford dark sensuality", "Jo Malone minimalist British")
