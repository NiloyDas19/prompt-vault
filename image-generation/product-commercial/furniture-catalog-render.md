## Purpose
Generate clean, isolated, or beautifully staged product shots of furniture for interior design catalogs.

## Prompt
Commercial product photography of a {FURNITURE_PIECE}, designed in {DESIGN_STYLE} style, made of {MATERIALS}, {STAGING_TYPE}, {LIGHTING_STYLE} lighting, {COLOR_PALETTE} color scheme, {SHADOW_TYPE} shadow on the floor, IKEA / West Elm catalog aesthetic, photorealistic 3D product visualization

## Variables
- {FURNITURE_PIECE} – Item (e.g., "mid-century modern lounge chair", "minimalist wooden dining table", "plush velvet modular sofa")
- {DESIGN_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "Scandinavian minimalist", "industrial vintage", "contemporary Italian")
- {MATERIALS} – Materials (e.g., "walnut wood and bouclé fabric", "matte black steel and cognac leather")
- {STAGING_TYPE} – Setting (e.g., "isolated on a pure white background", "staged in a soft-lit neutral studio corner with a single plant")
- {LIGHTING_STYLE} – Light (e.g., "soft diffused studio", "warm natural sunlight from a window")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "warm earthy neutrals", "stark monochrome")
- {SHADOW_TYPE} – Shadow (e.g., "soft drop shadow", "no shadow, floating", "long dramatic floor shadow")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "furniture catalog product photography" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (messy room, distorted legs, asymmetrical, people, pets)

## Notes
- For the cleanest catalog look, use "isolated on white seamless studio background."
