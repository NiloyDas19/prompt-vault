## Purpose
Generate aggressive, bold, and dynamic mascot logos suitable for esports teams, gaming clans, or Twitch channels.

## Prompt
Esports mascot logo vector design of a fierce {ANIMAL_OR_CHARACTER}, {FACIAL_EXPRESSION}, {STYLING_DETAILS}, thick dynamic outlines, aggressive sharp angles, {COLOR_SCHEME} color scheme, {SHADING_STYLE} cell shading, isolated on a dark background, bold sports team branding aesthetic, symmetrical composition, aggressive energy

## Variables
- {ANIMAL_OR_CHARACTER} – Mascot (e.g., "cybernetic wolf", "spartan warrior", "venomous cobra", "grinning skull")
- {FACIAL_EXPRESSION} – Expression (e.g., "roaring with glowing eyes", "intense glaring stare")
- {STYLING_DETAILS} – Details (e.g., "wearing a futuristic headset", "biting a dagger")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "neon purple and electric cyan", "crimson red and dark charcoal", "toxic green and black")
- {SHADING_STYLE} – Shading (e.g., "high contrast hard edge", "metallic gradient")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "esports mascot logo vector art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (photorealistic, 3d render, soft edges, watercolors, text, words)

## Notes
- To leave room for a team name later, keep the composition centralized with "solid dark background, isolated graphic."
