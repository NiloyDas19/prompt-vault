## Purpose
Generate elegant, high-end monogram logos interlocking initials, perfect for fashion houses, law firms, or luxury real estate.

## Prompt
Luxury monogram logo design interlocking the letters "{LETTERS}", {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} typography, {FLOURISHES} decorative flourishes, rendered in {MATERIAL_FINISH} on a {BACKGROUND_TEXTURE} background, {VIBE} aesthetic, high-end luxury brand identity, perfectly balanced, timeless elegance, minimalist and sophisticated

## Variables
- {LETTERS} – Initials (e.g., "A and V", "C and C", "M")
- {TYPOGRAPHY_STYLE} – Font style (e.g., "elegant high-contrast serif", "clean geometric sans-serif", "flowing calligraphy script")
- {FLOURISHES} – Extras (e.g., "subtle botanical vine intertwining", "geometric art deco lines", "no flourishes, pure typography")
- {MATERIAL_FINISH} – Finish (e.g., "matte gold foil stamping", "embossed glossy black", "rose gold metallic")
- {BACKGROUND_TEXTURE} – Background (e.g., "dark textured leather", "creamy thick cotton paper", "stark white")
- {VIBE} – Mood (e.g., "Parisian haute couture", "traditional British heritage", "modern minimalist luxury")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "luxury monogram logo design" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (3D, cartoon, colorful, complex illustration, messy lines, illegible)

## Notes
- AI sometimes struggles with exact letters. Using symmetrical letters (like "H", "M", "V") often produces cleaner results than complex asymmetrical pairs.
