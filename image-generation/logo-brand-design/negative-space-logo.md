## Purpose
Generate clever logos that use negative space to reveal a secondary image hidden within the primary silhouette.

## Prompt
Clever negative space logo design, the primary silhouette of {PRIMARY_SHAPE} containing the negative space cutout of {SECONDARY_SHAPE} inside it, {STYLE_DESCRIPTION}, {COLOR_PALETTE}, flat vector illustration, minimalist brand identity, optical illusion logo, award-winning graphic design, solid white background

## Variables
- {PRIMARY_SHAPE} – Outer shape (e.g., "a pine tree", "a coffee cup", "a bear's head")
- {SECONDARY_SHAPE} – Inner cutout (e.g., "a howling wolf", "a crescent moon", "a flying bird")
- {STYLE_DESCRIPTION} – Style (e.g., "smooth flowing curves", "sharp geometric edges", "monoline weight")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "solid black silhouette", "deep forest green", "two-tone blue and orange")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "clever negative space logo vector" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (gradients, 3d, shadows, messy, text, detailed illustration, outlines)

## Notes
- Negative space is hard for AI. Keep the shapes universally recognizable (e.g., a tree, a bird, a house, a key).
