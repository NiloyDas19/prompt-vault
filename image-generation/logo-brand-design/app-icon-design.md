## Purpose
Generate modern, clean iOS/Android style app icons for digital products.

## Prompt
Modern iOS app icon design for a {APP_TYPE} app, featuring a central {ICON_GRAPHIC} graphic, {DESIGN_TREND} design style, {COLOR_GRADIENT} vibrant background gradient, {LIGHTING_EFFECT} lighting effect, squircle shape frame, clean UI UX design aesthetic, dribbble top shot, highly polished digital art

## Variables
- {APP_TYPE} – App purpose (e.g., "fitness tracking", "music streaming", "crypto wallet", "meditation")
- {ICON_GRAPHIC} – Graphic (e.g., "a stylized lightning bolt", "minimalist overlapping soundwaves", "a 3D glowing lotus flower")
- {DESIGN_TREND} – Style (e.g., "glassmorphism", "neumorphism", "flat vector", "3D claymation style")
- {COLOR_GRADIENT} – Colors (e.g., "sunset orange to hot pink", "deep ocean blue to cyan", "stark black and neon green")
- {LIGHTING_EFFECT} – Light (e.g., "subtle inner shadow and glossy top highlight", "matte finish with soft drop shadow")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 300`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "iOS app icon design, rounded square" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (text, words, complex background, photograph, messy, uncentered)

## Notes
- For 3D icons (very trendy): use "3D rendered icon, soft studio lighting, cute clay render style, isometric."
