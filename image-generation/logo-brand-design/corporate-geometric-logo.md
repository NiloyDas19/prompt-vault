## Purpose
Generate abstract, highly professional geometric marks used by tech companies, banks, and large corporations.

## Prompt
Abstract geometric corporate logo mark for a {INDUSTRY} company, composed of {SHAPE_INTERACTION}, {STYLE_DESCRIPTION}, {COLOR_SCHEME}, {GRADIENT_OR_FLAT}, isolated on white background, clean vector graphics, modern corporate brand identity, scalable, simple and memorable

## Variables
- {INDUSTRY} – Sector (e.g., "global logistics", "fintech startup", "healthcare network", "data analytics")
- {SHAPE_INTERACTION} – Shapes (e.g., "three interlocking folded triangles", "a continuous moebius strip polygon", "overlapping translucent circles")
- {STYLE_DESCRIPTION} – Style (e.g., "creating an implied letter 'N'", "suggesting forward motion and growth", "evoking connectivity and networks")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "trustworthy navy blue and tech cyan", "vibrant energetic coral and purple")
- {GRADIENT_OR_FLAT} – Finish (e.g., "flat solid colors", "subtle modern gradients")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 50`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "abstract corporate logo mark, vector design" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (3D, shadows, complex, illustrative, literal, text, words)

## Notes
- The simpler the better. If it looks too complex, add "extremely minimalist, simple shapes only."
