## Purpose
Generate a clean, professional minimalist logo suitable for branding a business or product — delivered with symbolic clarity and visual impact.

## Prompt
Minimalist vector logo design for {BRAND_NAME}, a {BUSINESS_TYPE} company, single iconic symbol of {SYMBOL_CONCEPT} rendered in {DESIGN_STYLE} style, {LINE_WEIGHT} line weight, {COLOR_COUNT} color design using only {BRAND_COLORS}, perfect negative space balance, scalable from business card to billboard, {SHAPE_LANGUAGE} geometric language, white or transparent background, professional brand identity quality, suitable for trademark registration, clean and timeless

## Variables
- {BRAND_NAME} – The brand name
- {BUSINESS_TYPE} – What the company does (e.g., "sustainable tech", "artisan bakery", "legal consultancy")
- {SYMBOL_CONCEPT} – What the icon represents (e.g., "a stylized leaf merging with a circuit board", "an abstract letter M as a mountain peak", "interlocking rings")
- {DESIGN_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "geometric flat", "lettermark", "abstract mark", "combination mark", "monogram")
- {LINE_WEIGHT} – Line thickness (e.g., "thin elegant hairline", "medium balanced", "bold strong")
- {COLOR_COUNT} – Colors (e.g., "single color monochrome", "two-color", "full color")
- {BRAND_COLORS} – Specific colors (e.g., "deep navy #1A2B5F", "forest green and white", "black and gold")
- {SHAPE_LANGUAGE} – Shape type (e.g., "sharp angular", "rounded friendly", "asymmetric dynamic", "perfectly symmetrical")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 200 --no gradients, photorealism, complexity`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "vector logo, white background, minimal design" and specify brand name explicitly
**Stable Diffusion:** Use logo-specific LoRA. Negative: (complex, detailed, photorealistic, gradients unless specified, text errors)

## Notes
- For wordmark: add "the brand name '{BRAND_NAME}' in custom {TYPEFACE_STYLE} typography below the mark"
- For badge/emblem: add "circular badge format, vintage-inspired, craft brand aesthetic"
- Always request on white AND dark background versions for versatility
