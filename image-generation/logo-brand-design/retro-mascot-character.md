## Purpose
Generate charming, "rubber hose" 1930s-style retro mascot characters (think Cuphead or early Disney).

## Prompt
Vintage 1930s rubber hose animation style mascot character of a {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION}, {ACTION}, wearing {CLOTHING_DETAILS}, pie-cut eyes, thick black outlines, {COLOR_PALETTE}, distressed vintage print texture, retro cartoon aesthetic, flat vector illustration, isolated on off-white background

## Variables
- {CHARACTER_DESCRIPTION} – Mascot (e.g., "smiling coffee cup with arms and legs", "friendly walking hotdog", "mischievous little devil")
- {ACTION} – Doing what (e.g., "tipping a top hat", "strutting confidently", "winking and giving a thumbs up")
- {CLOTHING_DETAILS} – Clothes (e.g., "white gloves and big shoes", "a striped bowtie", "suspender pants")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "classic black and white", "vintage faded red, yellow, and black")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 100`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "1930s rubber hose cartoon mascot, vintage illustration" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (3d, realistic, modern cartoon, gradients, shading, shadows)

## Notes
- "Pie-cut eyes" and "white gloves" are the magic keywords for nailing this specific 1930s aesthetic.
