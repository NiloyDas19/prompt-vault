## Purpose
Generate delicate, artisanal, hand-drawn logos perfect for boutiques, bakeries, wedding photographers, or craft brands.

## Prompt
Delicate hand-drawn logo illustration of {SUBJECT}, {DRAWING_STYLE}, {DETAILS}, fine line art, {COLOR_PALETTE}, {BACKGROUND_TEXTURE}, feminine boutique brand identity, elegant organic aesthetic, Pinterest aesthetic, minimalist boho design

## Variables
- {SUBJECT} – What (e.g., "a continuous line drawing of a female face", "a wild botanical wildflower bouquet", "a pair of vintage scissors and thread")
- {DRAWING_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "single continuous line", "delicate pencil sketch", "loose watercolor wash with ink outlines")
- {DETAILS} – Extras (e.g., "surrounded by tiny scattered stars", "enclosed in a rough hand-drawn circle")
- {COLOR_PALETTE} – Colors (e.g., "charcoal ink on warm cream", "dusty rose, sage green, and gold", "pure black and white")
- {BACKGROUND_TEXTURE} – Background (e.g., "textured watercolor paper", "clean digital white")

## Modifiers
**Midjourney:** `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --stylize 150`
**DALL-E 3:** Add "hand-drawn boutique logo design, fine line art" at start
**Stable Diffusion:** Negative: (bold, corporate, geometric, 3d, symmetrical, thick lines)

## Notes
- Keep it organic. "Boho" and "Pinterest aesthetic" help push the model toward current indie branding trends.
