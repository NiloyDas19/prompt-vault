## Purpose
Generate a detailed vintage badge or emblem logo with crafted typography and intricate illustrated details.

## Prompt
Vintage {BADGE_SHAPE} badge emblem logo for {BRAND_OR_PURPOSE}, {CENTRAL_ILLUSTRATION} central illustration in {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} engraving style, surrounded by {BANNER_TYPE} ribbon banner reading "{TAGLINE}", {BORDER_STYLE} decorative border with {BORDER_MOTIFS} motifs, {TYPOGRAPHY_ARRANGEMENT} typography arrangement with {BRAND_NAME} as the primary text, {TEXTURE_OVERLAY} distressed texture overlay, {COLOR_SCHEME} {COLOR_COUNT} color scheme, {ERA_INFLUENCE} era influence, {SPECIALTY_TYPE} specialty brand aesthetic, screen printing quality, craft beer / artisan / heritage brand style

## Variables
- {BADGE_SHAPE} – Shape (e.g., "shield", "circular", "hexagonal", "pennant", "oval")
- {BRAND_OR_PURPOSE} – Use (e.g., "a craft brewery", "a barbershop", "a wilderness outfitter", "a premium coffee roaster")
- {CENTRAL_ILLUSTRATION} – Icon (e.g., "a leaping stag", "crossed axes", "a hop cone and wheat", "a mountain range")
- {ILLUSTRATION_STYLE} – Style (e.g., "woodcut", "stipple", "letterpress", "linework")
- {BANNER_TYPE} – Banner (e.g., "a curved ribbon below", "a waving flag banner above", "flanking side banners")
- {TAGLINE} – Banner text (e.g., "Est. 1987", "Handcrafted with Pride", "Since 1923")
- {BORDER_STYLE} – Border (e.g., "double line with inner rope", "geometric pattern", "dotted circular", "ornate flourishes")
- {BORDER_MOTIFS} – Decorative elements (e.g., "stars and hops", "pine branches", "laurel leaves", "waves")
- {TYPOGRAPHY_ARRANGEMENT} – Text layout (e.g., "curved arched above center", "stacked centered", "inline horizontal")
- {BRAND_NAME} – Brand name
- {TEXTURE_OVERLAY} – Aged effect (e.g., "heavy ink worn", "subtle aged paper", "light press texture", "no texture, clean")
- {COLOR_SCHEME} – Colors (e.g., "two-color black and gold", "three-color red, cream, and navy", "single color dark brown")
- {COLOR_COUNT} – Number of colors
- {ERA_INFLUENCE} – Period (e.g., "1920s prohibition", "1950s Americana", "Victorian heritage", "1970s Americana")
- {SPECIALTY_TYPE} – Industry (e.g., "craft beer", "artisan food", "heritage clothing", "outdoor adventure")
